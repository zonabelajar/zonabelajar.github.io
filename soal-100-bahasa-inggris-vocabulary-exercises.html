29. Vocabulary exercises ini tentang pertanyaan dari visiting niagara falls


Jawaban:

Vocabulary exercises tentang pertanyaan dari teks Visiting Niagar Falls yaitu

Waves are POUNDING against the pier. You can hear the sound very clearly.In the morning, the small town is covered in MIST.Explore Cheddar GORGE through photos in the internet. They inspire you to come and visit.Her shoes got SOAKED as the walked through the wet grass.Don't forget to bring your WATERPROOF jacket. It's dark outside, It's likely going to rain.Somerset ATTRACTIONS help her forget her complicated for the problems.Christiano Ronaldo came to Bali to campaign for the PRESERVATION of the environment.The street lights go on at DUSK. Without the lights, car drivers cannot see the traffic in front of them.Look at that side. An ILLUMINATED sign flashed on and off.This is a region of SCENIC beauty. All areas are covered with trees; clean water flows uninterrupted, and fresh air fills the sky.I remember having an EXHILARATING walk to mount bromo two years ago.The rainbow bridge CROSSES Niagara river.On her last vacation, her can swerved and PLUNGED off the cliff.The CHARM of the nature reminds him of God's greatness.Tropical forest in borneo is the largest wildlife SANCTUARY in IIndonesia.

Penjelasan:

Pada soal nomor 8, menggunakan simple present tense (verb +es/s) karena kalimat tersebut merupakan kalimat fakta dengan subject it (the rainbow bridge).

Pada soal nomor 9, menggunakan past tense (verb 2 / verb+ed). Perhatikan kata kerja sebelumnya 'swerved' dengan konjungsi 'and', sehingga kata kerja kedua (plunge) harus berbentuk past verb juga.
