3. pertanyaan spa saja biasanya yang di lontarkan saat job interview bahasa inggris ?​


1. Tell me about yourself (Jelaskan tentang dirimu)
2. Why should we hire you? (Kenapa kami harus menerima kamu?)
3. Why do you want this job? (Kenapa kau menginginkan pekerjaan ini?)
4. What is your strength? (Apa kelebihanmu?)
5. Do you work well with others? (Apa kau bisa bekerja sama dengan yang lain?)

14. Percakapan interview bahasa inggris membuat kue


Reni: Hey, how are you Dewi? By the way, I’d like to say thanks for your invitation. [Hai, apa kabar Dewi? Ngomong-omong, aku ingin mengucapkan terima kasih utuk undangannya.]

Dewi: I am very well Ren. Okay, let’s go to my lovely kitchen. [Aku sangat sehat Ren. Oke, mari kita ke dapur kesayanganku.]

Reni: Sure. I’ll follow you. [Tentu. Aku mengikutimu.]

Dewi: What are we making, Ren? [Apa yang akan kita masak Ren?]

Reni: I know that your kitchenware is complete enough to make everything. How about Rainbow Cake? [Aku tahu bahwa peralatan masakmu cukup lengkap untuk membuat masakan apapun. Bagaimana jikaRainbow Cake?]

Dewi: I love that cake very much. But, I’ve never made it before. [Aku sangat senang dengan kue itu. Tapi, aku belum pernah membuatnya.]

Reni: It is so easy Wi. I bring my recipe’s book. [Itu sangatlah mudah Wi. Aku membawa buku resep-resepku.]

Dewi: That’s good. What ingredients that we need? [Itu bagus. Bahan-bahan apa yang kita butuhkan?]

Reni: Let me check the recipe. Okay, here is the material; we need butter, milk, flour, some eggs, sugar, cochineal, baking powder and water. [Coba aku cek resepnya dulu. Oke, ini bahan-bahannya; kita membutuhkan mentega, susu, tepung, beberapa telur, gula, pewarna makanan, pengembang, dan air.]

Dewi: A moment please. Well, I’ve got the ingredients on the table. Now what? [Tunggu sebentar. Baiklah, aku telah menyiapkannya diatas meja. Sekarang gimana?]

Reni: Mix up the sugar, butter in mixing bowl while I heat up the milk. Then we will mix it together. [Aduk gula, mentega di mangkuk pengaduk sambil aku memanaskan susunya. Lalu, kita akan mencampur susunya juga.]

Dewi: Okay! Let’s do it! How about the eggs? [Oke! Mari kita lakukan! Bagaimana dengan telurnya?]

Reni: Give it to me please. I will combine it. [Tolong berikan kepadaku. Aku akan mencampurkannya.]

Dewi: Sure. By the way, why do you like cooking very much? [Tentu. Ngomong-omong, mengapa kamu sangat menyukai memasak?]

Reni: Since childhood my mother always took me to help her in the kitchen. So, over time I will love to cook. And how about you? [Sejak kecil ibuku selalu mengajakku untuk membantunya di dapur. Jadi, lama-kelamaan aku juga menyukai memasak. Bagaimana denganmu?]

Here is an example of a job interview for a customer service representative position:

Interviewer: Can you tell us about your previous experience in customer service?

Candidate: Sure, I have worked in customer service for three years at a call center, handling customer inquiries and complaints. I have experience with both phone and email support.

Interviewer: What is the essential quality a customer service representative should possess?

Candidate: Excellent communication skills are the essential quality a customer service representative should possess. Listening actively to customers, understanding their concerns, and providing clear and empathetic responses are crucial.

Interviewer: How do you handle demanding customers?

Candidate: I always approach demanding customers with patience and empathy. I try to understand their concerns and find a solution to satisfy them. If the problem is beyond my authority, I'll escalate it to a supervisor or the appropriate department.

Interviewer: Can you tell us about a time when you went above and beyond for a customer?

Candidate: One time, I received a call from a customer who was frustrated because their package had been delayed multiple times. I contacted the shipping company and discovered the box had been lost in transit. I went above and beyond by contacting the manufacturer, who could send a replacement package directly to the customer. The customer was extremely grateful, and I was happy that I could resolve the problem to their satisfaction.

Interviewer: Thank you for your answers. Is there anything you would like to ask us?

Candidate: Yes, I would like to know more about this company's training and development opportunities for customer service representatives.

Interviewer: We provide a comprehensive training program for new employees and ongoing training to help representatives stay updated with the latest industry practices. We also offer opportunities for advancement within the company for employees who excel in their roles.
