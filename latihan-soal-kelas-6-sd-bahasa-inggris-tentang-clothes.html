17. Writer on your book an change into passive 1. Lusi washes the clothes2. Lusi is washing the clothes3. Lusi washing the clothes4. Lusi was washing the clothes5. Lusi has washed the clothes6. Lusi had washed the clothes7. Lusi will wash the clothes8. Lusi can wash the clothesTolong bantuannya Kaka ​


Passive forms of the sentences =

The clothes are washed by LusiThe clothes are being washed by Lusi- (Can't be changed)The clothes were being washed by LusiThe clothes have been washed by LusiThe clothes had been washed by LusiThe clothes will be washed by LusiThe clothes can be washed by LusiPEMBAHASANPassive Voice

Passive voice adalah kalimat yang menggunakan kata kerja pasif. Kata kerja pasif didasari oleh penggunaan "to be + V3" . Di dalam kalimat pasif, posisi subjek bertukar dengan objek yang disertai perubahan kata kerja menjadi dikenai.

PENJELASAN

1. Lusi washes the clothes

The clothes are washed by Lusi.Simple present : S + is/am/are + Verb 3 + by + O

2. Lusi is washing the clothes

The clothes are being washed by Lusi.Present continuous : S + is/am/are + being + Verb 3 + by + O

3. Lusi washing the clothes

- (Can't be changed)We can't identify the type of sentence since it is not grammarly correct.

4. Lusi was washing the clothes

The clothes were being washed by Lusi.Past continuous : S + was/were + being + Verb 3 + by + O

5. Lusi has washed the clothes

The clothes have been washed by Lusi.Present perfect : S + has/have + been + Verb 3 + by + O

6. Lusi had washed the clothes

The clothes had been washed by Lusi.Past perfect : S + had + been + Verb 3 + by + O

7. Lusi will wash the clothes

The clothes will be washed by Lusi.Simple future : S + will be + Verb 3 + by + O

8. Lusi can wash the clothes

The clothes can be washed by Lusi.Simple present (with modals) : S + modal + be + Verb 3 + by + OPELAJARI LEBIH LANJUTPassive form of Simple Present brainly.co.id/tugas/29666042 Passive form of Simple Past brainly.co.id/tugas/29782277 Passive to Active brainly.co.id/tugas/29555484
