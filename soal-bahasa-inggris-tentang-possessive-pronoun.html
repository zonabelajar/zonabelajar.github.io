1. pengertian Possessive pronoun(jawabdalam bahasa inggris ya. ....)


possesive pronoun is pronoun used to indicate ownershipA possessive pronoun is a part of speech that attributes ownership to someone or something. Like any other pronoun, it substitutes a noun phrase and can prevent its repetition. For example, in the phrase, "These glasses are mine, not yours", the words "mine" and "yours" are possessive pronouns and stand for "my glasses" and "your glasses," respectively.

16. contoh kalimat pronoun : subject pronoun, object pronoun, possessive pronoun, replexive possessive, replexive pronoun​


Pronoun is a word that takes the place of a noun, such as: I, you, me, it, they, we, she, him, us. There are several types of pronouns: subjective, objective, possessive adjectives, and possessive pronouns.

Pembahasan

Contoh kalimat dengan subject pronoun yaitu

I have a cat.

Contoh kalimat dengan object pronoun yaitu

A cat sleeps with me everyday.

Contoh kalimat dengan possessive pronoun yaitu

That cat is mine.

Contoh kalimat dengan reflexive pronoun yaitu

My brother can study himself.

Contoh kalimat dengan adjective possessive yaitu

My cat has long black tail.

Pada soal tertulis "reflexive possessive", tetapi tidak ada pronoun dalam kategori ini. Yang ada adalah possessive pronoun dan adjective possessive.

1. Personal Pronoun atau Subject Pronoun

I (saya)

I will go to the US next year (Saya akan pergi ke Amerika Serikat tahun depan)

You (kamu)

You are really talented! (Kamu sangat berbakat!)

She (dia – perempuan)

He (dia – laki-laki)

He can speak English fluently (Dia dapat berbicara dalam bahasa Inggris dengan lancar)

They (mereka)

They are my neighbors (Mereka adalah tetanggaku)

We (kita, kami)

2. Object Pronouns

Me (saya)

Listen to me (Dengarkan saya)

us (kami, kita)

The teachers will be mad at us (Para guru akan marah pada kita)

you (kamu, kalian)

I’m glad to see you! (Saya senang berjumpa denganmu!)

mine (milikku)

The chicken noodle on the table is mine (Mie ayam di meja itu punyaku)

yours (milikmu, milik kalian)

hers (milik dia (perempuan)

his (milik dia (laki-laki)

theirs (milik mereka)

33. Makalah tentang possessive pronoun


POSSESSIVE PRONOUNS


Possessive Pronoun

Possessive pronouns adalah kata ganti yang menunjukkan kepemilikan atas suatu benda. Kata-kata yang biasa digunakan adalah mine, yours, his, hers, ours, theirs.




Contoh:

2.4 FUNCTION & TYPE POSSESSIVE PRONOUNS

Possessive pronouns dibedakan menjadi dua:

a. Dependent

Dependent Possessive Pronouns adalah possessive pronouns yang ditempatkan sebelum kata benda (noun).

Contoh:

This is your pen and that is mine.

This is my pencil and those are his.

b. Independent

Independent Possessive Pronoun adalah possessive pronouns yang diletakkan setelah kata kerja (verb) :

This is your pen and that is mine.

This is my pencil and those are his.

Possesive pronoun juga mempunyai dua fungsi atau kegunaan, yaitu sebagai kata sifat (As adjective) dan sebagai kanti ganti saja (As pronoun).
