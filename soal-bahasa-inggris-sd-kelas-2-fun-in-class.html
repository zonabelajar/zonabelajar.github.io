9. Mana yang benar "Class Leaders" atau " Chairman Class " untuk bahasa inggrisnya ketua kelas?​


"Class Leaders atau Chairman Class"?

Bahasa Inggris dari ketua kelas adalah =

Chairman Class or Class Leader.

Jadi, Chairman Class dan Class Leader sama-sama artinya ketua kelas.

Tetapi lebih cocok dengan

Chairman Class

karena

Chairman=>Ketua

Class=>Kelas

Sedangkan

Class=>Kelas

Leader =>Pemimpin

Jadi yang lebih tepat adalah

CHAIRMAN CLASS

36. Tolong jawab ya kak soal bahasa inggris ini Emily participated in a fun bike event on


Pembahasan

REWRITE THE SENTENCES AND PARAGRAPH TO FORM CHRONOLOGICAL STORIES!

TEXT 1

Early Sunday morning, Emily checked her bicycle. O, gosh! The tire was flat. Emily was very confused. She would participate in a fun bike event to commemorate Heroes Day. Fortunately, her neighbor mended the tire. Emily rode to the fun bike arena hurriedly.

Arriving at the fun bike arena, the fun bike had not begun yet. Emily showed her ticket and the committee gave her two pieces of small cards. One card was for snack and the other was for the door prize draws.

Moment later the fun bike began. Many people, old and young joined the event. They rode around the town happily. When they arrived at the finish lane, they exchanged their cards for snacks.

While the participants were enjoyed their snacks, the committee announced that it was the time for the door prize draws. There were many prizes provided for the event. Surprisingly, Emily got a laptop computer. How happy Emily was!
