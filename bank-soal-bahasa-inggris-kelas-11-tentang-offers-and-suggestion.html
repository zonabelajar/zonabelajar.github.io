9. Ada tugas bahasa inggris kelas 11 tentang suggestion, ada yang bisa bantu? :)


Explanation:


Giving suggestion is a type of expression which is used when you want to give an advice or recommendation for someone.

Here are some expressions that you can use:

- You should ...

- You ought to ...

- It's better for you to ...

- I suggest you ...

- I advise you to ...

- Why don't we/you ... ?

- How about ...?


Answer:

Here's the suggestion sentence for each statement

1. Be economical with detergent and washing-up liquids. a little will probably wash just as clean as a lot.

Suggestion: We should be economical with detergent and washing-up liquids because a little will probably wash just as clean as a lot.


2. If you are buying a car, remember that small cars pollute less than big ones.

Suggestion: It's best not to buy a big car because big cars pollute more than small ones.


3. Try not to throw away household equipment that is still usable just because a new model is on the market

Suggestion: The economists suggest us not to throw away household equipment that is still usable just because a new model is on the market.


4. Avoid too many 'disposables' like paper plates, cups, if you can't burn them.

Suggestion: The ecologists recommend that we should avoid too many 'disposables' like paper plates, cups, if we can't burn them.


5. Buy non-coloured toilet paper. the dyes used in colored paper pollute water.

Suggestion: The environmentalists suggest that we should buy non-coloured toilet paper because the dyes used in colored paper can pollute water.


6. Try making a compost heap in the garden. It enriches the land.

Suggestion: The Greenpeace members suggest we should try making a compost heap in the garden because It can enrich the land.

10. Tolong buatin offers and suggestions dari foto dibawah dong, kelas 11


"Offers" (penawaran) dan "suggestions" (saran) adalah dua konsep yang berbeda dalam konteks komunikasi dan bisnis. Berikut ini adalah jawaban dari foto soal tentang percakapan di atas:
Percakapan 1:

Concierge hotel: good evening, can i help you?

Customer: good evening, yes please. i would like a room for the night

Concierge hotel: Of course, we have several room options available. Are you looking for a single or double room?

Customer: A double room, please

Concierge hotel: You're welcome, Mr. Smith. Your room key will be ready at the front desk. Enjoy your stay, and if you need anything else, don't hesitate to ask. Good night!

Customer: thank, god night

Percakapan 2:

Dina: Let's watch a movie.

Rudi: What kind of movie?

Dina: A romantic movie.

Rudi: I don't like romantic movies. Let's watch something else.

Dina: Well, how about a comedy? There are some great comedies out right now.

Rudi: Comedy sounds good. Which one do you have in mind?

Dina: I heard "The Laugh Factory" is really funny. What do you think?

Rudi: "The Laugh Factory" sounds like a great choice. Let's go for it.

Dina: Perfect! I'll grab some popcorn, and we can start watching.

Percakapan 3:

A store attendant: Hi there, how can I help you?

A customer: I would like to return this shirt for a refund.

A store attendant: What is the problem with the shirt?

A customer: The shirt has a stain on it, and it's not the size I expected.

A store attendant: I apologize for the inconvenience. Do you have the receipt with you?

A customer: Yes, I do. Here's the receipt.

A store attendant: Thank you. We can process the refund for you. Would you prefer the refund to be credited back to your original payment method?

A customer: Yes, please. That would be great.

A store attendant: Alright, I'll start the refund process for you. It should take a few moments. Thank you for shopping with us, and I'm sorry for the inconvenience you've experienced.

A customer: Thank you for your help.

Pembahasan

Offers merujuk pada tindakan menawarkan sesuatu kepada orang lain. Ini bisa berupa penawaran produk atau layanan, atau bahkan penawaran bantuan atau kerjasama dalam konteks yang lebih umum. Contohnya, sebuah perusahaan bisa memberikan penawaran khusus pada produk mereka, seperti "Beli satu, dapatkan satu gratis," sebagai strategi pemasaran.

Suggestions merujuk pada memberikan ide atau rekomendasi kepada orang lain. Saran dapat berupa pandangan atau pendapat yang diberikan untuk membantu seseorang membuat keputusan atau memecahkan masalah. Misalnya, dalam percakapan sebelumnya, saran tersebut adalah ketika Dina menyarankan untuk menonton film komedi setelah Rudi mengatakan bahwa dia tidak suka film romantis.

20. Berikan contoh expressing offers and suggestion


A. Suggestion

a. Definition

Suggestion (saran) adalah mengajukan suatu

rencana untuk dipertimbangkan atau memberikan/menyumbangkan suatu ide. Hal-hal yang disarankan dapat diterima atau ditolak. Suggestion bersifat abstrak, dan hal-hal yang bisa disarankan seperti solution (solusi), advice (nasehat), plan (rencana), dan idea (ide).

b. Expressions Suggestion :

- Let's ...

- Why don't we ...

- We could ...

- What about ...

- I suggest that ...

- You might want to change ...

- I think ...

- I don't think ...

c. Responding Suggestion :

Accepting Suggestion

- Yes, let's go

- Ok, I will

- It's a good idea

- Okay, if you say so

- Let's

Declining Suggestion

- No, thank you. I don't feel like going

- Sorry, I think ...

- No, let's just do something else

- No, let's just go somewhere else

- Sorry, I can't. I have another appointment

- No, sorry.

d. Example :

- Dialogue 1 :

A : Let's go to the beach this weekend!

B : It's a good idea. Let's go!

- Dialogue 2 :

A : What don't you study for the test instead of playing outside?

B : Okay, if you say so

- Dialogue 3 :

A : I think you should go apologize to her

B : Sorry, I can't. It's her fault

- Dialogue 4 :

A : You might want to change your mind

B : No, sorry, I've planned this for months

B. Offers

a. Definition

Offers (tawaran) adalah memberikan sesuatu kepada seseorang sebagai hadiah (gifts), pertukaran (trade) atau bantuan (help). Pada umumnya, offers bersifat nyata (konkrit), dan hal-hal yang dapat diberikan kepada seseorang sebagai offers seperti food (makanan), money (uang), solution (solusi), friendship (persahabatan), atau bargain (penawaran).

b. Expression Offers :

- May I ...

- Can I ...

- Shall I ...

- Would you ...

- How about I ...

c. Responding Offers :

Accepting Offers

- Yes, please. I really appreciate it

- Thank you, it's very kind of you

- Yes, please, that be lovely

- Yes, please, that would be very kind of you

- Thank you, I appreciate your help

Declining Offers

- It's okay, I can do it myself

- No, thank you

- No, thanks

- Don't worry, I will do it myself

- That's alright, I will manage it on my own

d. Example :

- Dialogue 1 :

A : Can I help you?

B : Yes, please, I really appreciate your help

- Dialogue 2 :

A : Could I offer you something?

B : Yes, please, I want a cup of tea

- Dialogue 3 :

A : Would you care for another cup of tea?

B : No, thank you.

- Dialogue 4 :

A : Can I clean the car for you?

B : It's okay, I can do it myself

Giving Suggestion

Giving suggestion atau memberi saran adalah ungkapan yang digunakan seseorang untuk memberikan saran atau nasihat kepada orang lain.

Pattern yang digunakan untuk giving suggestion adalah:

Would you be so kind enough to + verb

Contoh kalimat:

Would you be so kind enough to play with me?

Would you be so kind enough to watch movie with me?

I would be so grateful if you would.....

Contoh kalimat:

I would be so grateful if you would play me.

I would be so grateful if you would watch movie with me.

How/What about... + Gerund

Contoh Kalimat:

How about watching movies?

How about playing football?

Suppose

Contoh kalimat:

Suppose that you want to play with me.

Suppose that you want to watch movies me.
