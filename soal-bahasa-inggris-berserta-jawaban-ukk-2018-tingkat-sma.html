Soal 1:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

My sister ________ tennis every Saturday morning.

a) plays

b) play

c) played

d) playing

Jawaban: a) plays

Soal 2:

Complete the sentence with the correct preposition:

I'm going to the store ________ some groceries.

a) at

b) with

c) for

d) to

Jawaban: c) for

Soal 3:

Choose the correct question tag for the following sentence:

He doesn't like coffee, ________?

a) does he?

b) isn't he?

c) doesn't he?

d) don't he?

Jawaban: a) does he?

Soal 4:

Identify the correct comparative form of the adjective "good":

a) gooder

b) goodest

c) better

d) goodier

Jawaban: c) better

Soal 5:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

She ________ a book when I arrived.

a) reads

b) is reading

c) read

d) has read

Jawaban: b) is reading

Soal 6:

Choose the correct word order for the sentence:

carrots / do / like / you / ?

a) You do like carrots?

b) Do you like carrots?

c) Carrots you do like?

d) Do like you carrots?

Jawaban: b) Do you like carrots?

Soal 7:

Identify the correct spelling of the word:

a) beutiful

b) beutifull

c) beautiful

d) beautifull

Jawaban: c) beautiful

Soal 8:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

The students ________ to the teacher's instructions.

a) listens

b) listen

c) listened

d) listening

Jawaban: b) listen

Soal 9:

Choose the correct possessive form of the noun:

The dog is ________ bone.

a) its

b) it's

c) their

d) they're

Jawaban: a) its

Soal 10:

Identify the correct punctuation for the sentence:

My favorite color is blue ________.

a) .

b) ,

c) !

d) ?

Jawaban: a) .

12. jelaskan cara mengolah hingga mengemas minuman sinom dalam bahasa inggris! yang panjang jawabnnya kak, ini buat ukk ​


=====☆Jawaban☆=====

Rasanya masam segar. Berkhasiat meredakan nyeri haid dan melangsingkan!

Bahan:

2 l air

75 g sinom (masih dengan tangkai), petiki **

75 g asam jawa

50 g kunyit, kupas, memarkan

100 g gula merah2

100 g gula pasir

Cara membuat:

Rebus air bersama daun sinom, asam jawa, dan kunyit di atas api kecil hingga mendidih.

Masukkan gula merah dan gula pasir. Aduk hingga larut, angkat. Diamkan hingga tidak panas, saring.

Didihkan kembali. Angkat. Sajikan hangat. (f)

Untuk 6 gelas (@ 250 ml)

Resep telah diuji coba di Dapur Uji Femina

**CATATAN

1. Sinom: Daun muda dari tanaman asam yang terdapat di pucuk tangkai. Memiliki rasa asam alami. Dapatkan di pasar tradisional bagian jamu.

2. Gula merah: Boleh menggunakan gula aren atau gula kelapa. Lebih banyak yang menggunakan gula aren karena punya aroma memikat.
