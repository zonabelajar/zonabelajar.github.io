22. Buatkan dualog bahasa inggris Yang ada instruction, invitation, prohibition, permission untuk 6 orang


Pembahasan

Dialog Bahasa Inggris yang ada instruction, invitation, prohibition, permission untuk 6 orang adalah:

Fitri, Edo, Made, Elsa, Udin, and Sara are having a conversation after school. They have to do the daily duty to clean the classroom before they go home.

Udin: I think we should share the duty. I will sweep and mop the floor but I need help from you.  

Edo: I agree with you, Udin. Let's share the tasks so we can finish these quickly. I will help you.  

Sara: I will also help you, Udin. After I throw away the garbage.

Udin: Great. Be careful, don't mess the garbage bin. (prohibition)

Sara: I won't.

Udin: Thanks a lot. Fitri, Made, can you dust and rearrange the the desks and chairs please? (instruction)

Made: Sure, I can.

Fitri: Yes, I can.

Udin: Great. But don't drag the desks and chairs. (prohibition)

Edo: You have to lift them so the floor will not be scratched. (instruction)

Made: OK, we will do so.

Elsa: What can I do?

Edo: Can you clean the whiteboard and window, please? {instruction}

Elsa : Sure. May I open the window? (asking permission) It will be easier to be cleaned.

Fitri : Of course, you may. (giving permission) It will also make the air circulation better.

Sara: After we finish these, what about stop by at my house? (invitation) My mother cooks tasty chicken noodles. I believe you all will like it.

Made: That will be great! Thanks, Sara.

Fitri: You are very kind, Sara. Thanks.

Elsa: Cool, I always love your mom's cooking, Sara.

Sara: It's my pleasure.

Edo: That will be nice. Thank you so much, Sara.

Udin: Thank you very much, Sara. OK, let's do our tasks, everyone.

32. Write example of prohibition and instruction (min.10)


Prohibitions

Don’t touch me!Don’t say it!Don’t drink the alcohol!Don’t disturb!Don’t run!Don’t be sad!Don’t step on the grass!Don’t be late!Don’t worry!Don’t be lazy!

Instructions

Open the book!Clean your room now!Let’s answer the question!Open the window, please!Let’s start the quiz!Watch the book first!Call me when you get home!Turn off the radio, please!Take your book!Let’s play the game!

Pembahasan

Prohibition adalah sebuah kalimat dalam bahasa Inggris yang digunakan untuk mengungkapkan sebuah larangan atau peringatan bagi orang lain untuk tidak melakukan sesuatu. Kalimat prohibition biasanya diawali dengan kalimat “Don’t / do not” yang artnya jangan. Prohibition memiliki tiga macam bentuk yaitu verbal prohibition, nominal prohibition dan prohibition no + verb-ing.

Verbal prohibition (Don’t + Verb 1)

Contoh: Don’t touch me!

Nominal prohibition (Don’t + be + Adverb/Noun/Adjective)

Contoh : Don’t be sad!

Prohibition no + verb-ing

Contoh : No smoking!

Instruction merupakan jenis kalimat dalam bahasa Inggris yang digunakan untuk memberi instruksi, nasihat atau permintaan. Rumus dari giving instruction adalah verb 1 + object. Contoh:

Open the book!Watch the label!Eat your vegetables!Bring your id card!Turn left and go straight!
