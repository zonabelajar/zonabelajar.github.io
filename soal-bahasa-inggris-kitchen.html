♧ Jawaban ♧

( + ) Rini (helps) her mother in the kitchen every morning

( - ) Rini help donot her mother in the kitchen every morning

( ? ) Does Rini help mother in the kitchen every morning

♧ Penjelasan ♧

☞Present tense☜

S + V + O

•S = >> st (she,he,it) + Ves

>> sj (i,you,we,they) + V1

•avx = >> do ( i,you,we,they)

>> does (she,he,it)

Contoh :

1.)

+ my father reads newspaper every morning

? does my father read newspaper every morning

- my father doesnot read newpaper every day

2.)

+ i do my bed every day

? do ido bed every day

- i donot do bed every day

3.)

+ in fact,the sun rises in the east

? does the sun rise in the eats

- the sun does not rise in the east
