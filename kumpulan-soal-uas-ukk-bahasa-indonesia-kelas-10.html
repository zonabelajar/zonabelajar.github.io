16. Soal UKK matematika kelas 10


Trigonometri

(sin 150° + cos 300°) / (tan 225° - sin 300°

= (sin 30° + cos 60°) / (tan 45° - (-sin 60°))

= (1/2 + 1/2) / (1 + 1/2 √3)

= 1 / ((2 + √3)/2)

= 2/(2 + √3)

= 2(2 - √3) / (2 + √3)(2 - √3)

= (4 - 2√3) / (4 - 3)

= 4 - 2√3 → jawaban
