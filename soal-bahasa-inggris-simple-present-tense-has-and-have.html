Simple present tense is a basic grammar which used to state facts which don't change, or events that happen routinely (repeated actions). Simple present tense devided into two kind of sentences, verbal and nominal. Each sentence has three forms (positive, negative, and interrogative)

10. Jelaskan Tentang Simple present tense dalam bahasa indonesia dan bahasa Inggris! ​


Jawaban:

Simple present tense adalah tenses yang digunakan ketika suatu kejadian sedang berlangsung saat ini atau kejadian yang berlangsung berulang kali (kebiasaan). Tenses ini merupakan tenses yang paling sering digunakan dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Simple present tense is a tense that is used when an event is currently taking place or an event that takes place repeatedly (habit). This tense is the most tense tense often used in English

Simple Present Tense is a verb tense to tell an action happens regularly, or to describe habits and general truth.

(Simple Present Tense adalah kata kerja untuk memberitahu suatu tindakan terjadi secara teratur, atau untuk menggambarkan kebiasaan dan kebenaran umum).

PEMBAHASAN

The Formula of Simple Present Tense (Rumus Simple Present Tense)

(1) Verbal Sentence (Kalimat Verbal)

(+) S + V1 (-s/es) + Complement(-) S + do/does + not + V1 + Complement(?) Do/does + S + V1 + Complement?

➡ For Example (Contohnya)

(+) I drink milk every morning. (Saya minum susu setiap pagi)(-) I do not drink milk every morning. (Saya tidak minum susu setiap pagi.(?) Do I drink milk every morning? (Apalah saya minum susu setiap pagi?)

➡ Three sentences above show habits, we can know from adverb of time "every morning."

(Tiga kalimat di atas menunjukkan kebiasaan, kita bisa mengetahuinya dari kata keterangan waktu "setiap pagi").

(2) Nominal Sentence (Kalimat Nomina)

(+) S + Be (is/am/are) + Complement (-) S + Be (is/am/are) + not + V1(?) Be (is/am/are) + S + Complement?

➡ For Example (Contohnya)

(+) You are a student. (Kamu adalah seorang murid).(-) You are not a student. (Kamu bukan seorang murid).(?) are you a student. (Apakah kamu adalah seorang murid?)

➡ Three sentences above show about general truth because you are a student in real life.

(Tiga kalimat di atas menunjukkan kebenaran umum karena kamu adalah seorang murid dalam kehidupan nyata).

Note! (Catatan)

Adverb of time (keterangan waktu)

Everyday (setiap hari) Every morning (setiap pagi)Often (sering)Usually (biasanya)Always (selalu)etc. (dll.)

                                     My Aunt's Office

                My name is Alicia. I am spending my holiday in Jakarta. I stay with my aunt. Her name is Farah Adusi. She is 22 years old. She works as an administrative staff of a Production House in Jakarta. I visited her office last week. Her office is unique. I will tell you about it.  

              Aunt Farah shares an office space with one of her colleagues, Miss Destri. She also works as an administrative staff of the company. They are very close friend.  They went to the same high school. My aunt's office is a room with size about 4 m long and 3 m wide, with light grey paint covers all the walls. When you enter the room, you will find a big window right across the room. In the morning, it is showered by the morning sun light because it faces the east direction. The room is bright so that the ceiling lights are not needed much during the day.

               There is a long wooden desk in the middle of the room with four chairs well arranged surround it. My aunt bought it when she traveled to Jepara two years ago. Two personal computers are put on the table. There is no cubicle in the office. Aunt Farah and Miss Destri sit in rows of seats face north in front of their own personal computers. There are a printer and a cordless telephone in the free space between them. There is also a small metal tray on the table in which they put the stationery neatly. In one side of the wall, there is a huge whiteboards. It is full of notes of the deadlines about some projects which they are working on.  

                 Miss Destri brought a flashy blue clock that is hanged on the wall next to the whiteboard. In the corner of the room there is a cabinet used to store many files. Right next to the cabinet, there is a small wooden table which is covered with white and blue stripes  tablecloth. There is a beautiful flower vase with a huge red roses bouquet on it. Near the window, there is a pile of books and work portfolios which are kept neatly on the shelf.  

                 My aunt's office is not too large but it is clean and well organized. The air is always fresh and cool because there is an air conditioner installed on the wall. I like how my aunt arrange her office. I will go there again tomorrow. Aunt Farah and Miss Destri will treat me a mini tour to the whole building and buy me a lunch in the office cafe. It will be so much fun.

beda pada rumus yang digunakan di setiap kalimat nya

Penjelasan:

1. simple present tense

pola nya:

verb: s+verb 1 + obj

ex: i go to school

non verb : s+ tobe (is, am, are) +adjective/adverb

ex: i am beautiful

2. simple past tense

pola nya:

verb: s+ verb 2+ obj

ex: i went to school

non verb: s+ tobe (was, were)+ adjective/adverb

ex: i was smart

3. simple future tense

polanya:

verb: s + Will/shall + verb 1 + obj

ex: i Will help you

non verb: s+Will/shall + be + adjective /adverb

ex: i Will be smart

4. present perfect tense

polanya:

verb: s +have/has+ verb 3+obj

ex: i have gone to the market

non verb: s+ have/ has + Been + adjective/adverb

ex: we have Been here
