Kategori Soal : Bahasa Inggris-Expressing Intention
Kelas : 1 SMA/X SMA

Pembahasan :

Talk 1 :

John : Hi, Mary! What are you going to do next holiday?

Mary : Hello, John. I am going to go to Brooklyn Bridge in New York. What about you?

John : I just want to stay at home.

 

Talk 2 :

Anna : I will meet Judith this afternoon. Would you like to go along with me, Maria?

Maria : I am afraid, I can’t. I have an appointment with my professor at that time.

Anna : That’s no problem.

 

Talk 3 :

Betty : Do you prefer singing to dancing, Miley?

Miley : I would rather sing than dance.

Betty : Great. It is my great pleasure to invite you to sing my favorite song at my birthday party.

Miley : What is your favorite song?

Betty : My heart will go on by Celine Dion

Miley : Okay. I’ll do that.

 

Talk 4 :

Monica : I would like to drink a glass of ice tea in the canteen. Would you mind treating me, Bob?

Bobby : I am sorry. I don’t have much money.

Monica : All you got to say is like that.

 

Talk 5 :

Judy : Clem, I heard that our boss is going to fire Ducan next Saturday.

Clement : I am sorry to hear that. What is he going to do next?

Judy : I suggested him to apply another job.

Proper vocabulary based on the purpose and the target Media used for the messageThe message lengthThe clarity of the message
