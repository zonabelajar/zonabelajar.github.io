Good morning Ladies and Gentlemen. My dear friends and my honorable teachers. I am here to deliver my speech about the important of health for our life. Tomorrow we will celebrate the National Health day on the twelft of November. For that i would like to remind again why it is important to keep our body fit.

Everyone here wants to be healthy and fit, right? As we all know that health is the most important factor in life. It is also the cheapest thing to acquire. We don’t need to buy many expensive stuff to be healthy. With good eating habits and doing regular physical activity we are one step closer to a healthier life. It sounds easy, but sometimes not so easy to do. With school, homework, many school activities, and hanging out with our friends, we are too busy to lead a healthy life. But then, if we neglect it, if we force our body to do all of those activities without taking care of it. Then eventually, we can get sick. And when we get sick, we cannot go to school, we cannot join school activities, and we cannot even have fun. As the saying goes, “An ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure.” It means that being healthy is much cheaper than being sick. Because we can imagine the cost that we have to spend to go to the doctor and buy the medicines. So which one do you prefer? Being sick or being healthy?

And while we are it, Staying healthy is an important part of growing up. Living a healthy lifestyle also can be beneficial not only physically abut also emotionally. In puberty, we have to deal with many problematic things in school, staying healthy can really help not to be overwhelmed. Staying healthy physically can help us stay healthy emotionally too. Keeping a good eating habit and taking care of our body will make our body string and help us to cope with stress. In a study conducted by researchers at University of Indonesia it was found that exercising for 30 minutes, three times a week can help an estimated 60% of patients overcome their depression without using anti-depressant medications. In addition, exercise can give us the time we need to relax, take a break and deal with the problem at hand.

The honorable Audiences, leading a healthy lifestyle can also improve the appearance of our skin, teeth and nails and give us that certain glow that makes us look great. So we will look good just by being healthy. We don’t need to do a certain diet to get slimmer or stronger. Our body will automatically shaped well as we grow into adulthood.

Ladies and Gentlemen, that is why we have to live healthy. By being healthy we can have a good life with full excitement without having to worry about being sick or about the cost of medical treatment. Secondly, aside of physically, staying healthy also can be good for us emotionally. It can’t reduce stress and keep us happy. And the last but not the least, leading a healthy lifestyle will makes us look good. And for us teenagers, looking good is important, right?

So maybe that’s all from what I can deliver today. I hope we will always stay happy and live a full and happy life. I thank you for your attention here.

15. contoh cerita singkat dalam bahasa Inggris✨ayolah aku pasti bisa nyusul mereka✨​


An Old Man in the Village

An old man lived in the village. He was one of the most unfortunate people in the world. The whole village was tired of him; he was always gloomy, he constantly complained and was always in a bad mood.

The longer he lived, the more bile he was becoming and the more poisonous were his words. People avoided him because his misfortune became contagious. It was even unnatural and insulting to be happy next to him.

He created the feeling of unhappiness in others.

But one day, when he turned eighty years old, an incredible thing happened. Instantly everyone started hearing the rumour that the Old Man had become happy, he doesn’t complain about anything anymore, always smiles, and even his face is freshened up.

The whole village gathered together. The villagers asked the old man about what happened.

He answers, “Nothing special. Eighty years I’ve been chasing happiness, and it was useless. And then I decided to live without happiness and just enjoy life. That’s why I’m happy now.”

Moral of the story: Jangan mengejar kebahagiaan. Nikmatilah hidupmu.

17. buatlah teks deskripsi tentang mengajak hidup sehat dalam bahasa Inggris​


Let’s Go! Helps to significantly decrease childhood obesity rates by increasing opportunities for healthy eating and active living where children and families live, learn, work and play. Our vision is to improve the lives of children and families we serve by increasing healthy eating and active living behaviors. The strategies for success are evidence based and align with national recommendations.

Our 5 priority strategies:

1. Limit unhealthy choices for snacks and celebrations; provide healthy choices.
2. Limit or eliminate sugary drinks; provide water.
3. Prohibit the use of food as a reward.
4. Provide opportunities to get physical activity every day.
5. Limit recreational screen time.

28. bikin prosedur teks hidup sehat bahasa Inggris​


How to live a healthy life.

Being active and healthy is the most important thing in everyone life nowadays, because all this new lifestyle with the gadgets and more make human being got a slight declining in their health. How to live a good and healthy life is an easy thing if we have enough intention and motivation to do it. Here are the tips:

First, get yourself a workout at least 3 times a week, find an easy workout and suits your condition. Secondly, you have to get enough sleep daily, for an adult is 7 to 8 hours daily, a good night sleep. Third, you have to drink enough water, at least 8 cups a day, stay hydrated.

Pembahasan

Di dalam teks prosedur (procedure text) di atas kita menerangkan tentang bagaimana caranya hidup sehat (how to live a healthy life). Karena teks prosedur di atas adalah tentang bagaimana cara melakukan sesuatu yang tidak memerlukan peralatan, maka tidak ada bagian bahan-bahan (ingredients) atau peralatan (tools) di dalam teks tersebut. Kamu bisa menambahkan prosedur lainnya ke dalam teks di atas dengan cara menambahkan urutan kalimat dengan kata-kata: next, after that, atau lastly. Kalimatnya bisa kamu buat sendiri dengan cara kamu buat dahulu kalimat dalam bahasa Indonesia lalu terjemahkan ke dalam bahasa Inggris dengan bantuan kamus bahasa Indonesia-Inggris.
