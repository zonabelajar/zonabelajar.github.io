How to make western pancake
Ingridients:
-2 cups (18oz/510g) self-rising or all-purpose flour (See tips below)
-2 or 3 eggs
-1 1/2 cups (350ml) of milk
-1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
-2 tablespoons of butter or vegetable oil
-5 tablespoons of sugar
-1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract (optional)
-A pinch of salt.

How to make :
1.Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat until creamy
2.Melt the butter in a microwave-safe bowl. Make sure that it's completely melted about a minute.
3.Add the butter and milk to the mix. Stir gently.
4. Heat the frying pan to a medium low flame
5. Pour about 3 tablespoons to 1/4 cup batter to frying pan
6.Cook for about two minutes or until the pancake is golden.
7.Enjoy! You can put some toppings to make different.

30. what are the language features of food/medicine lable​


Jawaban:

Food and medicine labels typically contain specific language features to provide essential information to consumers.

Here are some common language features found on food and medicine labels:

Ingredients List: Labels often include a list of ingredients used in the product, typically in descending order of quantity. This helps consumers identify what is in the product.Nutritional Information: Food labels provide information on the nutritional content of the product, including details about calories, fats, carbohydrates, protein, vitamins, and minerals. Medicine labels may include information on active ingredients and dosages.Allergen Information: Labels indicate if the product contains common allergens, such as nuts, soy, dairy, or gluten. This is crucial for individuals with allergies.Usage Directions: Medicine labels provide instructions on how to use the product, including dosage, frequency, and any special considerations (e.g., take with food).Expiration Date: Labels include an expiration date or "use by" date, ensuring that consumers know how long the product remains safe and effective.Storage Instructions: Information on how to store the product, such as temperature and humidity requirements, may be included.Warnings and Precautions: Medicine labels often contain warnings about potential side effects, interactions with other medications, and precautions for specific groups (e.g., pregnant women, children).Dosage Forms: Medicine labels may indicate the form in which the medication is available (e.g., tablets, capsules, liquid).Batch/Lot Numbers: These numbers help track and identify products in case of recalls or quality issues.Barcodes: Barcodes are used for inventory and retail purposes, making it easier to scan and process products at the point of sale.Brand and Manufacturer Information: Labels include the name of the brand or manufacturer, providing consumers with information about the source of the product.Promotional Language: Labels may feature promotional language or claims related to the product's benefits or special features.Government Regulations: Labels often adhere to specific government regulations and guidelines for accuracy and clarity in labeling.Multilingual Information: In some regions, labels may include information in multiple languages to cater to diverse populations.Symbols and Icons: Labels may use symbols and icons to convey information quickly (e.g., recycling symbols, allergy icons).

31. contoh tesis statement tentang fast food dalam bahasa inggris​


Junk Food

Junk food is identical with fast food. This type of food is generally consumed by urban residents who have limited time so that they choose to consume this food that does not need to wait for a long time to eat. Junk food is kind of food that is not advisable to be consumed too often because this type of food is lack of nutritional value but it contains high enough calories, fat, and sugar. Junk food has become part of people’s life in the urban areas that are very busy with the activity of work. Junk food includes a hamburger, hot dogs, French fries, carbonated drink, candy and various types of cake that use an artificial sweetener.

Too often consuming junk food can cause bad impacts for your health. The low nutritional content as well as the high calories conceived by junk food can trigger various diseases. The most possible impact of too much consuming junk food is obesity. Besides, junk food can also increase the risk of diabetes, a heart attack, as well as hypertension.

A study carried out by the University of Minnesota School of Public Health, researched the harmful effects of junk food. This research obtain the conclusion that eating fast food twice or more in a week have the possibility of 27 percent larger to develop diabetes and 56 percent die because of diseases of the heart, compared to participants who seldom or never eat fast food. Even the participants that are known eat fast food four times or more in a week, have the risk of death from a heart attack which increase to 80 percent. Western people are fond of consuming junk food so that their insulin hormone remains high the rest of the day. This case can lead to metabolic processes in the body to be disrupted as well as raising the risk of diabetes type 2.

The women who are pregnant should avoid eating junk food. Eating junk food is not only bad herself but also for the baby. The baby will be so vulnerable to diabetes and hypertension. Besides, a number of studies in china revealed that the rate of breast cancer in the country has increased due to the modern lifestyle that people consume junk food too often.

Furthermore, a research from the University of Adelaide shows that eating full of junk food for children will make their IQ two points lower than a child who does not eat much junk food. Even though the IQ difference is not so subtle, this study proves that children’s way of eating at age of 6 to 24 months give small but significant effects on their IQ when they reach eight years old. Therefore, it is important for parents to consider the long-term impact of food that is given to their children. In addition, some junk foods have the addictive substances as well as containing an artificial sweetener. These substances will make a child become more hyper and easily distracted. This will cause the child, especially a male, to like fighting.

Although junk foods taste good and can be obtained easily and quickly, it has enough risk to health so that you are advised not to consume this kind of food too often. It will be better if we include green vegetables and fruits to consume. A study conducted by the University of Leicester found that green vegetables and fruits are able to reduce the risk of diabetes type 2 until 14 percent.


32. Apa habitat, Activity, food , chraakteristic rusa dalam bahasa inggris


Describing Animals: Deer

Habitat

Deer live in grasslands, prairies & plains, mountains are forests, including hardwoods, coniferous and tropical forests.


Food

Deer are herbivores, they eat plants, fruits, acorns and nuts. During the autumn season, these type of things are more rare, so deer switch to grass and evergreen plants. In the winter season they eat whatever food available, for example like fallen leaves, twigs, bushes and woody plants.


Activity

Deer are active animals, especially during dusk when they leave their beds to go out and looking for food. They rest during the night, sometimes they even looking for food too at such time. Deer live in groups.


Characteristic

Deer have long, powerful legs, diminutive ail and long ears. They are in various size, but the largest could weigh up to 450 kilograms. Male deers are larger than the females.


Pembahasan

Untuk mengidentifikasi binatang berdasarkan informasi yang ada di tabel, pertama-tama kita harus mengetahui apakah itu pengertian dari habitat, food, activity dan characteristic. Berikut adalah penjelasannya.


Habitat is the natural home or environment of an animal, plant or other organism. Sehingga habitat adalah suatu tempat atau lokasi alami dari suatu binatang, tumbuhan atau organisme.


Food is any nutritious substance that people or animals eat or drink or that plants absorbs in order to maintain life and growth. Sederhananya, food adalah makanan yang dikonsumsi oleh manusia, binatang, atau yang diserap tumbuhan agar dapat bertumbuh dan bertahan hidup.


Activity is the condition in which things are happening or being done. Jadi, activity pada dasarnya adalah kegiatan yang dilakukan oleh binatang tersebut.


Characteristic is a typical or noticeable quality of someone or something. Karakteristik merupakan ciri khas yang jelas tentang sesuatu atau seseorang. Sehingga untuk identifikasi binatang, yang dilihat adalah ciri-ciri fisik dari binatang.
