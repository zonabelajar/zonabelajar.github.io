Contoh Personal Letter 1

Apt BLK 30, Eunos St. #05 – 116

Singapore 1441

2nd April 2009

Dear Liza,

I have received you letter asking me to meet you at your house this Sunday to discuss the urgent matter you have. I regret however to say that it will not be possible for me to see you on Sunday as I already have a previous appointment . I shall see you on Monday next week at 5 p.m . Your sincerely

Daisy

Liza sayang,

Saya telah menerima surat Anda yang meminta saya untuk menemui Anda di rumah Anda hari Minggu ini untuk membahas masalah mendesak yang Anda miliki. Namun saya menyesal mengatakan bahwa tidak mungkin bagi saya untuk melihat Anda pada hari Minggu karena saya sudah memiliki janji sebelumnya. Aku akan menemuimu pada hari Senin minggu depan jam 5 malam. Berharap perubahan tanggal akan nyaman bagi Anda.

Yang Tulus,

Daisy

6. tolong buatkan personal letter beserta strukturnya terimakasih (bahasa Inggris)​


Jawaban:

Jl. Merdeka No. 10

Jakarta 12345

21 Mei 2023

Amelia Putri

Jl. Jendral Sudirman No. 25

Jakarta 12346

Dear Amelia,

I hope this letter finds you well. It's been a while since we last spoke, and I wanted to take a moment to catch up.

Since we last talked, I've been busy with work and my family. I recently took a trip to Europe with my husband, and we had a wonderful time exploring different cities and trying new foods. How about you? What have you been up to?

I miss our conversations and would love to hear more about what's going on in your life. I hope we can catch up soon, either over the phone or in person.

Take care, and I look forward to hearing from you soon.

Sincerely,

Lisa

Penjelasan:

struktur personal letter dalam bahasa Inggris:

[Alamat pengirim]

[Tanggal]

[Alamat penerima]

Dear [nama penerima],

Opening: Buka surat dengan ucapan salam, contohnya "I hope this letter finds you well" atau "How are you doing?"

First paragraph: Mulailah surat dengan menjelaskan mengapa Anda menulis. Bisa jadi Anda ingin mengucapkan terima kasih, meminta maaf, atau hanya ingin mengirimkan kabar baik.

Second paragraph: Jelaskan lebih lanjut mengenai topik yang ingin Anda sampaikan. Anda bisa berbicara tentang kegiatan terbaru Anda, atau berbagi pengalaman dan pendapat Anda mengenai suatu topik.

Third paragraph: Sampaikan pesan akhir Anda, seperti harapan yang baik atau permintaan untuk menjaga hubungan baik.

Closing: Tutup surat dengan kata-kata ramah seperti "Sincerely" atau "Best regards", dan tandatangani dengan nama Anda.

Sincerely,

[Your name]

10. buatlah 1 contoh personal letter dalam (bahasa Inggris)beserta artinya ya..terimakasih​


Jawaban:

Dear Anna,

How are you my friend? I hope you are fine. I am writing this letter to tell you that I really miss you. It is too bad that we couldn’t spend this holiday together. I spent my holiday in Jogja, while you spent your holiday in Bali. Do you miss me too?

I visited some places in the city, but among those places, Candi Borobudur left the greatest impression on me. Let me tell you my dear how my tour on Candi Borobudur went. I spent about 1 hours to get to Candi Borobudur from my hotel by public transportations. I arrived at the beautiful location at 16:00 PM. Since I wanted to watch the sunset from the top of Candi Borobudur which was one of the best sunset spots in Jogjakarta. I climb and wait for it till sunset comes. I spent 3 hours there just enjoying the beautiful sunset and gorgeous view which nature offered.

It was the best and great experience, but it’d have been greater and more fun if you’d been there. Miss you so much. I try gudeg, the local culinary, too. It tasted sweet, but delicious. That’s all I can tell you about my holiday in Yogyakarta. I’ll tell you the rest another time. Now that I have shared mine, why don’t you share yours?

I hope you write me back soon dear.

Love,

Frisca

artinya :

Kepada Anna,

Apa kabarmu kawan? Semoga kamu baik-baik saja ya. Aku menulis surat ini untuk mengatakan bahwa aku sangat merindukanmu. Sayang sekali ya kita tidak bisa menghabiskan liburan dengan bersama-sama. Aku menghabiskan liburan di Jogja, sementara kamu menghabiskan liburan di Bali. Apakah kamu juga merindukan aku?

Disini aku mengunjungi beberapa tempat di Jogjakarta, tetapi dari semuanya, Candi Borobudur adalah yang paling mengesankan bagiku. Akan aku ceritakan bagaimana turku di Candi Borobudur berlangsung. Aku memerlukan waktu sekitar 1 jam untuk sampai ke candi Borobudur dari hotel tempatku menginap dengan mempergunakan kendaraan umum. Aku sampai di lokasi pada jam empat sore hari. Karena aku ingin melihat matahari terbenam atau sunset di puncak candi Borobudur yang adalah lokasi paling tepat dan terbaik untuk menyaksikan pemandangan matahari terbenam atau sunset. Aku naik ke puncaknya dan menunggu hingga saat sunset matahari terbenam tiba dan terjadi. Aku berada di lokasi itu sampai sekitar 3 jam. Pemandangannya sangat indah dan alami, natural.

Sungguh pengalaman yang luar biasa. Tetapi, tentunya akan lebih luar biasa dan akan lebih menyenangkan jika kamu juga berada di sini bersama ku, menyaksikan indahnya pemandangan di sini. Aku sangat merindukanmu. Aku sempat mencoba gudeg juga, kuliner hidangan lokal. Rasanya manis tapi cukup lezat. Itulah yang bisa aku ceritakan kepadamu tentang liburanku. Akan aku ceritakan sisanya lain waktu setelah kita bertemu. Sekarang aku telah membagikan cerita liburanku denganmu, bagaimana kalau kamu juga membagikan cerita liburanmu kepadaku?

Aku harapkan balasan surat darimu sesegera mungkin kawan.

Salam sayang,

Frisca

18. tolong buatkan personal letter surat pribadi beserta strukturnya dalam bahasa Inggris ​


Jawaban:

[Your Name]

[Your Address]

[City, State ZIP Code]

[Country]

[Date]

[Recipient's Address]

[City, State ZIP Code]

[Country]

Dear [Recipient's Name],

[Opening Paragraph]: In this paragraph, you should begin your letter by addressing the recipient and express your gratitude or appreciation towards them. You can also share a personal update, such as how you have been doing lately or any significant changes in your life.

[Body Paragraphs]: This is the main part of your letter where you can discuss different topics or ideas that you want to share with the recipient. You can share your thoughts, opinions, feelings, and experiences on various topics such as current events, hobbies, interests, and other personal matters. It is important to maintain a conversational tone and be honest and authentic in your writing.

[Closing Paragraph]: In this paragraph, you should conclude your letter by summarizing your main points and reiterating your appreciation or gratitude towards the recipient. You can also include any plans for the future, such as upcoming events or plans to meet in person.

Struktur :

The 1 part is called the heading. It includes the writer's complete address and the date that the letter was

written. If your letter is full-block style (all flush against the left margin) then the heading and all other parts of

the letter are flush left. In this style the paragraphs are not indented. This is how we will write our letters in class. Just so you know, many people write their letters semi-block style, which means that the heading and closing are tabbed to the middle of the paper and paragraphs are indented.

The 2nd part of a personal letter is called the salutation. The salutation is the opening greeting in any letter. In most personal letters we start the salutation with "Dear (whatever the person's name is)."

The 3rd part of a personal letter is called the body. The body is the "letter" part of the letter. It includes all of the writer's thoughts organized into paragraphs. In a letter, indentation is not required. However, in other writings, it is important to remember to indent.

The 4th part of a personal letter is called the closing. The closing includes the closing greeting followed by empty spaces or blank lines and then the writer's typed or printed first and last name. In the closing greeting only the first word is capitalized. Some examples of good closing greetings are as follows: Yours truly: Sincerely; Friends forever; and Love.

The 5th part of a personal letter is called the signature. The signature is signed in the space between the two parts of the closing. The signature should be written neatly in cursive. It should include your first and last name, or you may sign your first initial and last name.

33. bahasa inggris kelas XI tenses​


A. PAST PERFECT TENSE

Past Perfect Tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan suatu aksi (action) sudah selesai dilakukan pada masa lampau di waktu tertentu sebelum melakukan aksi berikutnya.

Past Perfect Tense juga dapat digunakan membentuk conditional sentence type 3 dan reported speech.

Past Perfect Tense menggunakan auxiliary verb Had + Past Participle (V3) dalam kalimatnya. Baik Singular Subject dan Plural Subject menggunakan bentuk yang sama :

(+) S + Had + V3 + O

(-) S + Had Not + V3 + O

(?) Had + S + V3 + O + ?

Examples :

- I had locked the door before I left my room

(Saya sudah mengunci pintu sebelum saya meninggalkan ruangan saya)

- If they had gone to your house that night, they would have met your parents

(Jika seandainya mereka pergi ke rumahmu malam itu, mereka pasti sudah bertemu orang tuamu)

- Ani said that she had already done her homework

(Ani berkata bahwa dia sudah mengerjakan pekerjaan rumahnya)

- The train hadn't left when you arrived

(Kereta apinya belum berangkat ketika kamu tiba)

- Had Mrs. Laila been a teacher when she got married?

(Apakah Bu Laila sudah menjadi seorang guru ketika dia menikah?)

B. PRESENT PERFECT TENSE

Present Perfect Tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu aksi sudah dimulai sejak masa lampau dalam waktu yang tidak spesifik, tapi aksi tersebut sudah selesai dilakukan di masa lampau pada waktu tertentu, tapi hasilnya masih dirasakan di masa sekarang; ataupun aksi tersebut belum selesai dilakukan, bahkan masih dilakukan hingga masa sekarang.

Keterangan waktu yang biasanya digunakan dalam Present Perfect Tense, antara lain :

- just

- already

- since

- for

- this week/month/year

- this morning/afternoon/evening

- yet

- never/ever

Present Perfect Tense menggunakan auxiliary verb Have/Has + Past Participle (V3) dalam kalimatnya.

Singular Subject (He, She, It) dan Singular Noun (kata benda tunggal) menggunakan Has

Plural Subject (They, I, We, You) dan Plural Noun (kata benda jamak) menggunakan Have

(+) S + Have/Has + V3 + O

(-) S + Have/Has Not + V3 + O

(?) Have/Has + S + V3 + O + ?

Examples :

- She has worked in the hospital since 2000

(Dia sudah bekerja di rumah sakot sejak tahun 2000)

- Mr. Agus and Mr. Budi have been teachers in this school for 20 years

(Pak Agus dan Pak Budi sudah menjadi guru di sekolah ini selama 20 tahun)

- We haven't finished our homework yet

(Kami belum menyelesaikan pekerjaan rumah kami)

- Have you ever seen this movie before?

(Apakah kamu pernah melihat film ini sebelumnya?)

- Lucia has sent the email this morning

(Lucia sudah mengirimkan emailnya pagi ini)

C. FUTURE PERFECT TENSE

Future Perfect Tense adalah tense yang menyatakan sebuah aksi akan diselesaikan di masa yang akan datang. Seseorang yang mengatakan sebuah kalimat dalam bentuk Future Perfect Tense memposisikan dirinya di masa sekarang, dan melihat sebuah aksi diselesaikan di masa yang akan datang.

Keterangan waktu yang biasanya digunakan dalam Future Present Tense, antara lain :

- until

- before/by the end of the day

- by the end of the week/month

- by next week/month/year

- by ...

- in a week/day/month/year

- after/before

Future Perfect Tense menggunakan simple future will have + past participle (V3) dalam kalimatnya. Baik Singular Subject maupun Plural Subject menggunakan bentuk yang sama :

(+) S + Will Have + V3 + O

(-) S + Will Not Have + V3 + O

(?) Will + S + Have + V3 + O + ?

Examples :

- My parents will have been at Singapore until Sunday

(Orangtua saya akan sudah berada di Singapura sanpai hari Minggu)

- He will have arrived home at 4 pm

(Dia akan sudah tiba di rumah pukul 4 sore)

- You won't have finished the task by midnight

(Kamu tidak akan sudah menyelesaikan tugasnya sampai tengah malam)

- Will we have received the package by Tuesday?

(Akankah kami menerima paketnya hari Selasa?)

- Will the shop have closed by 9 pm?

(Akankah tokonya tutup pada pukul 9?)
