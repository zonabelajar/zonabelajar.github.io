11. Directions: Complete thes sentences with either because or because of1. the water in most rivers is unsafe to drink...pollution2. some people think Harry succeeded in business...his charming personality rather than his business skills3. my lecture notes were incomplete...the instructor talked too fast​


Jawaban:

1. the water in most rivers is unsafe to drink because of pollution

2. some people think Harry succeeded in business because of his charming personality rather than his business skills

3. my lecture notes were incomplete because the instructor talked too fast​

Penjelasan:

because of dilanjutkan langsung dengan objek kalimat.

Dalam kalimat 1: because of pollution (pollution adalah objek kalimat)

Kalimat 2: some people think Harry succeeded in business because of his charming personality rather than his business skills

his charming personality adalah objek kalimat

Contoh lain:

I did not go because of the weather (weather adalah objek kalimat)

because (tanpa of) dilanjutkan dengan klausa (kumpulan kata-kata yang mempunyai subjek dan verb, yg bisa berdiri sendiri sebagai kalimat utuh)

Kalimat 3:

My lecture notes were incomplete because the instructor talked too fast​

Kita lihat, setelah because ada klausul the instructor talked too fast​ dimana kata-kata tersebut mempunyai fungsi sama dengan sebuah kalimat utuh.

Jadi kalau kita lepas klausa itu dan bikin satu kalimat tersendiri, sudah bisa:

The Instructor talked too fast.

Contoh lain:

I did not go because the weather was bad.

Setelah kata because, ada klausa the weather was bad, yang terlepas dari kalimat lengkapnya, sudah bisa berdiri sendiri sebagai satu kalimat.

Semoga bisa mengerti perbedaannya.

PEMBAHASAN

A. Present Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan berulang, kebiasaan, rutin dan fakta. Rumusnya:

(+) S + V1 (-s/es) + O

(-) S + do/does not + V1 + O

(?) Do/does + S + V1 + O?

Perintah Soal

Make these incomplete sentences into the correct one using present tense!

=> Buatlah kalimat dengan menggunakan Present Tense

1. The boys / not play / football on weekends.

=> The boys do not play football on weekends

2. We never / watch / TV in the morning.

=> We never watch TV in the morning

3. Water / boil / at 100° C.

=> Water boils at 100° C.

4. My father / not like / coffee.

=> My Father does not like coffee

5. Tarissa / go / to school early.

=> Tarissa goes to school early

B. Present Continuous

=> Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yg terjadi sekarang atau sedang berlangsung saat ini. Rumusnya:

(+) S + is/am/are + V-ing + O

(-) S + is/am/are + not + V-ing + O

(?) is/am/are + S + V-ing + O?

Perintah Soal

Make these incomplete sentences into the correct one using present continuous tense!

=> Buatlah kalimat tak lengkap berikut ke dalam kalimat Present Continuous

6. The boys / play / football now.

=> The boys are playing football now

7. Najwa / sing / in the bathroom.

=> Najwa is singing in the bathroom

8. I / not type / an email right now.

=> I am not typing an email right now

9. She / ride / a camel nicely.

=> She is riding a camel nicely

10. Wahyu and his friends / have / breakfast now.

=> Wahyu and his friends are having breakfast now

32. incomplete adjective clauses


Jawaban:

An adjective clause—also called an adjectival or relative clause—will meet three requirements:

First, it will contain a subject and verb.

Next, it will begin with a relative pronoun [who, whom, whose, that, or which] or a relative adverb [when, where, or why].

Finally, it will function as an adjective, answering the questions What kind? How many? or Which one?

Menjawab paragraf rumpang

Dalam menjawab sebuah paragraf rumpang, kita perlu mengetahui konteks dari cerita tersebut. Gunakan kamus untuk mengetahui arti atau makna dari kata kata yang sulit. Bila sudah disediakan pilihan kata sebagai jawaban, cobalah untuk mengartikannya tiap kata. Hal ini selain untuk menambah vocabulary, tapi juga membantu anda dalam menjawab pertanyaan tersebut.

Pembahasan

Karena tidak ada pilihan kata sebagai jawaban, maka saya akan menebak jawaban dengan kata yang sesuai.

Read the incomplete paragraph below and complete it with suitable word, phrase, or expression.
