1. Kalian mau tau bahasa Inggris? nih ... Bahasa Inggrisnya diantara between dan dibawah under disebelah next to disamping beside dibelakang behind yaa​


Jawaban:

Di antara: among, between

Di bawah: under, below, beneath

Di sebelah: next to, beside

Di samping: beside, next to

Di belakang: behind, at the back of

Note:

Thank you ya sekarang aku udah tau bahasa Inggris.


2. buatlah sebuah kalimat bahasa inggris menggunakan kata (under, beside, near, between at behind, in front of)


my home in front of my friend homeMAPEL : BAHASA INGGRIS
MATERI :
KELAS : 8
KODE SOAL : 8.5
KODE KATEGORISASI : 8.5.2

PEMBAHASAN
=================
Contoh kalimat dari (under, beside, near, between, at, behind, in front of) adalah..

UNDER = dibawah
The cat is UNDER the chair.
=> kucing berada di bawah kursi.

BESIDE = disamping
Maya sits BESIDE me.
=> Maya duduk disamping saya.

NEAR = Dekat
My school is NEAR from my house.
=> Sekolah saya dekat dari rumah saya.

BETWEEN (di antara)
Our school is BETWEEN the post office and the Police office.
=> Sekolah kami berada di antara kantor pos dan kantor polisi.

AT (di)
They are AT the library
=> Mereka berada di perpustakaan

BEHIND (dibelakang)
Garden is BEHIND our house.
=> Kebun berada di belakang rumah kami.

IN FRONT OF (di depan)
The teacher is IN FRONT OF the class.
=> Guru berada di depan kelas.

15. Cuma quiz tapi jangan asal jawab Bahasa Inggrisnya ditengah adalah... A. In B. On C. Between D. It


Jawaban: C (Between)

Penjelasan:

Arti 'between' adalah 'diantara'. Contoh: She put the box of pepper powder between the salt shaker and the sugar bowl.

'In' artinya 'dalam', 'on' artinya 'pada', dan 'it' artinya 'itu', jadi tidak ada yang relevan.

26. apa arti on in under beside behind next to in frent of acress between near


on = diluar/diatas (your phone is on the table)
in = didalam (My book is in my bag)
under = dibawah (i have this under control)
beside = disamping (i was beside you)
behind = dibelakang (there was someone behind your back)
next = sebelah (there's a flower shop next to the cafe)
to = untuk (i want to give a present to her
in front = didepan (there's a dog in front of me)
across = seberang (there's a garden across the river)
between = antara (this is a secret between me and you)
near = dekat (there's a swimming pool near my house)
