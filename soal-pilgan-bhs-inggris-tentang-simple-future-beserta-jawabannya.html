2. ibu memasak nasi besok.ksih bhs inggris simple future tense


Tomorrow mom will cook rice! Kalau kegiatan memasaknya sudah direncanakan sebelumnya, maka kalimatnya menjadi :
Mom is going to cook rice tomorrow.

Kalau keputusan untuk memasak itu baru dibuat pada saat itu juga (dadakan), maka :
Mom will cook rice tomorrow.

Tapi, kalo saya lihat dari pertanyaan kamu, kegiatan 'memasak' itu sudah direncanakan. Jadi pakai yang is going to.

25. Buatlh text future plan... . Rencanamu dmasa yg akn dtg dlm bhs Inggris dgn menerapkn pola simple present future s+will+V1...minimal 2 paragraf​


Jawaban:

My name ( Nama kamu ). I often think about the future.  I will be a flight attendant in the future. By becoming a flight attendant, I will greet everyone, give a sense of comfort and accompany the passengers on the plane to their destination. I will wear a colorful uniform and smile beautifully when greeting and saying goodbye to passengers. It must be a fun job.But, I also hope one day I also wish to become a veterinarian. I will inspect and save all kinds of animals. Veterinarians are a noble job.

From tomorrow I will study hard and obey the school rules. That way I will get high scores and be able to continue my education to achieve my goals in the future. I will make my parents proud of me.

Penjelasan:

Simple future tense biasa digunakan untuk menyatakan prediksi atau untuk menunjukkan kemampuan atau niat. Dengan rumus S + will/shall + V-1 + O atau S + am/is/are + going to + V-1 + O, bentuk kalimat simple future tense selalu memakai will/shall/going to.

Fungsi dan penggunaan simple future tense dapat digunakan untuk beberapa macam kondisi, diantaranya : untuk kejadian yang akan terjadi pada waktu yang akan datang atau melakukan suatu tawaran terhadap orang lain. Keterangan waktunya yaitu : next month, next time, in a little while atau yang lainnya dan ciri-ciri simple future tense salah satunya adalah predikat kalimat : “will + infinitive.”

30. Function dari simple future, future continous, future perfect ? Jawabnya jangan asal-asalan, pake bahasa inggris


function of simple future is to tell someone about an activity that relates with future action. example given is SHE WILL STUDY TOMORROW (dia akan belajar besok)
function of future continuous is to tell someone about an activity that relates with future action and is about to be in progress, example given is SHE WILL BE COOKING TOMORROW (dia akan sedang masak besok) 
function of future perfect is to tell someone about an activity that relates with future action and is about to complete/finish at a certain time, example given SHE WILL HAVE COME (dia akan sudah datang/akan hampir datang) - Function of Simple Future tense is used to express events or actions that will be done or will be occur in the future.

- Function of Future Continuous tense is used to express actions or event that to be going on at a certain time in the future. But, this actions has been started but not yet completed at that time.

-Function of Future perfect tense is used to express that an actions will have finished in the future..

40. apa rumus dari simple future dan future continous. beserta contoh!


simple future tense :
. Verbal Sentences :
 (+) S+Will+V1+o (-) S+Will+not+V1+o (?) Will+S+V1+o Example : (+)  She will meet me tomorrow.
 (-)  She will’ not meet me tomorrow
(?)  Will she meet me tomorrow ? 
future continus tense :
Formula’s : (+) S + will + be + v-ing+ O
(-) S + will not +be + V- ing + O
(?) Will + S+ be + V-ing + O Example : (+)  He will be teaching in my house tomorrow. (-)  He will not be teaching in my house tomorrow (?)  Will he be teaching in my house tomorrow ?
. 




Rumus simple future : sebject+will/shall+verb1+object
contoh : we shall go to jakarta tomorrow.
Rumus future continous : subject+be+verb-ing+object
contoh : she will be cleaning the floor
