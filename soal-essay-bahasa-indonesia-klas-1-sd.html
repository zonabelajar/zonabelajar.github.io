Diketahui umur Kelvin kurang dari 50  tahun. Saat  ini umurnya merupakan kelipatan 6 dan

tahun depan umurnya merupakan kelipatan 5. Berapakah umur Kelvin sekarang?

Jawab :

Kemungkinan umur Kelvin sekarang yaitu kelipatan 6 yang kurang dari 50

6  12  18  24  30  36  42  48

Jadi, kemungkinan umur Kelvin tahun depan adalah

6+1  12+1  18+1  24+1  30+1  36+1  42+1  48+1

  7    13    19    25    31    37    43    49

Karena umur Kelvin tahun depan merupakan kelipatan 5, dan bilangan kelipatan 5 hanya 25

maka umur Kelvin tahun depan adalah 25 umur Kelvin sekarang adalah 24.

My house is often affected by motor vehicle volution, but we are vigilant to anticipate it by not opening the door during the day, except when some of our families leave the house and immediately close it again so that the volume does not easily enter the house.

But there are also shortcomings in the neighborhood around the house, which is unpleasant every day often smells of sewage such as sewage water, garbage, etc. What causes the sting to sting our noses is rubbish that has been scattered on the sewers and then rot and finally cause a very unpleasant odor, what else when it rains heavily the water likes to quickly rise to the top so that frequent flooding in my area, which is caused by rubbish scattered on the sewers that are unable to flow water because too much garbage is scattered and clogged in the drain. To overcome this so as not to be affected by flooding in our area, we all residents often clean the waterways or industrial waste disposal so that where later it will rain heavily water will flow towards him.

And the garbage that is scattered about is taken up by us and we dispose of it in its place, besides that it is often held spraying to eradicate dengue mosquitoes that is something that our citizens often do to avoid any disaster or disaster. At night the environment arrived at my home, it was not crowded, even at 9 am it was very quiet, I don't know if the residents were lacking or indeed it was always quiet every night, but there was a good feeling when it was lonely at night, even if it was crowded I would be hard to sleep, most only the sound of motor cars passing in the middle of the night.
