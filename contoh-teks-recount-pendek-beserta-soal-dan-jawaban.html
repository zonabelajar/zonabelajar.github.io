2. contoh recount teks yang pendek!


Recount text adalah teks yang menceritakan pengalaman penulis di waktu lampau (recount text is a text which retells the writer's past experience). Karena berbentuk lampau, maka tense yang digunakan adalah past tense. Dan karena menceritakan pengalaman penulis, kata ganti yang digunakan adalah kata ganti orang pertama tunggal (I, my) dan kata ganti orang pertama jamak (we).

Pembahasan

Contoh recount text pendek yaitu

Parangtritis Beach

         Last holiday, my family had a vacation. We went to Parangtritis beach It is located in the south of Yogyakarta city. We leaved at 05.30 a.m. and we arrived at 06.30 a.m. It was the time of sunrise.

         I saw sunrise and the wave of the Indian Ocean. I played a kite with my sister and  also made sand castle with my family. When I was tired, I sat on the sand and enjoyed the sea wind. At 11 a.m my family ate seafood in the food court around the beach. After lunch, we leaved the beach and went home. I felt very tired but I was happy. Because I could go with my family.

"Let's set up our tent here," said our leader. The we started to work. We need two poles, some ropes, and pegs for the tent. In a short time the tent was ready. 12. contoh text recount beserta soal dan pembahasan


A Trip to the Zoo

Yesterday my family and I went to the zoo to see the elephant. When we got to the zoo, we went to the shop to buy some food to give to the animals.

After getting the food, we went to the nocturnal house where we saw birds and reptiles which only come out at night.

Before having lunch, we went for a ride on the elephant. It was a thrill to ride it. Dad fell off when he let go off the rope, but he was ok.

During the lunch we fed some birds in the park. In the afternoon we saw the animals being fed. When we returned home we were very tired but happy. It was because we had so much fun activities at many places at the zoo.

1. What happened to the writer's dad when he rode an elephant?
A. He felt a thrill
B. He felt fun
C. He fell off
D. He failed
Answer : C

2. Why did the writer and his family feel very tired after having a trip to the zoo?
A. They had to visit many places in the zoo
B. They took a long time to reach the zoo area
C. They had to feed a lot of animals in the zoo
D. They had no time to take a rest in the zoo
Answer : A

3. ... our family felt tired after visiting the zoo, we were still happy.
A. Since
B. Because
C. Although
D. Nevertheless
Answer : C

39. Contoh teks recount beserta penjelasannya menyangkut definisi,struktur,cara membuat, teks recount​


recount text adalah salah satu bentuk teks yang menceritakan kisah-kisah di masa lampau baik pengalaman pribadi, pengalaman kelompok, atau segala event-event tertentu yang ditulis dalam bahasa inggris.

—

Struktru Recount Text:

1. Orientation (Pembuka)

Orientation berisi awalan cerita. Bagian ini biasanya menjelaskan tentang latar cerita seperti tempat dan waktu kejadian.

2. Event (Isi)

Event berisi inti cerita dari recount text. Bagian ini menjelaskan kejadian secara berurutan dari kejadian A ke kejadin B.

3. Reorientation (Kesimpulan atau Penutup Teks)

Reorientation berisi pengulangan dan penutup jalannya cerita.

—

Contoh:

Orientation

Last year holiday of Lebaran I went to Surabaya visiting my grandmother and grandfather.

I had to go there because the previous holiday I wasn’t there.

Event

I missed them so much because I lived with them when I was a child.

At that time both of my parents had to go to another city to earn money so they left me with my grandma and grandpa.

After graduated from junior high school, I follow my parents living at Jakarta. Since that, I had visited them only at holiday time.

Unfortunately, I didn’t have any ticket of either train and plane so that I went to Surabaya by bus. It was so tiring because the traffic was too crowded and stagnant.

Normally, the trip needs around 24-28 hours but my trip at that time took 40 hours from Jakarta to Surabaya. That made me mad.

Reorientation

The madness and tiredness during the trip had gone after I met my grandma and grandpa. They were looked old. I was so happy knowing them in healthy condition.
