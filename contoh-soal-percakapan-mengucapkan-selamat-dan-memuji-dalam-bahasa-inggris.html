6. Percakapan 3 orang tentang ucapan selamat dalam bahasa inggris!


Congratulating atau ucapan selamat merupakan ucapan yang kita berikan kepada seseorang karena hal yang dicapainya. Berikut ini adalah percakapan 3 orang dengan ucapan selamat dalam bahasa Inggris.

A: Who won the painting competition last week?

B: I did, I won the first prize.

C: Really? Congratulations, B.

A: Well done, I am glad to hear that.

B: Thank you. What about your bike race, C?

C: Wish me luck, B. I will take part in a bike race next week.

B: Sure, Good luck. I hope you will win the race.

A: Sure, C. I hope you will win the first prize too, like B.

C: Thank you so much.

Pembahasan

Arti dari dialog di atas adalah

A: Siapa yang memenangkan kompetisi melukis minggu lalu?

B: Saya memenangkan hadiah pertama.

C: Benarkah? Selamat, B

A: Bagus sekali, saya senang mendengarnya.

B: Terima kasih. Bagaimana dengan balap sepedamu, C?

C: Doakan saya beruntung, B. Saya akan mengikuti balapan sepeda minggu depan.

B: Tentu, semoga berhasil. Saya harap kamu akan memenangkan perlombaan.

A: Tentu, C. Saya harap kamu akan memenangkan hadiah pertama juga, seperti B.

Perhatikan dalam menulis congratulations, harus diakhiri dengan s. Meski bukan bentuk jamak, penulisan congratulations harus seperti ini.

Congratulation Expression

Congratulation expression adalah ekspresi atau ungkapan yang kita ucapkan ketika seseorang meraih keberhasilan atau mencapai sukses dalam meraih atau melakukan sesuatu.

Contoh situasi dimana kita memberikan ucapan selamat :

Ulang tahun : Happy birthday Astri! Wishes you the best birthday ever!

Hari Raya keagamaan : Happy Eid Mubarak, Merry Christmas

Promosi pekerjaan : Congratulation on your promotion!, Good luck!, Best wishes for your new career!

Lulus ujian/graduation: Happy Graduation!, Congratulation!


Setelah memahami tentang expression congratulation dan bagaimana meresponnya, berikut contoh expression congratulation dalam dialog.

Situation : Maya get a scholarship to continue her study. Beni and Astri heard about this and both of them are happy for Maya and congratulate her.


Beni : "Hi Astri, do you know that Maya get a scholarship to continue her study?"

Astri : "Oh really?I am really happy for her and she deserved it. Where did she get the scholarship from?

Beni : "She's got the scholarship from the Education Department."

Astri : "That's a good one. Nowadays, it's pretty hard to get a scholarship, the competition is high."

Beni : "Yes, that's true. She has been study hard to get one."

Then, Maya join the conversation.

Astri : "Hi Maya, congratulation for winning the scholarship!"

Maya : Ohh..thank you very much."

Beni : "Yes, congratulation Maya, you deserved it. I am really happy for you and best wishes for your study."

Maya : "Thanks Ben!"

Astri : "So, when are you going to start your study?"

Maya : "Next year, I am going to the preparation session first to study Japanese six months and then I am going to Osaka next year."

Astri : " Good to hear that, I wish I can get a scholarship to finish my study,"

Maya : "There will be a new recruitment the next three months for other scholarship maybe you can search and find out if you meet the requirements. There's plenty of time for preparation."

Astri : "Thank you, I'll search the informations, hope I can meet the requirements.

Maya :"Yes, Good luck for your searching!"

Astri : "Thanks Maya, Good luck for your study!"

Beni : "See you later, Good luck for your study Maya!"

Maya : "Thanks Ben and Astri."
