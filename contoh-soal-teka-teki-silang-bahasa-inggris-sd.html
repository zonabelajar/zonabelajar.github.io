Jawaban:

Across

1. Simon Bolivar flounders to end colonialism.

2. Who was the first woman prime minister of Sri Lanka?

4. The bedroom is really cozy and comfortable.

6. I don’t like visiting my parents and other relatives.

7. Nelson Mandela spent 30 years in prison.

9. There’s lots of room in my house – it’s quite spacious.

13. The customer ordered a fish curry with rice.

14. The opposite of old.  

15. I read the newspaper before I went to bed last night.

16. Past tense of ‘eat’.

Down

1. A person who enjoys meeting other people is friendly.

2. The waiter took my order about an hour ago.

3. My father’s brother is my uncle.

5. Ni Ni’s kitchen is always clean and tidy

7. Bill Clinton was the president of the United States.

8. I went out of the house and walked down the street.

10. Did you put salt in the rice?

11. May May spend a lot of money on her new car.

12. When he grew up, he became rich.

13. Che Guevara wanted better conditions for poor people.

Untuk bagian mendatar 14. lawan kata old adalah New.

Untuk bagian mendatar 16. Verb-2 dari eat adalah ate.
