How to Make A Original Martabak

Mix dry flour, 2 tablespoons of sugar, yeast, baking powder, milk powder, and water. Stir the mixture until it's completely mixed and there's nothing to clot.

If using double acting baking powder (BPDA), leave the mixture for 30 minutes. If using single-acting baking powder, just leave it alone for 5-10 minutes.

While the mixture is left idle, place 3 tablespoons of margarine in a pan or Teflon pan. Melt on the stove fire. Then let it sit until it cools slightly.

Shake off the chicken eggs until no white parts clot again.

Add liquid margarine to the mixture that has been left alone. Also add the beaten egg.

Heat the Teflon on low heat, then pour the mixture to fill half the teflon. Shake the Teflon pan so that the dough sticks to the surface of the pan and forms a fringe.

Wait until the martabak fringes are dry and the center is bubbling. After that, sprinkle the remaining 1 tablespoon of sugar on the surface.

Cover the Teflon pan, let stand for about 5 minutes. After that, lift the martabak from the stove.

While still hot, spread the martabak with the remaining 1 tablespoon of margarine. Pour sweetened condensed milk. Cut and give toppings according to taste.

26. contoh kalimat "memberi instruksi" dalam bahasa inggris


Giving Instructions

The examples:

Wash your hand regularly and thoroughly.Do the task by following the instruction below.Submit your assignment on time. Brush the pan with cooking oil. Heat a steamer pan.

The translation:

Cuci tangan mu secara teratur dan menyeluruh. Kerjakan tugas dengan mengikuti instruksi di bawah ini. Kirimkan tugas mu tepat waktu. Olesi wajan dengan minyak goreng. Panaskan panci kukusan.

PEMBAHASAN:

Giving instruction is an expression that used to give or ask somebody to do something that what we want.

Ada dua bentuk pemberian instruksi, yaitu:

1. Verbal instruction

Rumus

Verb 1 + object!

Contoh:

Open your book.Read the text and answer the questions.

2. Nominal instruction

Rumus

Be + adjective/noun/adverb !

Contoh :

Be careful.Be brave.

37. percakapan tentang memberi instruksi pake bahasa inggris


Potato omelette

ingredients: 
4 eggs 
15 new potatoes 
2 onions 
3 tablespoons olive oil 

Recipe: 
Wash, peel and thinly slice potatoes. 
Slice onions. 
Beat the eggs in a bowl, season. 
Heat oil in a frying pan, sauté onions. 
Add potatoes and leave to cook over a low heat for 20 or 30 minutes. 
Leave to cool and add beaten egg. 
Cook for 15 minutes in frying pan. 

Advice: 
Finish off in oven for 10 minutes. 
Serve your omelette in pieces as a snack or starter, or as a main course with a light green salad and some Roquefort. 
Eat with sangria.
