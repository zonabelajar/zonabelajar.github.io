9. soal memmo bahasa inggris beserta pembahasan


Berikut adalah contoh soal mengenai memo dalam bahasa Inggris beserta pembahasannya:

Read this following text to answer questions number 1 to 3!

Dear Rihanna,

Don’t forget, we have had an appointment to go swimming at 3 pm. I’ll be waiting at home. Please be prepared to go after lunch and a nap. Remember to bring your suit. And don’t forget we’ll go there by bicycle. See you later!

Rai

1. When will they go to swim?

a. In the early morning

b. Before midnight

c. After breakfast

d. In the evening

2. Where will Rai waiting for Rihanna?

a. At the park

b. At the swimming pool

c. At her home

d. At the bus stop

3. What should Rihanna do before go to swim?

a. Make an appointment by pool’s website

b. Have a nap and lunch

c. Have a nap while ride a bicycle

d. Have a lunch and bring a swimsuit

Pembahasan

Memo adalah sebuah catatan singkat yang bertujuan untuk mengingatkan seseorang dalam melakukan sesuatu.

Berikut adalah pembahasan untuk masing-masing pertanyaan tentang memo tersebut:

1. Kapan mereka akan pergi berenang?

a. Pagi-pagi buta

b. Sebelum tengah malam

c. Setelah sarapan

d. Di sore hari

Karena pada teks disebutkan bahwa mereka akan pergi pada 3 pm (15.00), maka jawaban yg tepat adalah di sore hari atau in the evening (D).

2. Di mana Rai akan menunggu Rihanna?

a. Di taman

b. Di kolam renang

c. Di rumahnya

d. Di pemberhentian bus

Pada teks, kita bisa melihat ada kalimat “I’ll be waiting at home.” yang berarti aku tunggu di rumahku. Sehingga jawaban yang tepat adalah at her home (C).

3. Apa yang harus Rihanna lakukan sebelum pergi berenang?

a. Membuat janji di website kolam renang

b. Tidur siang dan makan siang

c. Tidur siang sambil naik sepeda

d. Makan siang dan membawa baju renang.

Kita bisa melihat suruhan Rere untuk “Please be prepared to go after lunch and a nap.” Yang berarti tolong bersiap-siap untuk pergi setelah makan siang dan tidur. Sehingga jawaban yang tepat adalah Take a nap and lunch (B).

13. teks report dalam bahasa indonesia dan bahasa inggris menurut strukturnya


Komodo Dragon.

Komodo dragons have thrived in the harsh climate of Indonesia’s Lesser Sunda Islands for millions of years, although amazingly, their existence was unknown to humans until about 100 years ago.

Reaching 10 feet (3 meters) in length and more than 300 pounds (136 kilograms), Komodo dragons are the heaviest lizards on Earth. They have long, flat heads with rounded snouts, scaly skin, bowed legs, and huge, muscular tails.

As the dominant predators on the handful of islands they inhabit, they will eat almost anything, including carrion, deer, pigs, smaller dragons, and even large water buffalo and humans. When hunting, Komodo dragons rely on camouflage and patience, lying in wait for passing prey. When a victim ambles by, the dragon springs, using its powerful legs, sharp claws and serrated, shark-like teeth to eviscerate its prey.

Animals that escape the jaws of a Komodo will only feel lucky briefly. Dragon saliva teems with over 50 strains of bacteria, and within 24 hours, the stricken creature usually dies of blood poisoning. Dragons calmly follow an escapee for miles as the bacteria takes effect, using their keen sense of smell to hone in on the corpse. A dragon can eat a whopping 80 percent of its body weight in a single feeding.

There is a stable population of about 3,000 to 5,000 Komodo dragons on the islands of Komodo, Gila Motang, Rinca, and Flores. However, a dearth of egg-laying females, poaching, human encroachment, and natural disasters has driven the species to endangered status.

Computers are a machine that handles information automatically. It can perform calculations and process data dan work with numbers to solve problems in a few seconds. It also can remember a great deal of information. So, it can be widely used in places, such as banks, offices, schools, courses, counters, or companies.

The computers work like calculators. Users have to command them to do this by pressing various buttons. To do the same work, users must give instructions to the computer. It calls a computer program. If we set up a program, the computer can provide a program and do all this work automatically without further help or instructions.

Pembahasan

Report text atau disebut juga dengan teks report merupakan sebuah laporan teks yang memberikan informasi mengenai suatu hal yang sebenarnya terjadi dari berbagai penelitian sistematis atau bersumber dari hasil analisis. Dalam teks report di atas, unsur kebahasaannya adalah sebagai berikut.

General Classification :

Corona virus is a deadly virus Corona virus or severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2) is a virus that attacks the respiratory system. Disease due to infection with this virus is called COVID-19

Description :

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus.

Most people infected with the COVID-19 virus will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and recover without requiring special treatment. Older people, and those with underlying medical problems like cardiovascular disease, diabetes, chronic respiratory disease, and cancer are more likely to develop serious illness.

The best way to prevent and slow down transmission is be well informed about the COVID-19 virus, the disease it causes and how it spreads. Protect yourself and others from infection by washing your hands or using an alcohol based rub frequently and not touching your face.

The COVID-19 virus spreads primarily through droplets of saliva or discharge from the nose when an infected person coughs or sneezes, so it’s important that you also practice respiratory etiquette (for example, by coughing into a flexed elbow).

At this time, there are no specific vaccines or treatments for COVID-19. However, there are many ongoing clinical trials evaluating potential treatments. WHO will continue to provide updated information as soon as clinical findings become available.

20. Buatin report teks tentang corona dalam bahasa inggris beserta artinya


Jawaban:

December 2019, the world was shocked with an outbreak of a respiratory virus in the city of Wuhan, the capital city of Hubei province of the republic of China, recently known as the novel coronavirus or COVID-19. This virus belongs to a group of coronavirus that can infect human. The virus spread so rapidly that it has become a worldwide pandemic within only two months.

Coronavirus is a group of related viruses that cause diseases in mammals and bird. in humans, the corona virus causes mild respiratory infections, such as some flu cases, and others that can be deadly such as SARS, MERS, and COVID -19.

Symptoms in other species vary: in chickens, they cause upper respiratory tract disease, whereas in cows and pigs they cause diarrhea. There is no vaccine or antiviral drug to prevent or treat human coronavirus infections.

Coronaviruses are enveloped viruses with a single-stranded RNA genome and symmetrical helical nucleocapsids. The coronavirus genome number ranges from 27-34 kilos of base pair, the largest among known RNA viruses. The name coronavirus comes from the Latin corona which means crown, which refers to the appearance of virus particles (virions): they have edges that are reminiscent of the crown or corona of the sun.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth, and they’re one of the most unique-looking animals, too. With their characteristic long noses, or trunks; large, floppy ears; and wide, thick legs, there is no other animal with a similar physique.

Most experts recognize two species of elephant: the Asian elephant (Elephas maximus) and the African elephant (Loxodonta africana), who live on separate continents and have many unique features. The Sword

A sword is a weapon that has been used by warriors and soldiers for centuries. It consists of a long blade made of metal that is sharpened to a keen edge and has a handle for gripping.

The first swords were made of bronze and were used by the ancient civilizations of Mesopotamia, Egypt, and Greece. Later on, iron became the preferred material for making swords, as it was stronger and more durable.

Swords come in many different shapes and sizes, and are designed for various purposes. Some are meant for cutting, while others are better suited for thrusting. There are also swords that are designed to be used on horseback, as well as swords that are used for ceremonial purposes.

In medieval Europe, knights carried swords as a symbol of their status and as a means of self-defense. The samurai of Japan also carried swords, which were considered a symbol of their honor and loyalty.

Today, swords are mostly used for ceremonial purposes, such as in weddings or other formal events. They are also used in martial arts and historical reenactments.

Overall, the sword has played a significant role in the history of warfare and has become an enduring symbol of strength and bravery.
