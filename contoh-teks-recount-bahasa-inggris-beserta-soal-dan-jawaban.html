Pembahasan

Faktual recount text adalah teks yang berisikan tentang laporan mengenai suatu peristiwa yang terjadi berdasarkan kan fakta

Generic structure recount text :

1. Orinetation, menjelaskan tentang pengenalan tokoh, tempat kejadian, kapan berlangsung dan sebagainya

2. Event, menjelaskan tentang isi teks yang berupa cerita mengenai kejadian atau pun suatu pengalaman yang akan di sampaikan oleh pembaca

3. Reorientation, berisikan tentang rangkuman atau rangkuman yang di tuliskan oleh penulis

Ciri-ciri recount text:

1. Menggunakan kalimat lampau atau past tense

2. Menggunakan part of speech adverb dan adverbial untuk menerangkan waktu

3. Menggunakan part of speech counjunction

I had my adolescence when I was thirteen.
It started with acne that showed up on my face. It was very annoying. It lowered my self-esteem and I was embarrassed to come out of my house and play with friends.

Fortunately, my Mum gave me a good medicine. In three weeks, the acnes started to vanish although those showed some black spots in my face.
That was my bad experience with adolescence, though there were still lots of good experience too.

17. Contoh teks descriptve, narative, recount dan report. dalam bahasa Inggris


Narrative Text

Generic structure:

1. Orientation

Yaitu berisi tentang pengenalan tokoh, waktu, serta tempat kejadiannya.

2. Complication

Yaitu gambaran masalah yang di alami oleh tokoh pada cerita yang harus dipecahkan.

3. Resolution

Yaitu bagaimana tokoh tsb menyelesaikan masalah.

Descriptive

Generic structure:

1. Identification : berisi tentang identifikasi hal / seorang yang akan dideskripsikan.

2. Description : berisi tentang penjelasan / penggambaran tentang hal / seseorang dengan menyebutkan beberapa sifatnya.

Recount Text

My Busy Day

Last Monday was a busy day for me. I spent my time to do a lot of activities from college to my home.

First, at the morning, I did my presentation’s assignment with my partner, she was Nurhidayah. It took 3 hours. And then we went to the campus for joining lecture. But, the lecturer said that our presentation would be started next week. It made us disappointed. The class was finished at 12.30.

After that, I had to go home because my grandmother was in a bad condition. She was hospitalized. So, it was a must for me to back home at that time. When I got there, there were so many members of my family. There were about 10 people. My aunt, my niece, my uncle and some of my cousins. We all hoped that our grandmother would get better soon.

Those activities made my day busy.

Generic structure:

1. Orientation: Menceritakan siapa saja yang terlibat dalam cerita, apa yang terjadi, dimana tempat peristiwa terjadi, dan kapan terjadi peristiwanya.

2. Events: Menceritakan apa yang terjadi (lagi) dan menceritakan urutan ceritanya.

3. Reorientation: Berisi penutup cerita/akhir cerita.

35. buat teks recount idola kamu dalam bahasa inggris?


Kurt Cobain was the lead singer and the guitarist of an American grunge band called Nirvana. He was born in Aberdeen, Washington, on 20 February 1967.
When Cobain was eight years old, his parents divorced. This divorce gave bad impact on his life. At his fourteenth birthday, his uncle gave him a guitar as the gift. Since that, he began work on his own songs. He and his friend, Krist Novoselic usually practiced music in the upstairs room of a salon own by Novoselic`s mother.

In 1986, they formed a grunge band named Nirvana. Cobain was the vocalist as well as guitarist and Novoselic played bass. They released their first album titled Bleach in 1989. In 1991 they released the second album, Never mind. It was their greatest album which made them a kind of popular superstar. This album included popular songs like Smell Like Teen Spirit, About A Girl, Come as You Are, In Bloom and Lithium.

However the popularity was intimidating to Cobain. He began addicted to drugs and became worse, even he overdosed on heroin. On March 30, he went to a rehabilitation but ran away. He was reported missing for a few days. Then on 8 April 1994, he was found dead in his house in Lake Washington. He had shot himself.

38. contoh teks recount Texk dalam bahasa Inggris​


I spent my last summer holiday in Seoul, South Korea. I went there with my friends

Terjemahan:

Aku pergi ke Seoul, Korea Selatan untuk menghabiskan liburan musim panasku. Aku pergi ke sana bersama teman-temanku.

On the first day, I was landed at Incheon Airport around 7 AM after a 6 hours long flight. Then I went to Seoul by train and checked in to the hotel I already booked. I decided to take a rest for a while. At night, I went to Hongdae, a famous district in South Korea. I went to eat traditional Korean food. On the next day, I went to the Gyeongbokgung Palace and National Museum. I also went to learn how to make Kimchi and see the scenery of Seoul from Seoul Tower. I went back to the hotel at 10 PM and immediately went to sleep.

Terjemahan:

Hari pertama, aku sampai di bandara Incheon sekitar jam 7 pagi setelah 6 jam penerbangan. Lalu aku ke Seoul dengan kereta dan check in di hotel yang telah aku pesan. Aku memutuskan untuk beristirahat sebentar. Malamnya, aku pergi ke Hongdae, daerah terkenal di Korea Selatan. Aku makan makanan khas Korea. Keesokan harinya, aku mengunjungi istana Gyeongbokgung dan Museum Nasional. Aku juga belajar cara membuat Kimchi dan menikmati pemandangan Seoul dari Seoul Tower. Aku kembali ke hotel jam 10 malam dan langsung tidur.

I spent my last day in Myeongdong and bought some stuffs and souvenirs for my family and friends. I also ate the street food there. I went to the airport at 3 PM because my flight was at 5 PM. I had a fantastic experience in South Korea and made wonderful memory with my friends. It was a great trip.

Terjemahan:

Hari terakhir aku habiskan di Myeongdong dan membeli beberapa barang dan oleh-oleh untuk keluarga dan teman-temanku. Aku juga makan jajanan di sana. Aku berangkat ke bandara pukul 3 sore karena penerbanganku pukul 5 sore. Aku mendapat pengalaman yang menyenangkan di Korea Selatan dan kenangan indah bersama teman-temanku. Pergi ke Korea Selatan merupakan pengalaman yang sangat menyenangkan.

Semoga terbantu

39. Buatlah teks recount tentang pengalaman pergi ke tempat caffe ! ​(buatlah teks tersebut dalam bentuk bahasa Inggris dan sertakan artinya)​


Jawaban:

Last weekend, I went to a cafe with my friends. It was a new cafe in town and we were all excited to try it out. When we arrived, we were greeted by a cozy and stylish atmosphere with a lot of greenery decorations. We ordered our drinks and some snacks and then found a table near the window.

As we were enjoying our drinks, we chatted and caught up with each other. The background music was pleasant and not too loud, allowing us to have a comfortable conversation. The cafe had a good selection of food and drinks, and the staff were friendly and attentive.

After a while, we decided to order another round of drinks and some desserts. We tried the cafe's specialty coffee and it was delicious. The dessert we ordered was also very tasty and beautifully presented.

Overall, it was a great experience. The cafe had a cozy atmosphere, good food and drinks, and friendly staff. It was the perfect place to catch up with friends and spend a relaxing afternoon. We definitely plan to go back again soon.

Artinya:

Akhir pekan lalu, saya pergi ke sebuah kafe dengan teman-teman saya. Kafe itu adalah kafe baru di kota dan kami semua sangat ingin mencobanya. Ketika kami tiba, kami disambut oleh suasana yang nyaman dan bergaya dengan banyak dekorasi tanaman hijau. Kami memesan minuman dan beberapa camilan kemudian mencari meja dekat jendela.

Saat kami menikmati minuman kami, kami mengobrol dan saling update satu sama lain. Musik latar belakangnya menyenangkan dan tidak terlalu keras, memungkinkan kami untuk memiliki percakapan yang nyaman. Kafe itu memiliki seleksi makanan dan minuman yang baik, dan stafnya ramah dan perhatian.

Setelah beberapa saat, kami memutuskan untuk memesan putaran minuman lagi dan beberapa dessert. Kami mencoba kopi spesial kafe dan itu enak. Dessert yang kami pesan juga sangat lezat dan disajikan dengan indah.

Secara keseluruhan, itu adalah pengalaman yang menyenangkan. Kafe itu memiliki suasana yang nyaman, makanan dan minuman yang baik, dan staf yang ramah. Itu adalah tempat yang sempurna untuk bertemu dengan teman-teman dan menghabiskan waktu santai. Kami pasti berencana untuk kembali lagi segera.

One day, when I was seven years old, my mommy bought a new bike for my birthday. I asked my momto teach me to ride. First, she refused my request and promised that he would teach me soon, exactly next week, when holidays is coming.
And the next week holiday are come. My mommy began to teach me to ride a bike around the field in my village. My mom was very patient gave me some direction. I was thrilled when I realized my ability to ride a bike. "Yes I can".
Someday, when I was alone at home, I intend to try my first riding ability. So, I tried to dare my self. All went well at first, but when I'm going back to my house and I had to pass through the narrow streets and slippery, I was nervous. I lost my control and I fell into a ditch.
Afterwards, I told my parents about the accident. I imagine mommy would get angry and never let me go up again. But the reality is quite the opposite, my mom is very proud of me. She just gave me some advice and because of the accident, I got permission to ride my own bike.
