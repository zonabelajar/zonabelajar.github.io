5. Tolong bantuin buat 5 soal PG beserta jawaban dan pembahasannya dari instruction text How to use an electrik iron Tugas dikumpulin besok


1. What is the text above?
Answer: Procedure text
2. How many step are there?
Answer: seven step
3. What is the first step?
Answer: Prepare the iron, the clothes, a place mat or any plat surface board, some clothing hanger.
4. After we apply the iron on the clothes surface evenly, what must we do?
Answer: Flip the clothes and apply the iron on the other side of the clothes evenly.
5. Before we place the clothes on the place mat, what must we do?
Answer: plug the iron cable into an electricity socket and wait until the iron get warmer.
