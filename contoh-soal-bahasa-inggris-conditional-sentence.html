3. contoh 10 soal dan jawaban tentang conditional sentence type 3 (dalam bahasa inggris)


1. They would have been there on time if they had caught the earlier bus.
fact: they didn't catch the earlier bus so they were not there on time
2. If Indra hadn't missed the train he would have attended the meeting.
fact: Indra missed the train so he didn't attended the meeting
3. Tari would have arrived late if she hadn't driven her car fast.
fact: Tari drave her car fast so she didn't arrived late
4. If ardy had worked hard he wouldn't have been fired.
fact: Ardy didn't work hard so he was fired
5. If Rasti hadn't played tennis she wouldn't have broken her leg.
fact: Rasti played tennis so she broke her leg

20. Contoh soal kalimat conditional sentence type 2


If my father were rich, I would live in a big villa with swimming polo inside.

(Jika ayah saya kaya, saya akan tinggal di sebuah vila besar dengan kolam renang di dalamnya.)

Keterangan: Dalam kalimat pengandaian ini, if my father was rich merupakan dependent clause yang tidak akan memiliki makna jika berdiri sendiri, sedangkan i would live in a big villa with swimming polo inside merupakan independent clause yang tetap memiliki makna meskipun tidak diikuti dengan kalimat di depan.

23. contoh soal contoh soal conditional sentence dan jawabannya


1. Type 0 : if you ..... (heat) ice, it .... (melt)
Answer :if you heat ice, it melts

2. Type 1 : if you .... (stand) in the rain, you .... (get) wet
Answer : if you stand in the rain, you will get wet

3. Type 2 : if i ... (be) you, i .... (buy) that car
Answer : if i were you, i would buy that car

4. Type 3 : if she .... (pay) me earlier, this .... (not, happen) to her
Answer : if she had paid me earlier, this wouldn't have happenee to her
