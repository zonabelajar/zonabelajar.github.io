10. perbedaan memo dan pesan singkat bahasa inggris


1.A letter is a short or long message that is sent by one person to another while a memo is a short message that is sent by a person to another.
2.A letter is more formal and contains more information while a memo is informal and is very short.
3.A memo is more concise and to the point as compared to a letter.
4.A letter is exchanged between businesses and their clients while a memo is exchanged between individuals within an organization.
5.A memo usually has a header that states where it is from and who it is intended for while a letter may or may not have this feature.

26. bagian bagian memo dalam bahasa inggris


bagian bagian memo sama saja seperti surat biasa,surat kepala,badan dan bagian kaki memo.
"semoga membantu"

Memo adalah singkatan dari memorandum yaitu sebuah pesan yang berisi pesan singkat yang bertujuan untuk menyampaikan informasi penting. Biasanya pesan berupa arahan, perintah, atau pengumuman. Informasi yang disampaikan haruslah dalam bentuk yang singkat, padat dan bisa dimengerti oleh penerima.

PembahasanMEMO

A. Pengertian Memo

Memo merupakan singkatan dari memorandum yang berfungsi menyampaikan pesan singkat, jelas, dan mudah dipahami yang memiliki tujuan penting. Memurut dari sistem pemakaiannya memo dibedakan menjadi dua jenis, yaitu:

Memo yang bersifat resmi : Memo yang bersifat resmi ini biasanya digunakkan olah pimpinan sebuah instansi atau perusahaan kepada bawahannya atau sebaliknya. Memo yang bersifat tidak resmi (pribadi) : Memo yang bersifat tidak resmi ini biasanya digunakan sebagai nota atau surat tidak resmi antar teman atau kerabat.  Memo jenis ini biasanya ditulis antar teman dengan tujuan menyampaikan pesan yang bersifat hubungan pribadi antar teman atau antar personal yang mempunyai hubungan dekat.

Didalam penggunaannya memo ini memiliki fungsi yaitu sebagai alat komunikasi yang bersifat arahan, saran, atau keterangan mengenai suatu hal agar pesan sampai kepada yang dituju secara cepat.

B. Ciri-ciri Memo Bahasa Inggris Yang Bersifat Resmi

Surat Khusus yang dibuat untuk keperluan khusus didalam sebuah Instansi atau Perusahaan. Merupakan sebuah bentuk komunikasi berupa arahan, saran, penjelasan mengenai suatu hal. Mempunyai bagian bentuk surat yang lebih singkat dibandingkan dengan surat resmi lainnya. Disebabkan oleh penggunannya yang terbatas memo selalu mencantumkan nama instansi atau perusahaan diatas kepala memo. Dilihat dari penggunannya dalam sebuah instansi atau perusahaan memo resmi ini dapat  disampaikan dalam bentuk vertikal dan horizontal : 1. Secara Vertikal : merupakan bentuk penyampaian dari atasan kebawahan atau sebaliknya yang bertujuan untuk mengingatkan atau memrintahkan.  2. Secara Horizontal : merupakan bentuk penyampaian memo kepada pihak yang memiliki jabatan yang sama.

C. Struktur Memo Dalam Bahasa Inggris

Sebuah memo umumnya memiliki 3 bagian generic structure, yaitu sebagai berikut:

1. Pembukaan : Bagian yang berisi penerima memo, pengirim memo tanggal memo dan tujuan dari memo. Yaitu:

         To :

        Name

        Adress

        Receiver

        Sender

        Date

        Subject

2. Isi pesan (Content) : Berupa pesan singkat yang padat dan mudah dimengerti oleh penerima memo.  Biasanya berupa memberikan saran, perintah. atau laporan.

3. Penutup: Bagian yang berisi tanda tangan dan nama jelas orang yang memberikan memo.

32. memo bahasa inggris tentang legenda beserta aritnya


In a hill far from the village, in Kalimantan area there lived a poor widow and a daughter. The widow's daughter is very beautiful. But unfortunately, he has a very bad behavior. The girl was very lazy, never helped her mother do the chores. It only works every day. Besides the slacker, the girl was spoiled. All his requests must be obeyed. Every time he asked for something to his mother must be granted, regardless of the situation of his poor mother, every day have to drudge to find a bite of rice. One day the girl's daughter took her mother down to the village to shop. The location of the village market is very far away, so they have to walk quite tiring. The girl walks around in nice clothes and preening so that the path people who see her will admire her beauty. While her mother walked behind her carrying a basket with a very dirty clothes. Because they live in a remote place, nobody knows that the two women who are walking are mothers and children.
As they began to enter the village, the villagers looked at them. They were so fascinated by the girl's beauty, especially the unsatisfied young villagers looking at her face. But when he saw the man walking behind the girl, it really contrasts. It makes people wonder. Among the people who saw it, a young man approached and asked the girl, "Hi, pretty girl. Is that behind your mother? " However, what is the girl's answer? "No," he said arrogantly. "He is my assistant!" The two mothers and children then went on their way. Not that far, approach a young man again and ask the girl. "Hi sweety. Is it your mother who walked behind you? " "No, no," the girl replied, tilting her head back. "He is a slave!" That's how every girl meets someone along the street asking about her mother, always the answer. Her mother was treated as her maid or slave. At first hearing her daughter's rebellious reply when asked by the person, the mother can still restrain herself. But after repeatedly hearing the same and very painful answer, finally the poor mother could not help herself. The mother prayed. "My God, I can not resist this insult. The servant's son is so self-righteous to treat himself as such. Yes, god punish this ungodly child! Punish him ... "
On the power of God Almighty, slowly the body of the ungodly girl turned to stone. change starts from feet. When the change had reached half the body, the girl cried for forgiveness to her mother. "Oh, Mother..mim .. forgive me, forgive your child's iniquity for so long. Mother ... Mother ... forgive your son .. "The girl kept wailing and crying begging her mother. But it was too late. The whole body of the girl eventually turned to stone. Despite being a stone, one can see that both eyes still shed tears, as if in tears. Therefore, the stone that comes from the girl who gets his mother's curse is called "Crying Stone".
