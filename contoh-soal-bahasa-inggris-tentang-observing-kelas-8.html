10. Observing and asking questions hal 88 kelas 8


Mengidentifikasi hewan berdasarkan habitat, karakteristik, aktifitas dan makanan.


Dalam exercise ini, siswa disajikan deskripsi dari empat hewan yaitu Gajah (Elephant), Harimau (Tiger), Beruang (Bear) dan Kera (Monkey). Siswa diminta untuk mengamati setiap teks deskripsi tersebut. Di bawah teks deskripsi, terdapat tabel yang berisi nama hewan, habitat, aktifitas, karakterisitik dan makanan. Siswa diminta untuk mengisi tabel tersebut sesuai dengan data yang sudah disediakan pada teks deskripsi tiap-tiap hewan.


Pembahasan

Elephants:

Habitat: Asia, AfricaFood: Grass and PlantsActivity: Use their long trunks to put food and water in their mouth.Characteristic: are the heaviest land animal.

Tigers:

Habitat: Grassland and ForestFood: MeatActivity: HuntCharacteristic: The biggest of all cats

Bears:

Habitat: Northern parts of the worldFood: Some of them eat meat, some of them eat honeyActivity: In winter, some bears find a snug place to hibernate.Characteristic: Bears have thick fur coats to protect them from cold

Monkey:

Habitat: In the forestFood: Plants, Birds' egg, small animal, insectsActivity: They can hold things in their handsCharacteristic: Monkeys are clever mammals that can solve problems.

27. contoh observing and questioning


“Contoh Percakapan” is a Bahasa Indonesia term which literally translates to “example conversation”. Contoh is example while Percakapan is conversation. In the call center industry, “Contoh Percakapan” essentially refers to an example dialogue between an agent and a customer. Many agents especially newbies look for a Contoh Percakapan so that they would have an idea on how an actual customer service is rendered. A Contoh Percakpan is also used by companies in order to evaluate their service. In another sense, Contoh Percakapan is a term used to refer to an online “forum”. And similarly, it is used by users to connect with other users who share the same interest on the subject of the forum.  Multi-lingual call center agents are in demand due to the influx of customer campaigns from countries such as China, Indonesia, Thailand, etc.  If you know Bahasa Indonesia language, you should be familiar with Contoh Percakapan.
