5. Ass. Maaf smuanya saya tolong di bantu dong contoh teks monolog bahasa inggris


Rafflesia Arnoldii 
Rafflesia Arnoldii is a rare flower that is well known because of the size of the flower's petals and the smell of rotten meat that it gives out. 
This enormous and rare flower grows in the forest of Southeast Asia, including the Phillipines. Its large flower can grow to 3 meters in diameter and weigh up to 11 kilograms. It does not have any leaves, stem, or roots and because of lacking clorophyls this plant can not photosynthesize making it a parasite, living on other plants nutrition. The flower has five petals red in colour and has white spots, which surrounds the middle of the flower much like a hole. At the base of the flower there is a part which has needles and contains the reproduction parts of the flower. 
Rafflesia Arnoldii reproduces with the help of flies that are attracted to the flowers smell (which some observers say smells much like bad meat and rotten eggs mixed). The flower only lasts for five to seven days and then dies.

28. contoh monolog bahasa Inggris yang berhubungan dengan time​


Monolog: The Importance of Time Management

Time is a valuable resource that we all have in common. It is something that we cannot control, but we can certainly manage it. Time management is crucial in our daily lives, whether it's in school, work, or personal endeavors. In this monologue, I will share with you the importance of time management and how it can positively impact our lives.

Time is limited, and we must make the most of it. Imagine a scenario where you have an important project to complete. You have a deadline looming, and yet you find yourself procrastinating, wasting precious time on trivial activities. This lack of time management can lead to stress, missed opportunities, and compromised quality of work.

On the other hand, effective time management allows us to be more productive and achieve our goals. By setting priorities and creating a schedule, we can allocate time for important tasks, ensuring that they are completed in a timely manner. This not only reduces stress but also provides a sense of accomplishment and boosts our confidence.

Time management also helps us maintain a healthy work-life balance. By organizing our time efficiently, we can dedicate quality time to our family, friends, and personal hobbies. We can pursue our passions, engage in self-care activities, and foster meaningful relationships. It is important to remember that time spent with loved ones and doing things we enjoy is equally valuable as time spent on work or responsibilities.

Furthermore, time management allows us to seize opportunities. When we manage our time well, we create space for new experiences, personal growth, and learning. We can explore new interests, attend workshops or courses, and expand our knowledge and skills. Time management empowers us to make the most of every moment and make progress towards our aspirations.

In conclusion, time management is essential for a fulfilling and successful life. By managing our time effectively, we can reduce stress, increase productivity, maintain a healthy work-life balance, and seize opportunities. Let us embrace the value of time and make a commitment to manage it wisely. Remember, time is a precious gift, and it is up to us to make the most of it.

31. contoh monolog bahasa Inggris tentang pekerjaan


Doctor is a popular job. It takes a great responsibility to work as a doctor. Minor mistakes made by a doctor can bring the lost of a patient.

There are a lot of jobs that doctors must do. They are expected to evaluate symptoms and run a test to determine what is wrong with a patient. If a. doctor does not know off handedly what the diagnosis should be, it is his or her responsibility to do the necessary research.

Notes are kept on each patient to ensure that any medical professional can walk into a room and know what has been done in the past. It is also a way to track the patients' progress during recovery.

Writing prescriptions is a major part of doctors' jobs. They must know how much medicine to prescribe based on the patient's history; they also need to know how two drugs can interact with one another.

A doctor needs to know not only how to treat the current conditions, but also how to advise the patient on how to prevent another occurrence.

(Recount)
Visiting Bali
There were so many places to see in Bali that my friend decided to join the tours to see as much as possible. My friend stayed in Kuta on arrival. He spent the first three days swimming and surfing on Kuta beach. He visited some tour agents and selected two tours. The first one was to Singaraja, the second was to Ubud. On the day of the tour, he was ready.My friend and his group drove on through mountains. Singaraja is a city of about 90 thousands people. It is a busy but quiet town. The street are lined with trees and there are many old Dutch houses. Then they returned very late in the evening to Kuta.The second tour to Ubud was a very different tour. It was not to see the scenery but to see the art and the craft of the island. The first stop was at Batubulan, a center of stone sculpture. There my friend watched young boys were carving away at big blocks of stone. The next stop was Celuk, a cente for silversmiths and goldensmiths. After that he stopped a little while for lunch at Sukawati and on to mass. Mass is a tourist center My friend ten-day-stay ended very quickly beside his two tour, all his day was spent on the beach. He went sailing or surfboarding every day. He was quiet satisfied.
