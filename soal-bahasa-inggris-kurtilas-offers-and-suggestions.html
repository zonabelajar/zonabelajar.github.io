13. Tolong buatin offers and suggestions dari foto dibawah dong, kelas 11


"Offers" (penawaran) dan "suggestions" (saran) adalah dua konsep yang berbeda dalam konteks komunikasi dan bisnis. Berikut ini adalah jawaban dari foto soal tentang percakapan di atas:
Percakapan 1:

Concierge hotel: good evening, can i help you?

Customer: good evening, yes please. i would like a room for the night

Concierge hotel: Of course, we have several room options available. Are you looking for a single or double room?

Customer: A double room, please

Concierge hotel: You're welcome, Mr. Smith. Your room key will be ready at the front desk. Enjoy your stay, and if you need anything else, don't hesitate to ask. Good night!

Customer: thank, god night

Percakapan 2:

Dina: Let's watch a movie.

Rudi: What kind of movie?

Dina: A romantic movie.

Rudi: I don't like romantic movies. Let's watch something else.

Dina: Well, how about a comedy? There are some great comedies out right now.

Rudi: Comedy sounds good. Which one do you have in mind?

Dina: I heard "The Laugh Factory" is really funny. What do you think?

Rudi: "The Laugh Factory" sounds like a great choice. Let's go for it.

Dina: Perfect! I'll grab some popcorn, and we can start watching.

Percakapan 3:

A store attendant: Hi there, how can I help you?

A customer: I would like to return this shirt for a refund.

A store attendant: What is the problem with the shirt?

A customer: The shirt has a stain on it, and it's not the size I expected.

A store attendant: I apologize for the inconvenience. Do you have the receipt with you?

A customer: Yes, I do. Here's the receipt.

A store attendant: Thank you. We can process the refund for you. Would you prefer the refund to be credited back to your original payment method?

A customer: Yes, please. That would be great.

A store attendant: Alright, I'll start the refund process for you. It should take a few moments. Thank you for shopping with us, and I'm sorry for the inconvenience you've experienced.

A customer: Thank you for your help.

Pembahasan

Offers merujuk pada tindakan menawarkan sesuatu kepada orang lain. Ini bisa berupa penawaran produk atau layanan, atau bahkan penawaran bantuan atau kerjasama dalam konteks yang lebih umum. Contohnya, sebuah perusahaan bisa memberikan penawaran khusus pada produk mereka, seperti "Beli satu, dapatkan satu gratis," sebagai strategi pemasaran.

Suggestions merujuk pada memberikan ide atau rekomendasi kepada orang lain. Saran dapat berupa pandangan atau pendapat yang diberikan untuk membantu seseorang membuat keputusan atau memecahkan masalah. Misalnya, dalam percakapan sebelumnya, saran tersebut adalah ketika Dina menyarankan untuk menonton film komedi setelah Rudi mengatakan bahwa dia tidak suka film romantis.

27. perbedaan dari suggestions dan offers


Pembahasan:


Suggestion

Suggestion (noun) dalam bahasa Indonesia berarti saran. Suggestion adalah suatu ungkapan yang digunakan untuk mengekpresikan saran atau nasihat. contoh ungkapan yang dapat digunakan untuk mengungkapkan saran adalah sebagi berikut.

- You should ...

- You ought to ...

- It's better for you to ...

- I suggest you ...

- I advise you to ...

- Why don't we/you ... ?

- How about ...?

- You had better ...

- Let's ...


Contoh kalimat saran:

- You should sleep early (kamu sebaiknya tidur lebih awal)

- How about making a cake? (bagaimana kalau membuat kue?)

- Let's go to the beach (mari kita pergi ke pantai)


Offer

Dalam bahasa inggris, Offer dapat digunakan sebagai verb yang berarti menawarkan atau sebagai noun yang berarti tawaran. Offer merupakan suatu ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menawarkan suatu bantuan atau barang. berikut contoh kalimat yang digunakan untuk mengekspresikan offer:

a. Untuk menawarkan bantuan kita dapat menggunakan ungkapan berikut:

- May I help you?

- Can I help you?

- What can I do for you?

- Would you like me to ...?

- Can I lend you a hand with that?


b. Untuk menawarkan barang kita dapat menggunakan ungkapan berikut:

- Would you like some ...?

- Would you like something to ...?

- Should I get you ...?

- What can I get for you?


Contoh kalimat menawarkan:

- Would you like some tea? (apakah kamu mau teh?)

- Would you like me to get you a drink? (apakah kamu ingin saya membantumu?)

- Should I get you a bandage? (haruskah saya mengambilkan perban untukmu?)


Dari penjelasan diatas, kita dapat menarik kesimpulan bahwa penggunaan suggestion dan offer cukup berbeda. Suggestion digunakan untuk memberikan saran kepada orang lain sedangkan offer digunakan untuk menawarkan bantuan atau barang kepada orang lain. Ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menyatakan suggestion dan offer pun berbeda.

29. Tolong buatkan percakapan suggestions and offers dengan tema di bank


Answer:

In saying suggestion and offer, you can express it in several different way. Here they are:

- why don't / shall we....?

- let's ......

- how/ what about + Ving?

Offer is used when you want to help someone else. Here are how you say it.

- shall I .....?

- would you like me to....?

Here's the dialogue:

Customer Service: Good morning, Sir, how may I be your assistance?

Customer: Yes, I can't withdraw my money at the ATM, it is said that I have to change my card.

CS: Well, let me check your card, Sir?

C: Here it is.

CS: Ah, yes, it's due to the bank policy, for the old ATM issued before 2010 has to be exchanged. Shall I process it now?

C: Please.

CS: OK, how about filling out this form first and signing it, Sir?

C: Sure. Anything else?

CS: That would be all, Sir. Shall we help you make the mobile banking, Sir?

C: Sorry, I don't think it's necessary. I'm fine with the regular one.

CS: OK, then, Sir, now, all you need to do is to make the new password and it's done.

C: OK.
