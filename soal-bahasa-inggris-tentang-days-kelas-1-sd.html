19. buatlah dialog bahasa Inggris 4 org, beserta pro dan kontra, judulnya full days school tolong bantu besok harus dikumpulkan ​


Jawaban:

Title: Full Days School

Characters:

John, a student

Maria, a student

Mr. Smith, a teacher

Mrs. Johnson, a parent

John: Hi, everyone. What do you think about the school planning to implement full days?

Maria: I'm all for it. We'll have more time to study and learn new things.

Mr. Smith: I agree with Maria. Full days would allow us to cover more material and provide more opportunities for students to participate in extracurricular activities.

Mrs. Johnson: I'm not sure. My daughter already comes home exhausted after a half-day of school. I'm worried that full days would be too tiring for her.

John: But wouldn't the longer school day help students prepare for the longer workday they'll face in the future?

Maria: And we could have more time to work on group projects and class discussions.

Mr. Smith: Plus, full days would mean more time for science labs and hands-on learning experiences.

Mrs. Johnson: But what about time for homework? My daughter already has a lot to do after school.

John: With full days, we could have designated homework time during the day. That way, students won't have to spend as much time doing homework at home.

Maria: And it would be easier for students to ask their teachers for help or clarification if they're struggling with a topic.

Mr. Smith: Exactly. And with more instructional time, we could cover more material, which would ultimately help students be better prepared for college and the workforce.

Mrs. Johnson: I can see your point. I think I need to think about this a bit more before making a decision.

John: That's fair. It's important to consider all sides before making a decision.

30. Ubahlah kalimat ini menjadi kalimat negatif (+) in will swim tomorrow in the swimming (-)mengunakan bahasa Inggris (+) my father will go to makasar for two days ( - )mengunakan bahasa Inggris


Jawaban:

(+) in will swim tomorrow in the swimming pool.

(-) in will not swim tomorrow in the swimming pool.

(+) My Father will go to Makassar for two days.

(-) My Father will not go to Makassar for two days.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Simple Future Tense karena menjelaskan aktivitas kegiatan di masa datang.

Simple Future Tense:

(+) Subject + Will + Verb-1 + Object

(-) Subject + Will + not + Verb-1 + Object
