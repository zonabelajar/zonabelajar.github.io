Tenses

1. Simple Present Tense

Verb :

(+) S + V1 (s/es)

(-) S + Do not/ Does not + V1

(?) Do/Does + S + V1 + ?

Non Verb :

(+) S + Am/Is/Are + Adjective/Noun

(-) S + Am not/Is not/Are not + Adjective/Noun

(?) Am/Is/Are + S + Adjective/Noun + ?

2. Present Continuous Tense

Verb :

(+) S + Am/Is/Are + Ving

(-) S + Am not/Is not/Are not + Ving

(?) Am/Is/Are + S + Ving + ?

We can’t use to be in continuous tenses

3. Present Perfect Tense

Verb :

(+) S + Have/Has + V3(ed)

(-) S + Have not/Has not + V3(ed)

(?) Have/Has + S + V3(ed) + ?

4. Present Perfect Continuous Tense

Verb :

(+) S + Have/Has + Been + Ving

(-) S + Have not/Has not + Been + Ving

(?) Have/Has + S + Been + Ving + ?

We can’t use to be in continuous tenses

5. Simple Past Tense

Verb :

(+) S + V2(ed)

(-) S + Did not + V1

(?) Did + S + V1 + ?

Non Verb :

(+) S + Was/Were + Adjective/Noun

(-) S + Was not/Were not+ Adjective/Noun

(?) Was/Were + S + Adjective/Noun + ?

6. Past Continuous Tense

Verb :

(+) S + Was/Were + Ving

(-)  S + Was not/Were not + Ving

(?) Was/Were + S Ving + ?

We can’t use to be in continuous tenses

7. Past Perfect Tense

Verb :

(+) S + Had + V3 (ed)

(-) S + Had not + V3 (ed)

(?) Had + S + V3 (ed) + ?

8. Past Perfect Continuous Tense

Verb :

(+) S + Had + Been + Ving

(-) S + Had not + Been + Ving

(?) Had + S + Been + Ving + ?

We can’t use to be in continuous tenses

9. Simple Future Tense

Verb :

(+) S + Will + V1

(-) S + Will not + V1

(?) Will + S + V1 + ?

Non Verb :

(+) S + Will + Be + Adjective/Noun

(-) S + Will not + Be + Adjective/Noun

(?) Will + S + Be + Adjective/Noun + ?

10. Future Continuous Tense

Verb :

(+) S + Will + Be + Ving

(-) S + Will not + Be + Ving

(?) Will + S + Be + Ving + ?

We can’t use to be in continuous tenses

11. Future Perfect Tense  

Verb :

(+) S + Will + Have + V3(ed)

(-) S + Will not + Have + V3(ed)

(?) Will + S + Have + V3(ed) + ?

12. Future Perfect Continuous Tense

Verb :

(+) S + Will + Have + Been + Ving

(-) S + Will not + Have + Been + Ving

(?) Will + Have + S + Been + Ving + ?

We can’t use to be in continuous tenses

13. Past Future Tense

Verb :

(+) S + Would + V1

(-) S + Would not + V1

(?) Would + S + V1 + ?

14. Past Future Continuous Tense

Verb :

(+) S + Should/Would + Be + Ving

(-) S + Should not/Would not + Be + Ving

(?) Should/Would + S + Be + Ving + ?

Verb :

(+) S + Should/Would + Have + V3

20. buatlah 8 contoh kalimat past continuous tense dan preset continuous tense​


Berikut adalah delapan contoh kalimat dalam bentuk Past Continuous Tense dan Present Continuous Tense:

Past Continuous Tense:
1. I was studying for my exam last night.
(Saya sedang belajar untuk ujian saya semalam.)

2. They were playing football in the park when it started raining.
(Mereka sedang bermain sepak bola di taman ketika hujan mulai turun.)

3. She was cooking dinner while he was watching TV.
(Dia sedang memasak makan malam sementara dia sedang menonton TV.)

4. We were walking along the beach when we saw a beautiful sunset.
(Kami sedang berjalan di sepanjang pantai ketika kami melihat matahari terbenam yang indah.)

Present Continuous Tense:
1. I am reading a book right now.
(Saya sedang membaca buku sekarang.)

2. They are having dinner at the restaurant.
(Mereka sedang makan malam di restoran.)

3. She is writing an email to her friend.
(Dia sedang menulis email kepada temannya.)

4. We are watching a movie tonight.
(Kami sedang menonton film malam ini.)

Perhatikan bahwa dalam contoh-contoh di atas, Past Continuous Tense menunjukkan aksi yang sedang berlangsung di masa lampau, sedangkan Present Continuous Tense menunjukkan aksi yang sedang berlangsung di saat ini.

24. kalimat past tense past continuous future tense modal (must) active dan passive menggunakan kata invite dalam bahasa Inggris ​


Kalimat dengan modal "must" dan "invite" dalam bahasa Inggris dengan grammar: past tense, past continuous, future tense..

1. Simple Present

(Active):

- They must invite their friends to the party.

- We must invite the clients for a business meeting.

(Passive):

- Their friends must be invited to the party.

- The clients must be invited for a business meeting.

2. Simple Past

(Active):

- She must have invited him to the wedding.

- The company must have invited them to the event.

(Passive):

- He must have been invited to the wedding by her.

- They must have been invited to the event by the company.

3. Future

(Active):

- You must invite everyone to the charity event tomorrow.

- We must invite our neighbors to the block party next week.

(Passive):

- Everyone must be invited to the charity event tomorrow.

- Our neighbors must be invited to the block party next week.

Berikut ini adalah contoh kalimat-kalimat yang menggunakan kata "invite" dalam berbagai bentuk tense dan voice dalam bahasa Inggris:

Present Tense (Simple Present):

- Active: She invites her friends to the party.

- Passive: The guests are invited to the party.

Past Tense (Simple Past):

- Active: He invited me to his wedding last year.

- Passive: I was invited to his wedding last year.

Present Continuous:

- Active: They are inviting guests for the charity event.

- Passive: The guests are being invited for the charity event.

Past Continuous:

- Active: We were inviting people to join our club when it started raining.

- Passive: People were being invited to join our club when it started raining.

Present Perfect:

- Active: The company has invited a renowned speaker for the conference.

- Passive: A renowned speaker has been invited for the conference.

Past Perfect:

- Active: She had invited all her relatives to the family reunion before she left.

- Passive: All her relatives had been invited to the family reunion before she left.

Future Tense (be going to):

- Active: We are going to invite our neighbors to the barbecue party next weekend.

- Passive: Our neighbors are going to be invited to the barbecue party next weekend.

Modal Verb 1 (can):

- Active: Can you invite Sarah to the meeting tomorrow?

- Passive: Can Sarah be invited to the meeting tomorrow?

Modal Verb 2 (should):

- Active: You should invite your parents to the graduation ceremony.

- Passive: Your parents should be invited to the graduation ceremony.

39. jelaskan dengan jelas tentang past continuous tense beserta contohnya(semua dalam bahasa inggris)​


Past continuous tense or also commonly known as past progressive tense is a tense that is used to express activities that are happening at a certain time in the past.
The action / activity has been and is ongoing but has not been completed at that time and sometimes also interspersed with an action / activity that occurs briefly.

as example ;
1.my mother 'was' cooking fried rice while I 'was' repairing my laptop.

2.'were' yo u working all night yesterday?

3.rina 'wasn't' doing her home work when Santi called her.

4.we 'weren't' playing outside when the teacher 'came'.
