8. Type l : if he us a bird,he may fly awayType ll :The real fact :Type lll :The real fact :bantu jawabnya dong:")​


CONDITIONAL SENTENCE

Type I : If he is a bird, he may fly away.

Type II : If he were a bird, he might fly away.

The real fact : He is not a bird, so he is impossible to fly.

Type III : If he had been a bird, he might have flown away.

The real fact : He wasn't a bird, so he was impossible to fly.

EXPLANATION

Conditional type 1 : keinginan yang kemungkinan terjadi saat ini ataupun nanti-nanti (real possibility) berdasarkan prediksi.

Rumus

If+Simple present, Simple future

If+S+verb1(s/es)..., S+will/would+verb1......

Exp.

If she finishes her study, she will go to the market

Fact ➡ She probably goes to the market.

Conditional type 2 : keinginan yang diragukan terjadi saat ini atau mendatang.

Rumus :

If+Simple past, Past future

If+S+verb2 ......, S+would/might+verb1......

Exp.

If I invited her to the party, she would be happy.

Fact ➡ She is sad because I don't invite her to the party.

Conditional type 3 : keinginan yang tidak terwujud di waktu lampau. Pengulasannya saat ini hanyalah sebagai dampak dari kejadian di waktu lampau.

Rumus :

If+Past perfect, Past future perfect

If+S+had+verb3...., S+would/might+have+verb3....

Exp.

If he had told the truth, his mother wouldn't have been angry.

Fact ➡ he didn't tell the truth, so his mother was angry.

Penjelasan:

Klausa dalam tata bahasa adalah sekumpulan kata yang terdiri dari subjek dan predikat, walau dalam beberapa bahasa dan beberapa jenis klausa subjek dari klausa mungkin tidak tampak secara eksplisit. Hal ini umum dalam bahasa bersubjek nol. Sebuah kalimat paling sederhana terdiri dari satu klausa, sedangkan kalimat yang lebih rumit terdiri dari beberapa klausa.

Klausa sering kali dikontraskan dengan frasa. Sebuah kumpulan kata dikatakan sebagai klausa apabila ia mempunyai predikat dan sebuah subjek, sementara sebuah frasa berisi kata kerja tanpa subjek atau berisi subjek tanpa predikat. Sebagai contoh, kalimat “Aku tidak tahu kalau kamu membuat lukisan itu,” adalah klausa dan sebuah kalimat penuh, sedangkan lukisan itu dan membuat lukisan itu adalah frasa. Ahli bahasa masa kini tidak membuat perbedaan seperti itu; mereka menerima ide akan klausa nonfinitif, klausa yang diatur di sekitar kata kerja infinitif.

A clause is a group of words that contains both a subject and a predicate but cannot always be considered as a full grammatical sentence.

Clause Definition: A clause consists of a subject and a verb and is the smallest grammatical unit that expresses a thought.

What is a Clause in a Sentence?

What is a clause? In its simplest form, a clause in grammar is a subject plus a verb. The subject is the entity “doing” the action of the sentence and the verb is the action that subject completes. A clause creates a complete thought (an idea or a statement that can stand alone).

A complete thought is also called a main clause or independent clause (IC).

Examples of clauses:

Subject + verb. = complete thought (IC)

I eat. = complete thought (IC)

Sharon speaks. = complete thought (IC)

A clause may include the verb predicate as well. But, it must include at least the subject and verb to be considered a clause.

21. "If I hold down the fn + f5 key, it will reduce the brightness of the laptop screen."1. Conditional Sentence Type 0Answer : 2. Conditional Sentence Type 1Answer : 3. Conditional Sentence Type 2Answer : Fact : 4. Conditional Sentence Type 3Answer : Fact : ​


CONDITIONAL SENTENCE

1. If I hold down the fn + f5 key, it reduces the brightness of the laptop screen. (C.S. type O)

2. If I hold down the fn + f5 key, it will reduce the brightness of the laptop screen. (C.S. type I)

3. If I held down the fn + f5 key, it would reduce the brightness of the laptop screen. (C.S. type II)

Fact : I don't hold down the fn + f5 key, so it doesn't reduce the brightness of the laptop screen.

4. If I had held down the fn + f5 key, it would have reduced the brightness of the laptop screen. (C.S. type III)

Fact : I didn't hold down the fn + f5 key, so it didn't reduce the brightness of the laptop screen.

EXPLANATION

Conditional sentence adalah kalimat andai², di mana suatu keinginan dapat terwujud apabila persyaratannya terpenuhi.

A. Conditional sentence type 0

↪menjabarkan kebenaran umum, selalu benar terjadi [Fact].

Formula :

If+Simple present, .....simple present

If+S+verb¹(s/es)..., S+verb¹(s/es)

Exp.

If the water is heated at 100 degrees Celcius, it boils.

B. Conditional sentence type I

↪menjabarkan keinginan yang kemungkinan terjadi saat ini ataupun nanti-nanti (real possibility) berdasarkan prediksi.

Formula :

If+Simple present, ....Simple future

If+S+verb1(s/es)..., S+will+verb1......

Exp.

If I have enough money, I will buy the new book.

C. Conditional sentence type II

↪menjabarkan keinginan yang diragukan terjadi saat ini atau mendatang (hypothetical situation). Pakai auxiliary BE "were" utk semua bentuk subyek sbg bentuk harapan yg blm tentu terwujud (Subjunctive).

Formula :

If+Simple past, ....Past future

If+S+verb2 ......, S+would+verb1......

Exp.

If I were you, I would tell it to your mom.

D. Conditional sentence type III

↪menjabarkan keinginan yang tidak terwujud di waktu lampau (truly hypothetical). Pengulasannya saat ini hanyalah sebagai dampak dari kejadian di waktu lampau.

Formula :

If+Past perfect,..... Past future perfect

If+S+had+verb3...., S+would+have+verb3....

Exp.

If he had kept the promise, his girl friend wouldn't have been disappointed.

CONDITIONAL SENTENCE

[1]

Type 3 : If I had been fine, I would have gone for a work.

Fact : I wasn't fine, so I didn't go for a work.

[2]

Type 3 : If my mother had brought much money, she would have bought a new phone.

Fact : My mother didn't bring much money, so she didn't buy a new phone.

[3]

Type 3 : If John hadn't made a noise, the baby would have slept well.

Fact : John made a noise, so the baby didn't sleep well.

[4.]

Type 3 : If I had known your address, I would have taken you.

Fact : I didn't know your address, so I didn't take you.

[5.]

Type 3 : If it had rained hard, I would have stayed at home yesterday

Fact : It didn't rain hard, so I didn't stay at home.

[6.]

Type 3 : If we hadn't taken an umbrella, we would have got wet on the way.

Fact : We took an umbrella, so we didn't get wet on the way.

[7.]

Type 3 : If students had passed UN, the teacher would have been happy.

Fact : Students didn't pass UN, so the teacher was sad.

EXPLANATION

Conditional type 1 [First conditional] menjabarkan keinginan yang kemungkinan terjadi saat ini ataupun nanti-nanti (real possibility) berdasarkan prediksi.

Rumus

If+Simple present, Simple future

If+S+verb1(s/es)..., S+will+verb1......

Exp.

If she has enough money, she will buy a new car.

Fact ➡ She probably buys a new car someday

Conditional type 2 [Second conditional] menjabarkan keinginan yang diragukan terjadi saat ini atau mendatang.

Rumus :

If+Simple past, Past future

If+S+verb2 ......, S+would+verb1......

Exp.

If I didn't visit my neighbor's house, I would meet Mr. Jack.

Fact ➡ I visit my neighbor's house, so I don't meet Mr. Jack.

Conditional type 3 [The third conditional] menjabarkan keinginan yang tidak terwujud di waktu lampau. Pengulasannya saat ini hanyalah sebagai dampak dari kejadian di waktu lampau.

Rumus :

If+Past perfect, Past future perfect

If+S+had+verb3...., S+would+have+verb3....

Exp.

If he had told the truth, his boss wouldn't have been angry.

Fact ➡ he didn't tell the truth, so his boss was angry.

^_hai, brainly fellows!_^

Let me try to help you change into a right conditional type!

Jawaban:

Apabila diketahui:

Type 1:

If he is a bird, he may fly.

Maka dapat dijawab:

Type 2:

If he was a bird, he might fly.

In fact:

He may not fly because he wasn't a bird.

Type 3:

If he had been a bird, he might fly.

In fact:

He may never fly because he wasn't a bird.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat di atas semua berupa dalam bentuk conditional.

Kalimat kondisional terbagi dalam 3 bentuk:

Conditional Type 1

Merupakan kalimat dengan bentuk sesuatu apa yang terjadi pada saat ini apabila subyek berupa subyek kedua (obyek) benar-benar dapat terjadi atau apabila subyek mengharapkan sesuatu dapat terjadi/dimiliki olehnya maka sesuatu dijamin akan subyek rasakan. Selain itu, sesuatu yang diibaratkan dapat ada pada masa kini (present)

Penggunaan:

If + S + simple present + O + will/can/may + V1 (be) + ROS

ROS = rest of sentence

Contoh lainnya:

If I have much money, I will buy a car.She will not give you a gift if you are naughty.

Conditional Type 2

Merupakan kalimat yang mencirikan sesuatu yang tidak sesuai dengan kenyataan pada saat ini melainkan fakta yang diharapkan dapat benar-benar terjadi dengan yang dapat dihubungkan dengan dunia nyata dalam kondisi masa lampau (past) atau masa lampau yang bakal terjadi pada masa depan (past future)

Penggunaan:

If + S + simple past + O + would/could/might + V1 (be) + ROS

Contoh lainnya:

If I had much money, I would buy a new house in Jakarta.If you weren't good she wouldn't give you a gift.

Conditional Type 3

Merupakan kalimat dengan bentuk harapan (expectation) yang pada dasarnya sudah terjadi/terdapat sebelumnya. Dengan suatu harapan yang diinginkan benar-benar terjadi pada masa lampau namun tidak dapat dirasakan atau tidak ada keberadaannya karena tidak sesuai atau benar-benar tidak terjadi (hanya sebuah harapan) dengan kejadian masa lampau yang mutlak (past perfect)

Penggunaan:

if + S + past perfect + O + would/could/might + have (been) V3 + ROS

Contoh lainnya:

If I had graduated at the right time, I would have travelled Paris.You would have given she a letter if she had been sick.

Catatan:

ROS (rest of sentence) adalah sisa dari kalimat yang digunakan dapat berupa object dan complement
