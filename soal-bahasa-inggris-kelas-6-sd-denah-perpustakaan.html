C: Hi A! I'm C. Nice to meet you!
A: Oh hi! I'm A. Nice to meet you too!
D: Hey guys! What are you guys doing here?
B: Nothing...
D: How about if we go to the library?
C: Yeah.. That's a good idea.
(go to the library together)
E: Welcome to Yellow Library. Please keep silent during reading the books. If you have any questions, you may ask to that guy.
(seat in a group)
A: I'm gonna choose a book to read.
B: I just read together with you okay A?
A: Okay fine. Just wait here. How about you, C and D?
C: I'm too lazy to read the books here.
D: Yeah me too.. Me and C will gonna have a chit chat and you with B reading books?
B: That's fine.
(while A and B reading a book, C and D have a chip chat)
C: Do you often go to Library?
D: Yes. But now I'm not too often because the novels are decreased during the 2016 until now.
C: You finish reading a novel for 1 day?
D: No. I usually borrow it from library and continue reading at home.
C: How to borrow a book from Library?
D: It's so easy. You just need a library card member to borrow it. But don't forgot to return the book back to the Library?
C: How about if I forgot return back it to the Library?
D: Maximum day of borrowing a book is 1 week. So if you borrow book more than 1 week, you need to pay.
C: How about if I lost the book at my home?
D: You need to buy the new one.
C: Do you have any experience like that?
D: No. Because I usually finish reading novel only about 3 or 4 days.
C: I wanted to go to the toilet. I'll ask to that guy. You don't need to help me to show the way okay..
D: Okay.. Bye!
(C ask to the information guy)
C: Excuse me sir, do you know where is the toilet near here?
F: It's on the 2nd floor of the library. Besides library stock books.
C: Okay. Thanks sir.
F: Your welcome.
(C back to the library)
C: Hey guys it's already 4PM. I need to go home.
D: Yeah me too. Before my mom is gonna angry.
A: I've just finished reading the book with B, let's back to home B!
B: Ya! Okay bye guys!
