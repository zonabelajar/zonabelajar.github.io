1. Tolong buatkan text dialog daily activity dengan 3 soal pilihan ganda. Terimakasih


Daily activity adalah kegiatan atau aktivitas yang dilakukan setiap hari. Daily activity bisa juga diartikan sebagai kebiasaaan sehari-hari.

Dalam membuat sebuah daily activity, kita harus menggunakan simple present tense. Itu karena simple present tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan sebuah kebiasaan.

Simple present tense mempunyai formula:

S + V1 atau S + V1s/es


V1 untuk I, You, They, dan We

Vis/es untuk He, she dan it.


Pembahasan

Membuat percakapan tentang daily activity beserta 3 soal pilihan ganda:

Teacher: Hello everyone. Today, we are going to discuss about daily activity. First of all, I will ask you what do you do everyday.

Teacher: Rina, what do you do at 6 o'clock every morning?

Rina: I take a bath at 6 o'clock every morning mam.

Teacher: Good. Next, Anton, what do you do every afternoon?

Anton: I always take a nap every afternoon mam. School activity consumes my energy. I need to take a rest after that.

Teacher: Okay Anton, nice answer. Next, Marisa, What do you do before sleeping?

Marisa: I always make my bed, wash my hands and feet and have a pray before sleeping mam. I ask God to protect me during my sleeping.

Teacher: You do it well Marisa. Okay, please open your book on the page 25. We will start the lesson.


Questions:

1. What does usually Rina do every morning?

A. She goes sleeping

B. She takes a bath

C. She sweeps the floor

D. She plays badminton


2. Why Anton always take a nap every afternoon? Because.....

A. School activity takes his energy too much.

B. He plays football before that.

C. He feels sleepy.

D. He is full (Kenyang)


3. What does Marisa do before sleeping, except?

A. She has a pray

B. She makes a bed.

C. She watches Television

D. She washes her hands and feet



Demikian adalah contoh percakapan tentang daily activity beserta soalnya. Semoga membantu menjawab.
