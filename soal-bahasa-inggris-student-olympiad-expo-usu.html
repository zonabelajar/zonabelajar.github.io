3. contoh dialog bahasa inggris tentang education expo​


Jawaban:

Austin : "What are you going to do after graduate?" (Apa yang akan kamu lakukan setelah lulus?)

Paris : "I’m going to continue studying for a master’s degree. How about you?" (Aku akan melanjutkan kuliah S2. Bagaimana dengan kamu?)

Austin : "I’m going to find a job as soon as I graduate" (Aku akan mencari kerja segera setelah lulus)

Paris : "Good luck" (Semoga beruntung)

Austin : "Thanks. Anyway, why are you planning to continue studying instead of finding a job?" (Terima kasih. Ngomong-ngomong, mengapa kamu berencana untuk melanjutkan kuliah alih-alih mencari kerja?)

Paris : "Because education is very important aspect to me. It can help me to open more doors of opportunities" (Karena pendidikan adalah aspek terpenting bagiku. Pendidikan dapat membantuku membuka lebih banyak pintu kesempatan)

Austin: "What opportunities are they?" (Kesempatan macam apa itu?

Paris : "The opportunities to get better job to secure my future" (Kesempatan untuk mendapatkan pekerjaan yang lebih baik agar masa depanku terjamin)

Austin : "Are you sure about that? There are some people with master’s degree that are having difficulty to find a job because of their lack experience of work" (Kamu yakin tentang itu? Ada beberapa orang yang memiliki gelar S2 yang kesulitan mencari pekerjaan karena kurangnya pengalaman kerja)

Paris : "It’s very unfortunate, but as I said earlier, education opens more opportunities. There are many companies and institutions which will hire people with higher educational background even without work experience. All we need to do is that only to keep trying until we get what we want. Besides, there is nothing wrong to keep studying. I believe knowledge that we obtain from studying in college will really useful for us" (Memang sangat disayangkan, tapi seperti yang telah kusebutkan tadi, pendidikan membuka lebih banyak kesempatan. Banyak perusahaan dan institusi yang akan menerima orang-orang dengan latar pendidikan yang tinggi meskipun tanpa pengalaman kerja. Yang harus kita lakukan hanyalah terus mencoba sampai kita mendapatkan apa yang kita inginkan. Lagi pula, tidak ada salahnya untuk terus belajar. Aku percaya ilmu yang kita dapatkan dari kuliah akan berguna bagi kita)

Austin : "I get your points, and I agree that education is very important. But for me, if we can get a job after we graduate, why bother going back to school? I mean, in the end, after going back to school, we still have to get a job, don’t we?" (Aku mengerti maksudmu, dan aku setuju bahwa pendidikan itu sangat penting. Namun bagiku, jika kita bisa mendapatkan pekerjaan setelah lulus, kenapa repot-repot kembali kuliah lagi? Maksudku, pada akhirnya setelah kuliah lagi, kita harus mendapatkan pekerjaan juga, kan?)

Paris : "Yes, that is right, but every person has different opinion towards this matter, and I already stated mine" (Ya, itu memang benar, tetapi setiap orang memiliki pendapat yang berbeda mengenai hal ini, dan aku telah mengungkapkan pendapatku)

Austin : "Yeah, you’re right" (Ya, kamu benar)

6. Gaya bahasa ,cerita asal usu suku talang mamak


Jawaban:

Asal Usul

Menurut Obdeyn, Asisten Residen Indragiri, Masyarakat Talang Mamak berasal dari Pagaruyung yang terdesak akibat konflik adat dan agama. Berdasarkan hikayat yang berkembang pada masyarakat tersebut, bahwa nenek moyang mereka turun dari Gunung Marapi menuju ke Talukkuantan, menelusuri Batang Kuantan dipimpin oleh Datuk Patih bergelar Perpatih Nan Sebatang, kemudian membangun pemukiman pada sehiliran sungai tersebut.[3]

Bahasa

Masyarakat Talang Mamak dalam percakapan sehari-hari menggunakan bahasa yang disebut dengan Bahasa Talang Mamak, walaupun dalam percakapan dengan pihak di luar komunitas, mereka biasa menggunakan Bahasa Melayu. Dalam kosakata Bahasa Talang Mamak ini terdapat pengaruh Bahasa Minang dan Bahasa Melayu.[4]

Pemukiman

Suku Talang Mamak sendiri tersebar di kecamatan:

Batang Cenaku, Indragiri Hulu, Riau

18. tolong buat dialog antara Mr. Arya, Mrs Sonia dan Student pake bahasa inggris


Writing Dialogue Based on the Given SituationJawaban
Pendahuluan

Pada soal diminta untuk menuliskan dialog berdasarkan situasi yakni Mrs. Sonia yang bercerita di depan kelas dengan caranya yang sangat mengaggumkan dan menarik, sehingga Mr. Arya dan para murid kagum dan terpesona dengan penampilan Mrs Sonia dan memberikan pujian terhadapnya.


Berdasarkan situasi yang digambarkan, maka ungkapan yang digunakan adalah compliment atau memberikan pujian. Penjelasan lebih lengkap mengenai compliment akan kakak cantumkann linknya di bawah untuk kalian pelajar lebih lanjut.


Nah, sekarang mari kita buat dialognya.


Pembahasan

Mr. Arya: Dear class, today we have our guest who will be telling a story. She is Mrs. Sonia, please welcome!

Mrs. Sonia: Thanks Mr. Arya. Hi guys are you doing well today?

Students: Yes Mrs. Sonia.

Mrs. Sonia: Okay, so firstly I would like to thank the opportunity to attend this class today. In this occasion I would like to tell you guys a story about Mariam, a young girl who fought for her life. Not by anybody else but her. Only herself.

So let’s start the story. Maryam was born as an orphan and raised by her evil aunt. One day her aunt was doing something which is off limit, and Maryam decided to escape from the house without having any single money. For a week she was being homeless and relying on kind people who feed her. One day there was an old man who offered a job to Maryam as a dishwasher in his restaurant. Of course, gladly Maryam took the job. She was a hardworking girl, and the old man was seeing it too. He was impressed by her faith and effort, so he promote her to be a restaurant coordinator. Maryam was beyond happy. It was way more than she was ever expected before. 10 years later and now the old man has passed away, he gave away the restaurant to Maryam as a gift for taking care of him and the restaurant. Now Maryam is becoming a successful businesswoman. We need to learn from her experience.

Students and Mr. Arya: *clapping*

Mr. Arya: Well done Mrs. Sonia! What a wonderful way of story telling. It was such a touchy moment and you captivated us by your words.

Mrs. Sonia: Thank you Mr. Arya for your kind words.
