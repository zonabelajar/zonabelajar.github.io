Text 1. For numbers 1-8.
I love dogs very much. I keep some
dogs in my house. They are Casper,
Midas, Brwonie and Dottie. Casper is a
dachshund. He’s short with long body
and four strong legs. Brwonie is a
collie. She has long and thick fur.
What color is her fur? Brown, of
course that’s why I call her Brownie.
Dottie is a Dalmatian. She has a slim
body and four long legs. She has thin
fur and dots all over her body. The last
is Midas. He is a bulldog. He has a
large head, a short neck and thick
short legs. He’s very strong.
I always take care of my dogs
everyday.

above?
a. Recount b. Narrative c. Descriptive
d. Report

Orientations d. Identifications
