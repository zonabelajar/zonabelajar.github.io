School Holidays at Home Helping Mother

Last semester break, I went everywhere. I just stay at home and do activities to help my mother at home. This year's semester holiday coincides with the time of Ramadan and Eid. So there are many activities that I do.

My activities at home include helping mothers make Eid cakes, preparing iftar menus, helping mothers cook at dawn, and many more.

I usually wake up my mother at 5 am to pray in the morning. Then I went back to sleep until 6. Then I cleaned the house and my mother cooked. Then, after cooking and eating breakfast, I helped my mother wash the dishes.

Around 8 am I watched my favorite shows, Doraemon and Spongebob. After this event, I played at my friend's house named Ani and Siska. We usually play in front of the house. We played many now forgotten traditional games.

Apart from helping my mother, she usually plays at my grandfather's place, whose house is not far from home. Day after day I don't feel like vacation time is ending very quickly. In the end, I had to get back to my student routine. Even though I'm not traveling or traveling during the holidays, this semester break seems a lot of fun to me
