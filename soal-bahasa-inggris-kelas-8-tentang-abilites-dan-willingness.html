5. tolong buatkan dialog bahasa inggris yang di dalam dialog itu ada willingness untuk 3 orang dong


Modal will dan would biasanya digunakan untuk membicarakan mengenai masa yang akan datang atau future tense. Namun, kedua modal ini juga bisa digunakan untuk mengekspresikan keinginan/willingness dan ketidakinginan/unwillingness.

Will digunakan untuk mengekspresikan willingness pada saat sekarang:

I will take you to the hospital, I don't have anything to do this afternoon.If you will wait for a moment, I'll call the taxi.

Will not atau won't digunakan untuk mengekspresikan ketidakinginan/unwillingness atau refusal

Andi just won't answer the phone.The door won't open, I think I forget the pass code.

Would dan wouldn't digunakan untuk membicarakan masa lampau/past tense

I took the phone back to the shop, but they wouldn't give me a replacement.Anita would love to visit you if you were at home.Pembahasan

Situasi: Mum sedang sibuk didapur untuk mempersiapkan makanan untuk pesta ulang tahun, dia meminta Gio untuk membantu adiknya membeli sesuatu di stationary shop dan membeli cake dari bakery.

12. Tolong Kak Batuanya Soal 1. Buatlah 2 kalimat dari ability dan willingness. Nanti Ku follow​


jawaban:

Ability adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk mengekspresikan bentuk kemampuan. Kata yang paling umum digunakan adalah can, could (past) dan managed to. Berikut ini adalah formula untuk menyatakan ability dalam bentuk present tense:

1. Is/am/are able to + main verb

Thomas is able to play the piano.

Katy is not able to play the piano.

2. Can + main verb

Katy can play the flute.

Thomas can't play the flute.

Willingness adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk mengekspresikan bentuk kesediaan. Kata yang paling umum digunakan adalah will, would (past). Berikut ini adalah contoh kalimat untuk menyatakan willingness dalam bentuk present tense:

We’ll see you tomorrow.

Perhaps dad will led me the car.

Contoh dialog:

Katy: Can you help me with my piano practice tomorrow?

Thomas: I'm afraid I can't make it for Sunday. What about next week on Saturday. I will be free and then I can help you whole day.

Katy: Sure, that will be great! Thanks Thomas.

17. What is willingness?? Can you give me 5 example of willingness?!


Willingness is (in Indonesia: Kerelaan) the quality or state of being prepared to do something; readiness.  

synonyms:

readiness, inclination, will, wish, desire, alacrity  

"we appreciate your willingness to help"


Examples:

- "the ability and willingness of workers to migrate"

- "It also expressed willingness that the companies should have rights of way in the streets"

- "Many unfounded rumours of a willingness on the part of the Confederate States to make peace were circulated to weaken the Union war spirit"

- "On the other hand, there were signs of a greater willingness among the Socialists to co-operate with their old enemies the Liberals"

- "He soon showed a willingness to rule as a non-partisan, and favoured the re-enfranchisement of white citizens"
