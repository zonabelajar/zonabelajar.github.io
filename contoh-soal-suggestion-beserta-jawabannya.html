19. buatlah 6 soal pilihan ganda 5 option tentang if clause suggestion beserta jawabannya​


Jawaban:

Part 1 (If Clause – Intermediate Level)

Soal

Fill in the correct forms of the verbs in brackets, according to the type of the IF-sentence shown at the end!

1. If he _______ (EAT) everything he _______ (BE) ill. (3)

2. The police _______ (ARREST) him if they _______ (CATCH) him. (1)

3. What _______ (HAPPEN) if my parachute _______ (NOT OPEN)? (1)

Jawaban

1. If he HAD EATEN everything he WOULD HAVE BEEN ill. (3)

2. The police WILL ARREST him if they CATCH him. (1)

3. What WILL HAPPEN if my parachute DOESN’T OPEN? (1)

Part 2 (If Clause – Intermediate Level)

Soal

Fill in the correct form of the verb in brackets!

1. If Anne’s father gets seriously ill, they _______ (BRING) him to the hospital.

2. If I _______ (BE) you I would pay more attention in class.

3. They _______ (NOT GO) out if they don’t get a babysitter.

Jawaban

1. If Anne’s father gets seriously ill, they WILL BRING him to the hospital.

2. If I WAS/ WERE you I would pay more attention in class.

3. They WON’T GO out if they don’t get a babysitter.

Part 3 (If Clause – Intermediate Level)

Soal

Put the verbs in brackets into their correct tense!

1. The burglars _______ (NOT DO) so much damage if you had locked all the drawers.

2. If he had slipped, he _______ (FALL) 500 metres.

3. They would have forced their way into the house if I _______ (NOT CALL) for help.

Jawaban

1. The burglars WOULDN’T HAVE DONE so much damage if you had locked all the drawers.

2. If he had slipped, he WOULD HAVE FALLEN 500 metres.

3. They would have forced their way into the house if I HADN’T CALLED for help.


20. Contoh soal tentang expression suggestion dalam bahasa inggris


Giving Suggestion

Giving suggestion atau memberi saran adalah ungkapan yang digunakan seseorang untuk memberikan saran atau nasihat kepada orang lain.

Pattern yang digunakan untuk giving suggestion adalah:

Would you be so kind enough to + verb

Contoh kalimat:

Would you be so kind enough to play with me?

Would you be so kind enough to watch movie with me?

I would be so grateful if you would.....

Contoh kalimat:

I would be so grateful if you would play me.

I would be so grateful if you would watch movie with me.

How/What about... + Gerund

Contoh Kalimat:

How about watching movies?

How about playing football?

Suppose

Contoh kalimat:

Suppose that you want to play with me.

Suppose that you want to watch movies me.

21. Pengertian suggestion and offering beserta contohnya


A suggestion is an idea someone proposes. You can accept or reject a suggestion.
example:
I recommed you listen to the new Paramore album
You should try this candy

An Offering is something that you give or offer to someone
example:
Can I help you?
What can I do for you?
Could I get something to relieve you?
Would you like some tea?

38. contoh dialog suggestion beserta artinya ??..


Dialog suggestion adalah percakapan yang memberikan saran. Saran adalah pendapat yang diutarakan oleh seseorang yang dapat digunakan sebagai bahan pertimbangan. Berikut adalah contoh dialog suggestion beserta artinya:

Rose : Hey, is everything good? (Hai, apakah semua baik-baik saja?)

Cinta : No, I failed my exam. I think I studied very hard. But, I still failed the exam. What should I do? Do you have any suggestion for me? (Tidak, aku tidak lulus ujian. Padahal aku sudah belajar dengan giat, tapi masih tetap gagal. Apa yang harus aku lakukan? Apakah kamu ada saran untukku?)

Rose : Well, yeah I do! Cinta, I think you should take Mrs. Rina's extra class. (Ya aku punya! Cinta, aku pikir kamu perlu ambil kelas tambahan Bu Rina)

Cinta : Okay then, I will take Mrs. Rina extra class. Thanks for suggestion, Rose. (Baiklah kalau begitu, aku akan mengambil kelas tambahannya Bu Rina. Terima kasih atas sarannya, Rose).

Pembahasan:

Suggestion memiliki arti sebagai saran yang diberikan dalam suatu forum diskusi. Dalam dialogue suggestion, memberikan ide, memberikan pendapat seringkali digunakan dalam percakapan berlangsung. Adapun cara-cara untuk menuliskan kata suggestion yakni:

I think you should "Aku pikir kamu harus ...."How about ....What about ....

Expression Giving Suggestion and Opinion

Expression Giving Suggestion

Expression giving suggestion adalah ekspresi untuk memberikan saran kepada orang lain, biasanya menggunakan should/had better/ought to/how about atau why don't. Untuk lebih jelasnya berikut contoh dalam kalimat :

You should study hard for the coming exam.Why don't you go home early tonight?You had better tell him the truth.

Asking and Giving Opinion Expression

Dalam asking opinion atau menanyakan tentang pendapat seseorang mengenai suatu hal, phrase yang sering digunakan sebagai berikut :

What is your opinion about....What do you think/reckon?Do you have any idea..Do you have any commentsDo you agree with me?What are your thoughts on that?Would you agree with me that...

Giving Opinion

Dalam giving opinion atau memberikan pendapat, phrase yang sering digunakan diantaranya :

In my opinion/in my humble opinion..I reckon/I think..From my point of viewAs far as I concernedIn my viewI believe/I feel/certainI am surePembahasan
