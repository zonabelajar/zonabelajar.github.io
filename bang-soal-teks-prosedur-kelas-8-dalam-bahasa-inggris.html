contoh teks prosedur dalam bahasa Inggris mengenai "How to Make a Cup of Tea" (Cara Membuat Teh):

How to Make a Cup of Tea

Introduction:

Making a cup of tea is a simple and enjoyable process. Follow these step-by-step instructions to make a perfect cup of tea.

Materials:

- Tea bag or loose tea leaves

- A cup

- Boiling water

- A teaspoon

- Sugar, milk, or lemon (optional)

Procedure:

Step 1: Boil Water

1. Start by boiling a cup of fresh water. You can use a kettle or a microwave to heat the water.

Step 2: Place a Tea Bag or Tea Leaves

2. Place a tea bag in the cup or, if you prefer loose tea leaves, use a tea strainer. If you're using a tea bag, make sure the string is hanging over the edge of the cup for easy removal.

Step 3: Pour Hot Water

3. Once the water reaches boiling point, carefully pour it over the tea bag or tea leaves in the cup. Be sure to fill the cup but leave some space at the top.

Step 4: Steep the Tea

4. Let the tea steep in the hot water for about 3-5 minutes. This allows the flavors to infuse.

Step 5: Remove the Tea Bag

5. After steeping, carefully remove the tea bag by pulling it up using the string or take out the tea strainer.

Step 6: Add Extras (Optional)

6. If desired, add sugar, milk, or a slice of lemon to your tea. Stir gently with a teaspoon to blend the flavors.

Step 7: Enjoy

7. Your cup of tea is ready to enjoy. Sip it slowly and savor the taste.

Conclusion:

Making a cup of tea is a simple yet delightful process. You can adjust the ingredients to suit your taste and preferences. Whether you like it plain or with added flavorings, a well-made cup of tea can be a soothing and comforting beverage.

How To Make Pancake

Ingredients:

1 cup all purpose flour

2 tablespoons of sugar

2 teaspoons of baking powder

½ teaspoons of salt

1 cup full cream milk

2 tablespoons of unsalted butter or vegetable oil

1 egg

toppings such as maple syrup, honey, strawberry jam or chocolate syrup

How To Make:

Add flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt into a small bowl. Whisk them together.

After that, pour into a medium bowl.

Add milk, butter or oil, and egg. Mix the dry ingredients. Whisk until just moistened. Remember, do not overmix.

Heat a cast-iron or non-stick pan. add oil. you can rub the pan with oiled paper towel.

Pour two to three tablespoons of batter onto the pan. You can use the back of the spoon to spread the batter.

Let it cook until the surface has bubbles and a few bursts.

After that, flip the pancake carefully and wait until browned.

Serve warm and add some toppings.

--------------------------------Novel Apple Pudding--------------------------------

Ingredients :

4 green apples, peeled, cored and thickly sliced

1/4 cup/45g self-raising flour, sifted

60g butter or margarine, cubed

1/2 cup/100g caster sugar

1 1/4 cups/300 ml water

Method :

1. place apple in a heat-proof dish, place flour in a bowl, rub in margarine with a spatula,

2. stir in sugar and water (mixture should be lumpy), pour over apples,

3. bake at 180˚C for 1 hour or until syrupy and golden. serve warm with cream or ice cream.

serves 6

preparation time 15 minutes

cooking time 60 to 70 minutes

**How to Make a Cup of Coffee**

**Introduction:** Making a delicious cup of coffee is an easy process that can be done in a few simple steps. Here's how to do it.

**Materials Needed:**

- Freshly ground coffee beans

- Coffee maker

- Filter

- Water

- Coffee mug

- Spoon

- Optional: sugar, cream, or milk

**Procedure:**

1. **Prepare the Coffee Maker:**

- Open the top of the coffee maker.

- Place a clean filter into the filter basket.

2. **Measure Coffee:**

- For a standard cup of coffee, use one to two tablespoons of coffee grounds for every six ounces of water. Adjust based on your taste preferences.

3. **Add Coffee Grounds:**

- Put the measured coffee grounds into the filter.

4. **Add Water:**

- Pour cold, clean water into the coffee maker's water reservoir. Use the amount needed for the number of cups you want to make.

5. **Start Brewing:**

- Close the coffee maker's lid.

- Turn on the coffee maker. It will heat the water and drip it over the coffee grounds.

6. **Wait for Brewing:**

- Be patient. The coffee maker will take a few minutes to brew the coffee.

7. **Pour and Customize:**

- Once brewing is complete, carefully pour the hot coffee into your coffee mug.

- Add sugar, cream, milk, or any other additions to taste.

8. **Enjoy:**

- Stir your coffee and enjoy your freshly brewed cup of coffee!

Remember to be cautious when handling hot liquids, and ensure that your coffee maker is clean and in good working order for the best results.

Making Compost Fertilizer

Compost is a fertilizer from the breakdown of animal and plant remains with the help of living organisms. Compost is a type of organic fertilizer.

Plants and gardens will become more fertile and make the soil more loose by using compost. Therefore, compost can be used as one of the best fertilizer choices.

Here's how to make your own compost easily:

Tool

Hoe

Gloves

Gunny sack

Shovel

Ingredients

Water

Cattle manure

Chopped straw

Leaf litter

husk charcoal

Sawdust

Sugar

EM4

Steps

First, prepare in advance the media to make compost that is safe from rain and sunlight.

Second, make a decomposer solution. The trick is to dissolve EM4 with sugar into water.

Third, make the first layer of compost. By stirring the mixture of manure and husk charcoal evenly. Then add the decomposer and mix until smooth.

Then create a second layer. Sprinkle leaf litter, sawdust, and chopped hay. Stir until smooth and water again with the decomposer.

Close tightly the fertilizer material with a burlap sack.

The next day, re-mix the ingredients until evenly distributed.

Check regularly in the morning and evening. The trick, put on gloves and put your hands into the compost mixture. If it feels hot and your hands can't handle the heat, it means that the fertilizer has not been finished and cannot be used.

Go back to check the fertilizer on the fourth day. Usually the fertilizer is ready to use on the fourth day.

If your hands are able to withstand the heat of the fertilizer mixture, it means that the fertilizer is ready to be used.

Homemade compost is finished.

38. tugas teks prosedur bahasa Inggris​


Jawaban:

A procedure text is a type of written text that provides step-by-step instructions for completing a task. The purpose of a procedure text is to make sure the reader understands how to complete a task correctly and efficiently.

The following are the steps for writing a procedure text in English:

Identify the task: Choose a task that you want to write instructions for and make sure it is clear and specific.

Break down the task into steps: Divide the task into smaller, manageable steps. Make sure each step is clear and concise.

Write the introduction: Start with a brief introduction that explains what the task is and what the reader can expect to learn from the instructions.

Write the steps: Write each step in clear and simple language, using active verbs. Make sure to include all necessary details and information.

Use bullet points or numbered lists: To make the instructions easier to follow, use bullet points or numbered lists to organize the steps.

Include warnings or caution statements: If there are any potential dangers or risks associated with the task, make sure to include them in the instructions.

Write the conclusion: End with a brief conclusion that summarizes the steps and reminds the reader of the overall goal of the task.

Proofread and revise: Before publishing the procedure text, make sure to proofread it for clarity, accuracy, and completeness. Consider revising it if necessary.

Overall, the key to writing an effective procedure text is to provide clear and concise instructions that are easy to follow.
