13. Difference of sympathy and attention (jawab pake bahasa inggris)


Sympathy is the perception, understanding, and reaction to the distress or need of another life form.
Attention is the behavioral and cognitive process of selectively concentrating on a discrete aspect of information, whether deemed subjective or objective, while ignoring other perceivable information.

sympathy : feeling sad about someone's misfortune

attention : notice about someone or something

28. Soal dan jawaban pilihan ganda bahasa inggris tentang attention


Questions and Answers about Asking and Giving AttentionJawaban
Pendahuluan

Expressing attention adalah suatu ungkapan yang digunakan untuk meminta perhatian atau mengajak seseorang untuk fokus perhatiannya pada kita atau orang yang sedang berbicara.

Dalam mengungkapkan attention ada dua macam hal, yakni memberikan (giving) atau meminta (asking).


Contoh asking attention:

- Look!

- Listen!

- Hey!

- Hello?

- Attention, please?

- Excuse me?

- Sorry to bother you


Contoh giving attention:

- Tell me more about it.

- Really?

- What’s next?

- And then what?

- Is that all?

- I see.


Pembahasan

Lala: Really?

Andien: Yeah, I don’t know what got him so loud. As if like nobody will hear him if he lower his volume.

Lala: I see.

Andien: So annoying.
