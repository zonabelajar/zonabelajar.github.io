8. Jelaskan tentang jati diri dalam bahasa inggris


pelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
kategori : Introduction
kata kunci : simple present tense

pembahasan:
pada perkenalan, tense yang digunakan adalah simple present tense.
subject + v1 untuk verbal present tense
subject + am/is/are + noun untuk nominal present tense

example:
Good morning, friends. I would like introduce myself.
my name is gumanti ning rahayu
my nick name is ayuk.
I live with my parents at 902 east kusumanegara street, bantul.
I was graduated from SMPN 1 Yogyakarta (menggunakan passive past tense karena "saya telah lulus")
I was born in Binjai, North Sumatera on July eleventh, fifteen years ago.
My hobbies are cooking and cycling.
I want to be an English teacher someday. My favorite subject is English. That's why I really want to improve my English, so I can speak and write English fluently. And I love teaching too. I always help my sisters to do their English homework.
I think that's all enough. Nice to meet you all. Good bye.

20. "Saya hanya orang biasa yang masih mencari jati diri saya"tolong bantu ubah jadi bahasa inggris,,yang betul ya​


Jawaban:

"Saya hanya orang biasa yang masih mencari jati diri saya"

I am just an ordinary human who is still trying to find out who I really am!

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan suatu hal yang akan dialami oleh semua manusia yang menginginkan suatu perubahan realitas ke arah yang lebih baik dalam hidupnya. Ekspresi mencari jati diri siapa sesungguhnya diri ini (.... trying to find out who I really am!) membutuhkan usaha keras dan cerdas untuk bisa meraih cita cita dan menemukan jati diri yang sesungguhnya.

Semoga membantu ya.

Jawaban:

I'm just an ordinary human-being who is still looking for my identity.

atau

I'm just an ordinary person who is still searching for my identity.

Penjelasan:

Per-word translation:

I'm = saya adalahjust = cuma/hanyaordinary = biasa sajahuman-being/person = manusiawho = yang (penghubung klausa yg mendeskripsikan seseorang)is still = masihsearching for/looking for = mencarimy = kepunyaan akuidentity = identitas/jatidiri.

Those sentences use present continuous tense for the right clause, because the person is still looking for his/her identity.

Kalimat-kalimat diatas pake tense yang present continuous di klausanya, krn orangnya masih nyari identitas/jatidiri.

Masih dan lagi nyari jatidiri ya.

Present continuous (still):I + am + still + verb-ingYou/we/they/(+2 orang/benda) + are + still + verb-ingHe/she/it/(1 orang/benda)/the person + is + still + verb-ing

Contoh: the person who is still searching for my identity.

Large tree,straight trunk,can grow to 30-40 m tall.
Large-leaved,who shed in the dry season.
Teak trees can grow in areas with annual rainfall from 1500 to 2000 mm / year and a temperature of 27-36 ° C in both the lowland or highland.
The area is very good for the development of land with teak ie pH 4. 5-7 and not overrun with water . Teak has a wide elliptical leaf form , and can reach 30-60 cm in adulthood.
