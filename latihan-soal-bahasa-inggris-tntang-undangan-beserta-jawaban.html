School Valedictory
Assalamu’alaikum wr.wb

With all due respect to the honorable Mr. Principal, to the honorable homeroom teachers and other teachers as well as to my dearest friends.

First of all, let’s say our praise and gratitude to the Allah Swt the One and Almighty because of His grace and blessing upon us so that on this good occasion today we can still gather and meet up altogether at this blissful place in the best of our health.

Shalawat and salam may always be upon our adoration and shining model, Prophet Muhammad Saw, upon all of his companions, friends, families, and not forget all of us as his followers, who will insya Allah always be obedient to his teachings.

To all the honorable audience,
Allow me on the behalf of all of my friends to convey a few words and messages regarding this farewell ceremony.
Time flies, it has been 3 years going since our first time to be in this school. Tough, happy, we have experienced and gone through together. And today is the top-end of our days. We will be taking a leave each other and there will be no more togetherness and cohesiveness we will find in the future.
Every single of beautiful story we had here will only be a piece of memories in the future. One day later we will definitely be missing beautiful times like this.

Not forget, we are really thankful to all of the teachers of this school for teaching us the meaning of struggle that finally have brought us to this gateway that leads us to the real life. Without them, we would be nothing. There a lot of things they have taught us, even though sometimes we felt tired, fed up, bored, yet with all of their hearts and patience they stayed teaching and guiding us. To my friends, I wish that this farewell and leaving could somehow tighten our solidarity and I hope this leaving will not be the end of everything.
Go all-out for your goals, My Friends! So that you can get a better living in the future. I wish everything we had in here all this time could be some knowledge that Insya Allah would become all useful and all-to-the good for the sake of people, nation, and religion, and the most important is for our own selves. Aamiin.

I think that’s all I could convey to everyone here. Please, forgive me for the more and less and all of mistakes on my words because mistakes come from human while superiorities come from Allah Swt. Thank you for the attention.

Wabilahitaufik walhidayah wassalamu’alaikum wr.wb

11. Dialog bhasa inggris tntang naiknya harga beras


Rice Price Increase (Kenaikan Harga Beras)

Interviewer (Wartawan) : Tara

Speaker (Narasumber) : Mr. Somat, owner of the "Wakwau" Shop in Pangkalan Susu

W : Mr. Slamet, busy days before Lebaran like this, how is the supply of basic necessities from the distributor, is it still stable or experiencing obstacles to cause the distribution process to stagnate? N : 50% is still smooth, mbak, especially staples such as oil, chili, and sugar. However, there are still some who start often late. W : What items have delayed delivery sir?N : Basic needs that experience delays are rice and eggs. W : The holiday is getting closer, has there been an increase in the price of rice sir? N : Although it is not very significant, but the increase in rice prices has begun to be felt since the beginning of the fasting month yesterday, Ms. Although there are still some whose prices remain stable. W : How do consumers react to this rice price increase sir? N : Yes, as usual there are those who are silent but many are also protesting, even though they finally realize that something like this is common. W : Does the effect of the late distribution of goods and the increase in prices affect your sales turnover every day? N : A little influential also, though only a little. The difference is about 10% of sales before there is a price increase. W : From the beginning of fasting, usually when does the peak of price increase occur? N : Usually from the start fasting continues to crawl up to after Lebaran. W : Does that happen every year? N : Usually that is so. W : Do you usually have market operations from related officials, is it not influential? N : No, you go down the most when there is a visit, after that go up again. W : Alright sir, thank you very much for the information. Hopefully your merchandise is still in demand, sir. N : You're welcome, thank you for praying.

15. Cerita bhasa inggris tntang paskibraka selection


Paskibra
PASKIBRA
Mungkin sudah tidak asing lagi apa kata paskibra, disini saya akan sedikit berbagi cerita mengenai PASKIBRA. Paskibra adalah Pasukan Pengibar Bendera atau bisa di sebut Passus ( Pasukan Khusus ). Banyak orang yang bilang kalo masuk paskibra itu menyeramkan, kejam, cape, dll. Padahal paskibra  itu tidak seperti itu, di paskibra itu banyak loh manfaat yang dapat diambil. Kita dapat belajar berorganisasi, bagaimana cara berhadapan dengan orang banyak, bagaimana sikap kita dengan orang yang lebih tua, selain itu kita juga mendapatkan teman atau bahkan seperti keluarga. Saya akan sedikit bercerita tentang pengalaman di paskibra.Saat saya SMA saya mengikuti eskul paskibra, awalnya saya ikut karena coba-coba aja dan berniat tidak serius tapi justru berlanjut sampai lulus. Saya masuk kedalam angkatan 16, awalnya angkatan saya banyak yang dari 54 orang lalu sisa 28 orang. Mungkin ada yang bilang paskibra kejam, malah kalo saya rasa, eskul paskibra ini saya belajar mandiri, mental saya juga jd bukan mental tempe yang mudah takut atau menyerah. Sewaktu saya menjadi anggota saya sering di DK atau Dinamika Kelompok, tapi karena DK ini saya dan angkatan saya menyatu dan seperti keluarga. Saat saya kelas 1 dan kelas 2 saya mengikuti beberapa kejuaraan paskibra, memang saat latihan sangat lelah. Dari pagi latihan sampai sore, kehujanan, kepanasan, dll. Dari beberapa lomba yang saya ikuti tidak semua juara, memang sangat sedih jika tidak juara tapi itu bukan alasan untuk menyerah. Melainkan menjadi motivasi untuk lebih baik lagi, hasilnya pun saya dan teman – teman menjuarai beberapa lomba. Disini juga saya belajar berorganisasi, saya pernah menjadi panitia dalam membuat acara LKBB, ternyata membuat acara itu tidak mudah loh, tapi dengan semangat 45 dari seluruh panitia dan juga purna – purna yang mendukung acara ini dapat terlaksana. Di paskibra juga tidak sedikit yang cinta lokasi. Pokoknya masuk paskibra tidak akan menyesal deh.Maaf kalo salah!

17. Soal menyangkut tntang *Transformator*#Tolong di jawab bsk dikumpul


diketahui :

Ns = 5.000 lilitan

Ip = 2 A

Is = 1 A

Vs = 3.000 v

ditanya :

a)  Np

b)  Vp

jawab :

a)  Np / Ns  =  Is / Ip

   Np / 5.000  =  1 / 2

   Np  =  5.000 x 1/2

          =  2.500 lilitan


b)  Vp / Vs  =  Is / Ip

    Vp / 3.000  =  1/2

     Vp  =  3.000 x 1/2

            =  1.500 v
