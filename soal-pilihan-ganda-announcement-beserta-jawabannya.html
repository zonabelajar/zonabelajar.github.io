6. Contoh announcement beserta jawabanya


Spoken Announcement - School Trip

[Opening]

Dear all, today I will deliver the summary of today’s OSIS’ meeting regarding to our school trip. Please listen carefully.

[Content]

Our school trip destination is Borobodur Temple. The trip itself will last for 3 days 4 nights departing on October 27th. The cost for this trip is IDR 150,000 including transportation, meals and accommodation. If you’re interested please make a confirmation by October 20th to the organizing committee through email at osis@sma-ic.com or send an sms to 0858582134.

[Closing]

That is all for today’s announcement. Let me know if you have any question. Thank you.

Pembahasan

Announcement adalah jenis teks pengumuman yang membagian informasi mengenai suatu hal kepada banyak orang.

Announcement ada dalam bentuk tertulis maupun lisan. Untuk contoh kali ini adalah spoken announcement (lisan) yang diucapkan untuk mengajak dan memberitahukan siswa yang tertarik untuk mengikuti tur sekolah ke Borobudur.

Generic Structure of Announcement

1. Stating Purpose: The text that contains what event will be held

2. Stating Day and Date: Day and Date realization. The text that contains when the event will be held

3. Stating Place: The text that contains where the event will be held

4. Informing Sender: The text that contains name of the person who will be contacted

The first announcement is about study tour to Parapat for students of Grade IX SMPN 2 Medan.

The second announcement is about lost suitcase.

February 18th 2019, at 7 a.m.

At least 30 minutes before departure.

Student ID card, mobile phone and small note.

We can hear the announcement at the airport.

In the waiting room gate 25.

I should contact to the information center about it.

Yes, it’s the generic structure.
