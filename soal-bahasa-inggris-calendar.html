Membuat kalender

1 .Pada menu file , klik baru.

2. Klik bawaan, lalu klik kalender. (Di Publisher 2010, klik kalender di bawah yang paling populer.)

3. Klik kalender yang ingin Anda buat. Kalender dikategorikan menurut tipe (misalnya, halaman penuh atau ukuran dompet).

4 .Di panel tugas, klik opsi yang Anda inginkan, seperti skema warna atau font, orientasi halaman, dan apakah akan memperlihatkan satu bulan atau satu tahun per halaman kalender (untuk mengubah bulan ketika kalender dimulai, klik Atur tanggal kalender).

29. When was the calendar year invented by humans?​


Jawaban:

The calendar year, as a system for measuring and organizing time, was invented by humans thousands of years ago. The exact date of its invention is not known, but various ancient civilizations, such as the Sumerians and Egyptians, developed early calendar systems based on the lunar or solar cycles. The Gregorian calendar, which is the calendar most commonly used today, was introduced by Pope Gregory XIII in 1582 as a reform of the Julian calendar
