16. Buat kan kalimat tentang to green the school yaitu activities that i should do dengan activities that i should not do masing masing 10 kalimat,ke dalam bahasa inggris dan bahasa indonasia


SHOULD AND SHOULDN'T

 

Jawaban  

 

Pendahuluan  

Should dan shouldn't mempunyai dua tujuan, yaitu:  

Should dan shouldn't digunakan ketika kita ingin memberikan saran kepada orang lain.

Should dan shouldn't digunakan untuk menyatakan sebuah kewajiban. Walaupun digunakan untuk menyatakan kewajiban, tingkatannya masih dibawah MUST.

Formula:

Should dan shouldn't adalah sebuah modals, jadi harus diikuti V1

S + Should/Shouldn't + V1


Pembahasan  

 Buatlah kalimat menggunakan should dan shouldn't dengan tema "green the school"

Should

You should plant many trees to green the school.You should water the plant everyday.You should clean the blackboard every after class.You should keep the garden clean.You should wipe the withered leaves.You should arrange the flower vase well.You should pick the litter and throw it into trash can.You should reduce the use of paper and tissue.You should nurture the plant regularly.You should wash your hand after planting or nurturing plant.

Shouldn't

You shouldn't destroy any plant in the school.\You shouldn't let the plant dried.You shouldn't let the withered leaves pollute your school.You shouldn't litter everywhere.You shouldn't let the plant died.You shouldn't use many paper and tissue.You shouldn't pick any flower from the vase.You shouldn't forget to water the flower.You shouldn't forget to nurture the plant.You shouldn't forget to wash your hand after planting or nurturing.
