17. soal pilihan ganda tentang past tense vs present perfect


Simple Past Tense vs Present Perfect Tense

Simple Past Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang berlangsung di waktu lampau.

Keterangan waktu yang sering digunakan dalam simple past tense antara lain:

last week/ night/ month/ yearyesterdayonce upon a timeone daythis morningdan lain lain

Pattern yang digunakan dalam simple past tense adalah:

(+)S + V2

(-)S + did not + V1  

(?) Did + S + V1


Present Perfect Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian sebelum sekaran (lampau). Perbedaan Present Perfect Tense dengan Simple Past Tense adalah: Simple Past Tense memerlukan kata keterangan waktu yang jelas, sedangkan Present Perfect Tense tidak.

Namun, biasanya dalam present perfect tense menggunakan kata since dan for.

Since diikuti dengan waktu yang jelas, seperti since 1992, since monday, dan lain-lain.

For diikuti dengan durasi waktu, seperti for a week, for ten years, dan lain lain.

19. Buat fungsi simple past tense dan future tense, rumusnya ,contoh kalimatnya (+, _, ?) Dan contoh soal pilihan ganda 4 (2 past tense, 2 future tense)​


Jawaban:

Simple past tense untuk membicarakan aksi yang terjadi pada satu waktu tertentu di masa lampau

future tense untuk membicarakan aksi yang terjadi pada satu waktu tertentu di masa yang akan datang.

rumus:

simple past

+ ) s + v2+ O.

-) s + did + v1 + O

?) did + s +v1 + O ?.

simple future

+ ) s + will/shall +v1 + O

- ) s + will/shall+not+v1+O

? ) will/shall+s+v1+O ?.

contoh :

simple past tense

+ ) i ate rice , yesterday( saya makan nasi kemarin)

-) i did eat rice, yesterday(saya tidak makan nasi kemarin)

? ) did i eat rice,yesterday ?.(apakah saya makan nasi kemarin?)

simple future

+ ) i will eat rice tomorrow ( aku akan makan nasi besok)

- ) i wil not eat rice tomorrow(aku tidak akan makan nasi besok)

? ) will i eat rice tomorrow ?.(akankan aku makan nasi besok?)

note: untuk penggunaan "will" ketika subjek kalimat berbentuk (i,you,we,they,she,he,it,dan nama orang lebih dari satu nama ex: doni and riza)

untuk penggunaan shall (I,she,he,it,dan nama orang tunggal ex : doni )

37. pengertian, rumus, dan contoh past event


Past event = sebuah keadaan/kejadian yang telah berlalu (sudah di masa lampau)

Past tense
Simple :
(+) S + V2 + O
Cth : He(S) bought(V2) a cake(O)
(-) S + did + not + V1 + O
Cth : They(S) didn't/did not play(V2) cards(O)
(?) Did + S + V1 + O + ?
Cth : Did you(S) watch(V1) the movie(O) ?

Past continuous :
(+) S + was/were(to be) + V1-ing + O
I(S) was(to be) doing(V1-ing) my homework(O)
(-) S + was/were(to be) + not + V1-ing + O
We(S) were(to be) not riding(V1-ing) our bicycles(O)
(?) was/were(to be) + S + V1-ing + O + ?
Was(to be) she(S) walking(V1-ing) her dog(O) ?

Past perfect :
(+) S + had + V3 + O
He(S) had eaten(V3) crepes(O)
(-) S + had + not + V3 + O
We(S) had not gone(V3) to school(O)
(?) Had + S + V3 + O + ?
Had Sheena(S) brought(V3) her book(O) ?

Past perfect continuous :
(+) S + had + been + V-ing + O
They(S) had been playing(V-ing) volleyball(O)
(-) S + had + not + been + V-ing + O
I(S) had not been seeing(V-ing) her(O)
(?) Had + S + been + V-ing + O + ?
Had Mira(S) been reading(V-ing) books(O) ?
Past event adalah kejadian yang lampau atau telah berlalu.

Simple past

- I went to the library yesterday.

(-) S+tobe(did)+not+V1+O
- She did not come to my party last sunday

(?) did+S+V1+O
- did you delive my order yesterday?

kelas : SMA
Pelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
Kategori : Past event text
kata kunci : past verb, past tense

pembahasan:
Past event di sini mungkin maksudnya teks yang terjadi di waktu lampau. Dalam bahasa Inggris, ada beberapa teks yang terjadi di waktu lampau, yaitu
1) recount text
2) spoof text
3) news item
4) biography recount text
5) narrative text

Pada teks lampau, menggunakan past verb (verb 2) dengan rumus
(+) subject + v2
(-) subject + did not + v1
(?) did + subject + v1?
yes, subject did.
no, subject did not.

contoh past event dalam recount text:

An Unlucky Camping

it was the beginning of wet season, but the weather was fine in the morning. my classmates and I went to camping to the camping ground.

we set up our tent in the middle of field near a small river. as soon as this was done, we cooked a meal over the open fire. everything was alright in the afternoon. but at night while we were singing songs by the campfire, a strong wind blew. it bought disorder to our tent. we couldn't sleep because it was thundering and raining heavily. lighting intensified the downpours. soon the surrounding areas became flooded. we all panicked.

Contoh biography recount text:

LEVI STRAUSS

Levi Strauss came to the United States from Bavaria as a teenager.
After spending several years in New York, he made a journey to California when the gold rush was at its peak. He got the idea to sell tents to the gold miners, but they were not interested. 
He recycled the brown canvas cloth into sturdy pants which became a great demand in mining camps. Later he began making workpants in a blue cotton fabrics imported from France, called "Serge de Nimes", but known in America as "Denim".
Along with Jacob Davis, a tailor from Nevada, Strauss patented a pattern of copper rivets to strengthen the stress point on pants, especially the pockets. They first became knows as waist overalls, then "levis".
They became so popular that Strauss concentrated most of his effort on the manufacture and sales of the durable work pants.

B: She has curly red hair and a cute smile. Everybody likes her. (Rambutnya merah keriting dan senyumannya sangat manis. Setiap orang menyukainya).

Pakaian
