3. apa yang dimaksud unsur explicit dan implicit dalam bahasa inggris?


kelas : SMA
Pelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
kategori : opposite meaning
kata kunci : explicit, implicit

pembahasan:
Dalam bahasa Indonesiam unsur explicit sama dengan unsur tersurat. sedangkan unsur implicit adalah unsur tersirat.

Makna kata:
Implicit is indirectly stated or implied.
Explicit is directly stated and spelled out.

contoh pada kalimat:
She said she likes black shoes by saying she likes all colors but white.
Pada kalimat di atas, ia menyukai sepatu hitam. secara tersirat, dia berkata menyukai semua sepatu selain putih

He said, you will not attend that birthday party.
pada kalimat di atas, secara jelas bahwa kamu TIDAK diundang di pesta ulang tahun itu.

11. Bantu Jawab Soal Bahasa Inggris Given the real fact (meaning) of the following sentences​


Jawaban:

1. I would have been happy if you had taught me.

Real fact/it means: The speaker would have experienced happiness if the person being addressed had taught them something.

2. If she had seen you, she would have called you.

Real fact/it means: If the woman had seen you, she would have made a phone call to you.

3. He would have sung if he had gone from here.

Real fact/it means: If he had left this place, he would have sung.

4. He would have gotten a gift if he had won in the contest.

Real fact/it means: If he had been successful in the contest, he would have received a gift.

5. If I had mastered the presentation's material last night, I would not have been nervous right now.

Real fact/it means: If the speaker had fully understood and prepared the material for the presentation the previous night, they would not be feeling nervous at the present moment.

6. If I finished my Chemistry assignments, I would accompany you to the bookstore.

Real fact/it means: If the speaker completed their Chemistry assignments, they would go with the person being addressed to the bookstore.

7. My brother would have gone to the ball if he had had enough money.

Real fact/it means: If the speaker's brother had sufficient funds, he would have attended the ball.

8. If he had been free, he could have gone to the beach.

Real fact/it means: If he had not been occupied or had no prior commitments, he would have had the opportunity to go to the beach.

9. You couldn't have gone to her house if you hadn't known her address.

Real fact/it means: If you had not been aware of her address, it would have been impossible for you to visit her house.

10. He would have met the doctor if he hadn't come late.

Real fact/it means: If he had arrived on time, he would have had the chance to meet the doctor.

11. They would have won the match if they had practiced a lot.

Real fact/it means: If they had put in a significant amount of practice, they would have emerged as the winners of the match.

12. They would have attended the meeting if we had taken the first train.

Real fact/it means: If the speaker and the others had caught the first train, they would have been able to attend the meeting.

13. If they had invited fewer people, the party could have been held at their house.

Real fact/it means: If a smaller number of individuals had been invited, it would have been possible to host the party at their house.

14. Rudi would have lost his way in London if he hadn't been able to speak English well.

Real fact/it means: If Rudi had not possessed good English language skills, he would have faced difficulty navigating through London.

15. I would build the house if I had a lot of money.

Real fact/it means: If the speaker had a substantial amount of money, they would construct the house.

35. contoh pertanyaan implicit dan explicit dalam announcement?


a.   Pengertian

Ujian Nasional (UN) adalah kegiatan pengukuran dan penilaian kompetensi peserta didik secara nasional untuk jenjang pendidikan dasar dan menengah. UN bertujuan menilai pencapaian kompetensi lulusan secara nasional pada mata pelajaran tertentu dalam kelompok mata pelajaran ilmu pengetahuan dan teknologi. Mata pelajaran yang di UN-kan adalah Bahasa Indonesia, Matematika, Bahasa Inggris, dan IPA. Peserta UN dinyatakan apabila mencapai syarat – syarat nilai tertentu. Untuk tahun ini, peserta UN dinyatakan lulus jika memiliki nilai rata – rata minimal 5,5 untuk seluruh mata pelajaran yang diujikan, dengan nilai minimal 4,0 paling banyak dua mata pelajaran dan minimal 7,0 untuk mata pelajaran lainnya.

 

b.   Standar Kelulusan (SKL)

Ada dua kemampuan yang diujikan dalam UN bahasa Inggris, yaitu:

1. READING (Membaca) Memahami makna dalam wacana tertulis pendek baik teks fungsional pendek berbentuk caution, greeting card, short message, invitation, announcement, label, advertisement, & letter maupun esai sederhana berbentuk descriptive, procedure, report, narrative dan recount dalam konteks kehidupan sehari – hari. Disini, siswa akan diuji kemampuannya untuk:

Menentukan gambaran umum (finding general idea)Menentukan ide utama / pikiran utama (finding main idea)Menentukan informasi rinci tersurat (finding explicit detailed information)Menentukan informasi tersirat (finding implicit information)Menentukan rujukan kata (finding reference word) danMenentukan makna (persamaan/lawan) kata, frase, atau kalimat.

39. buatlah 10 kalimat active dan passive meaning ​dlm bahasa Inggris


1. Passive: Napa Valley is known for its excellent wines.

Active: [Many people] know Napa Valley for its excellent wines.

2. Passive: Twenty civilians were killed in the bomb explosion.

Active: Someone killed twenty civilians in the bomb explosion

3. Passive: The Mona Lisa was painted by Leonardo Da Vinci. (agent =Leonardo Da Vinci )

Active: Leonaro Da Vinci painted the Mona Lisa.

4. Active: While Mr. Taylor was driving down Highway 101, a police officer pulled him over and gave him a speeding ticket.

Passive: While Mr. Taylor was driving down Highway 101, he was pulled over and given a ticket by a police officer.

5. A:The Beatles wrote "A Hard Day's Night".

P:"A Hard Day's Night" was written by the Beatles.

6. A: Spielberg directed the movie ET.

P: The movie ET was directed by Spielberg.

7. A:My father built this house.

P: This house was built by my father.

8. A:Lee Harvey Oswald killed President Kennedy.

P : President Kennedy was killed by Lee Harvey Oswald.

9. A:

Somebody has stolen my wallet.

P : My wallet has been stolen.

10. A:XYZ Company is painting our house.

P: Our house is being painted.
