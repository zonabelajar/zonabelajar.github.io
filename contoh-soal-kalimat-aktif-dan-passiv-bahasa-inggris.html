7. lelaki tersebut menjual buah buahan setiap hari.diubah kedalam kalimat passiv


the man sells fruits every day
maaf kalau salah

Subject : English

Category : Passive Voice

Level : JHS VIII

Code : 8.5

Keywords : by, passive

-------------

The passive voice is one of the three grammatical voice.

In passive voice, the noun or noun phrase that is the object of an active voice becomes the subject.  

Construction of passive voice : S + be + past participle  (v₃)

Function of the passive voice : to emphasize the object and the action instead of the subject of a sentence .

-----------

Lelaki tersebut menjual buah-buahan setiap hari.

The man sells fruits every day.

Kalimat aktif di atas menggunakan simple present tense (S + v₁) karena keterangannya adalah every day.

Konstruksi kalimat pasif untuk simple present tense adalah S + am/is/are + v₃

Maka kalimat pasifnya adalah

Fruits are sold by the man every day.

19. Ubahlah kalimat passiv dalam kalimat my mother cooks asam padeh in the kitchen


Jawaban:

Asam padeh is cooked by my mother in the kitchen.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat pada soal adalah kalimat aktif dengan tense Simple Present. Maka untuk mengubahnya menjadi kalimat pasif, formula sederhananya adalah sbb.:

Subject + to be (kata kerja bantu) + Verb 3 + Object

Dikarenakan tense kalimatnya adalah Simple Present, maka to be yang digunakan untuk membantu Verb 3 ialah is.

Jawaban:

Asam Padeh is cooked by My Mother In the Kitchen.

Penjelasan:

CARA BIAR JADI PASIF.

- Kita amati verbnya dulu (kata kerja/predikatnya) yakni cooks (karena ada tambahan S maka itu verb 1)

- Untuk mengubah ke passive karena Verb 1 kita pakai to be Is (karena mother is). Kenapa gak was? (Was untuk Verb 2)

- is + Past participle/alias verb 3 dari cooks = cooked

Kalimat passive

Penjelasan:

Kalimat soal adalah bentuk Active dari Past Tense, karena terdiri dari Subjek dan Verb 2

(+) S + Verb 2 + O

= Edwin + studied + English

(Edwin belajar B.inggris)

Maka kalimat Passive:

(+) S + To Be (was/were) + Verb 3 + By

= English + was + studied + By Edwin

(B. Inggris dipelajari oleh Edwin)

V2/V3 = Studied

39. kalimat passiv i didnt make that pie. Did mrs french make it?


Answer:


Passive sentence is a form of sentence which focuses on object, so it starts with an object at the beginning of the sentence instead of using subject. The pattern is : O + auxiliary verb + V3 + by + S

The sentence above is past tense so the past passive of it is

O + was/were + V3 + by + S

The answer of such exercise is

'That pie wasn't made by me. Was it made by Mrs.French?'


Hopefully that is clear enough for you.


Kelas: SMP

Mapel: English

Kategori: passive voice

40. tolong rubah kalimat passiv ini "chiken is thrown by udin" ke aktive​


Jawaban:

Udin throw the chicken.

Penjelasan:

Formula/Rumusnya:

Active: S+V1+O

Passive: S+to be+V3+O

Jawaban:

Udin throws chicken

Pembahasan

chiken is thrown by udin

Structure Kalimatnya:

(+) S + Be (is/am/are) + V3 + By Agent

Subject => Chicken Tobe => is (karena Subjek tunggal)Verb 3 => thrown By agent => By udin

Berdasarkan keterangan di atas:Kalimat bentuk Passive karena Subjek diberi aksi oleh Agent (By Agent)Kalimat di atas menggunakan Pola Passive dari Present Tense Be (is) + V3 (thrown)

Maka Rumus yg digunakan:

Active : (+) S + V1 (-s/es) + O

=> Udin + throws + chicken

Catatan:

Verb+s (throws) karena Subjek tunggal yaitu udin seorang
