2. Contoh soal tentang expression suggestion dalam bahasa inggris


Giving Suggestion

Giving suggestion atau memberi saran adalah ungkapan yang digunakan seseorang untuk memberikan saran atau nasihat kepada orang lain.

Pattern yang digunakan untuk giving suggestion adalah:

Would you be so kind enough to + verb

Contoh kalimat:

Would you be so kind enough to play with me?

Would you be so kind enough to watch movie with me?

I would be so grateful if you would.....

Contoh kalimat:

I would be so grateful if you would play me.

I would be so grateful if you would watch movie with me.

How/What about... + Gerund

Contoh Kalimat:

How about watching movies?

How about playing football?

Suppose

Contoh kalimat:

Suppose that you want to play with me.

Suppose that you want to watch movies me.

18. Contoh dialog expression of instruction 3 orang


Dialog menggunakan ekspreksi pemberian instruksi harus menggunakan kalimat yang benar agar maksudnya tersampaikan. Nah, berikut ini akan kakak jelaskan apa itu giving instruction dan kata-kata yang sering digunakan.

Memberikan instruksi adalah sebuah ungkapan yang digunakan agar orang lain melakukan hal yang kita minta atau instruksikan. Kata instruksi juga berarti imperatif. Untuk memberikan instruksi atau membuat kalimat imperatif, kalian tidak selalu harus membutuhkan subjek di awal kalimat, karena biasanya subjeknya sudah jelas yaitu orang yang diajak bicara.


Tense yang digunakan adalah simple present.


Beberapa ungkapan untuk memberikan instruksi:

First,……..And then,……Next,……..Lastly,……..The first thing you do is………..Once you’ve done that, then………In the end,……….

Sementara itu, berikut ini adalah contoh-contoh kalimat imperatif yang tidak membutuhkan subjek di awal kalimat. Cukup menuliskan dengan struktur be + adjektif. Contoh:

Be quiet!Be strong!
Pembahasan

Andi: Mom, what are you cooking?

Dian: Yes, mom, is there anything we can help?

Mom: I'm not cooking, just blending some smoothies. Okay guys, if you'd like to help me, please listen all my instruction carefully okay?

Andi & Dian: Yes mom!

Mom: Firstly, Andi, wash all the greens vegetables, I've already prepared spinach, kale and cucumber. Remember to wash it very carefully okay?

Andi: Okay.

Mom: After that, Dian, I've washed the fruits already, so please chop the fruits in the table. There are apples, bananas, and pineapple. No need to be too small, just a cubed size.

Dian: Yes Mom.

Mom: Now after you've finished, Andi, place the vegetables into the blender first. And Dian continue putting the chopped fruits. I will pour some water and ice cubes.

Andi & Dian: Done mom!

Mom: Okay so I will blend until it becomes smooth, and ready to drink. You guys want some?

Andi & Dian: Of course!!!

V1 + Object

Let’s + V1 + Object

V1 + Object + Please (bisa di awal atau di akhir kalimat)
