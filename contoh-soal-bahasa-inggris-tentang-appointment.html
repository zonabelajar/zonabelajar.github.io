2. buatlah contoh percakapan bahasa inggris declining appointment dalam dunia perkantoran.​


Jawaban:Making and declining appointment (membuat dan membatalkan/menolak janji).

Receptionist : Good morning. What can I do for you Mam? (Selamat pagi. Ada yang bisa saya bantu Ibu?)

Bella : Good morning. My name is Bella. I would like to meet Dr. Simpson to check my teeth. (Selamat pagi. Nama saya Bella. Saya ingin bertemu Dr. Simpson untuk memeriksakan gigi saya).

Receptionist : Have you made the appointment with him before? (Sudahkah anda membuat janji dengannya sebelumnya?).

Bella : Yes, I have. I have called him and he told me to come to the clinic at 9 A.M. (Ya, sudah. Saya sudah menelfonnya dan dia memberitahu saya untuk datang ke klinik jam 9 pagi).

Receptionist : I’m sorry Mam. Unfortunately he has just gone to attend a meeting. Didn’t he tell you? (Maaf Bu. Sayangnya dia baru saja pergi untuk menghadiri sebuah pertemuan. Tidakkah dia memberitahu anda Bu?).

Bella : No, he didn’t give any confirmation to me. (Tidak, dia tidak memberi konfirmasi ke saya).

Receptionist : Would you mind waiting him Mam? (Maukah anda menunggu dia Bu?).

Bella : Hmm, what time is the meeting over? (Hmm, jam berapakah pertemuannya selesai?).

Receptionist : Probably it will be over around 11 A.M. (Kemungkinan pertemuannya akan selesai sekitar jam 11 siang).

Bella : I wish I could, but unfortunately I have to pick my father up in the airport at 11 A.M. Maybe I’d like to change my appointment with Dr. Simpson. Can you rearrange the appointment for me please? (Saya harap saya bisa, namun sayangnya saya harus menjemput ayah saya di bandara jam 11 pagi. Mungkin saya ingin merubah janji dengan Dr. Simpson. Bisakah anda mengatur ulang janjinya untuk saya?).

Receptionist : With my pleasure Mam. Let me check his schedule first. How about tomorrow at 10 A.M? He is available at that time. (Dengan senang hati Bu. Coba saya cek jadwalnya terlebih dahulu. Bagaimana jika besok jam 10 pagi? Dr. Simpson luang pada jam tersebut).

Bella : Alright. I will come tomorrow at 10 A.M. Thank you. (Baiklah. Saya akan datang besok jam 10 pagi. Terima kasih).

Receptionist : You’re welcome. (Terima kasih kembali).

Hotel: Should you have any questions or requests, please dial 'O' from your room. Also, there is internet available in the lobby 24 hours a day.

Guest: Ok, and what time is check-out?

Hotel: At midday, sir.

Guest: Ok, thanks.

Hotel: My pleasure, sir. Have a wonderful stay at the Grand Woodward Hotel.

11. buat dialog make an appointment! tolong dibantu​


Patricia: Hello, I’d like to make an appointment to see the doctor, please. (Halo. Saya ingin membuat janji bertemu dengan dokter.)

Receptionist: Have you been here before? (Apakah Anda pernah ke sini sebelumnya?)

Patricia: No, I haven’t. (Belum pernah.)

Receptionist: Okay. What is your name? (Siapa nama Anda?)

Patricia: It’s Patricia Sanchez. (Patricia Sanchez.)

Receptionist: Alright, Mrs. Sanchez. Can I arrange an appointment today at 1 p.m.? (Baik. Nyonya Sanchez. Apa saya bisa jadwalkan untuk bertemu dokter hari ini pukul 1 siang?)

Patricia: Sure. No problem. (Baik Tidak ada masalah.)

Receptionist: Please fill this registration form to proceed this appointment. (Mohon isi formulir pendaftaran ini untuk menyetujui jadwal ini.)

Patricia: Sure. (Reading the form carefully.) Should I fill in the medical insurance number column? I don’t have medical insurance. (Baik. [Membaca formulir itu dengan seksama.] Apa saya harus mengisi kolom nomor asuransi kesehatan? Saya tidak punya asuransi kesehatan.)

Receptionist: You can skip that. (Anda bisa mengosonginya.)

After a moment. (Setelah beberapa lama.)

Patricia: Here you go. (Ini formulirnya.)

Receptionist: Thank you. You have been arranged to see the doctor at 1 p.m. (Terima kasih. Anda telah dijadwalkan bertemu dokter pukul 1 siang.)

Patricia: Okay. See you. (Baik. Sampai jumpa.)

13. languange feature make an appointment ​


Making an appointment in English typically involves the following language features:

Verbs: "make," "schedule," "reserve," "book," "request," etc.Nouns: "appointment," "meeting," "visit," "reservation," etc.Time expressions: "tomorrow," "next week," "at 2 PM," "on Tuesday," etc.Modals: "can," "could," "would," "should," "may," etc.

Examples of making an appointment in English could be:

Can I make an appointment for next Wednesday at 3 PM?I would like to schedule a visit with the doctor.I am calling to request an appointment for next Monday at 9 AM.I was wondering if it's possible to book a meeting for next Friday.May I reserve an appointment for the day after tomorrow?

It's important to note that the language used may vary depending on the context, formality and the culture you are in.

19. Rivano, Sorry, I have to cancel our appointment to go window shopping today. I forgot that I have had an appointment with the dentist today. Thanks for your understanding. Virnandabahasa Indonesianya ​


Jawaban:

Rivano, Maaf, saya harus membatalkan janji kita untuk belanja jendela hari ini. Saya lupa bahwa saya punya janji dengan dokter gigi hari ini. Terima kasih atas pengertian Anda

Jawaban:

Translations:

Rivano, Maaf, aku harus membatalkan janji kita untuk window shopping (jalan-jalan di mall) hari ini. Aku lupa bahwa aku punya janji dengan dokter gigi hari ini.

Terima kasih atas pengertianmu.

Virnanda

Penjelasan:

Jawaban merupakan materi pembelajaran Translations (terjemahan) dari teks Bahasa Inggris.

Bentuk kalimat yang digunakan dalam teks adalah Simple Present Tense karena menyatakan fakta, Simple Past Tense karena menjelaskan aktivitas kegiatan di masa lampau dan Present Perfect Tense karena menjelaskan aktivitas kegiatan yang sudah dilakukan sebelumnya dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Simple Present Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

Simple Past Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-2 + Object

Present Perfect Tense:

(+) Subject + Have/Has + Verb-3 + Object

- come on time or earlier as your client might be having another agenda afterwards.

27. What is reservation and an appointment?​


a reservation is an arrangement to keep something.an appointment is a formal arrangement with someone. For example, a doctor, a dentist, a lawyer, a consultant.

semoga membantu :)

Reservation itu memesan atau booking, misal memesan kamar di hotel, memesan meja di restoran, memesan tempat di suatu lokasi.

Dalam bahasa inggris:

Appointment is making an appointment to meet, or it could mean a position of position and it could be a position of furniture in the room

Reservation is ordering or booking, for example booking a room at a hotel, ordering a table at a restaurant, ordering a place at a location.
