6. Contoh cerita bahasa inggris yang didalam 1 cerita terdapat simple past tense, present perfect tense, past perfect tense


Haunted theatre
Last week, me and my family went to XXI in Kuningan city mall of Jakarta. When we arrived at the mall, we directly rushed to XXI cinema and bought the tickets. After we bought the tickets, we went to have lunch. We had our lunch in Ichiban sushi. We checked the time and it's our turn to watch so we decided to hurry and go to the cinema. When we arrived, the cinema was very deserted and quiet. Then we finally realized that there was no one. Not a single soul except the silent waiter, the ticket cashier and us. The atmosphere was really quiet and my mother was scared so we entered the the studio quickly. Inside the studio, there was no one again. I started to feel the chills and cringed. Not long later the lamps was turned off and the movie began. During the movie, I felt something or someone touched me. I was curious so I looked behind. I saw a shadow of a man without the person. I thought my minds was playing tricks with me so i just forget about the shadow and enjoyed the movie. After the movie finished, I asked my mother and father whether they had felt someone touched them. They said that they also felt the same. Suddenly, a nearby lamp explodes by itself! Me and my family was really shocked and ran away to the parking lot. We rushed home as fast as we can. At home, I did a research about the cinema and found out that there was a person called Mr. Jenovocic who died during the fire in the cinema. When I read the review, people said that his shadow and soul never left the cinema and always appear on 23rd May, the day he died. I also found out that 23rd May was the day when I and my family went there. I faced left and right to check if there are any shadows. Fortunately, there are no shadows. But, I forgot to face behind. When I faced behind I saw a shadow and went into a coma for two whole days. When I woke up I realized that shadow was my own shadow.

10. Sebutkan rumus dari bahasa Inggris simple present tense, simple future tense, Simple Past Tense, simple past future tense, rumusnya bentuk aktif dan pasif makasih​


Jawaban:

Simple present tense

Active voice : S + V1s/es

Passive voice : S + am/is/are + verb-3

Simple Past Tense

Active voice: S + verb-2

Passive voice: S + was/were + verb-3

Simple Future Tense

Active voice: S + will + V1

Passive voice : S + will + be + verb-3

Simple Past Future Tense

Active voice: S + would + V1

Passive Voice : S + Would + be + verb-3

                                     My Aunt's Office

                My name is Alicia. I am spending my holiday in Jakarta. I stay with my aunt. Her name is Farah Adusi. She is 22 years old. She works as an administrative staff of a Production House in Jakarta. I visited her office last week. Her office is unique. I will tell you about it.  

              Aunt Farah shares an office space with one of her colleagues, Miss Destri. She also works as an administrative staff of the company. They are very close friend.  They went to the same high school. My aunt's office is a room with size about 4 m long and 3 m wide, with light grey paint covers all the walls. When you enter the room, you will find a big window right across the room. In the morning, it is showered by the morning sun light because it faces the east direction. The room is bright so that the ceiling lights are not needed much during the day.

               There is a long wooden desk in the middle of the room with four chairs well arranged surround it. My aunt bought it when she traveled to Jepara two years ago. Two personal computers are put on the table. There is no cubicle in the office. Aunt Farah and Miss Destri sit in rows of seats face north in front of their own personal computers. There are a printer and a cordless telephone in the free space between them. There is also a small metal tray on the table in which they put the stationery neatly. In one side of the wall, there is a huge whiteboards. It is full of notes of the deadlines about some projects which they are working on.  

                 Miss Destri brought a flashy blue clock that is hanged on the wall next to the whiteboard. In the corner of the room there is a cabinet used to store many files. Right next to the cabinet, there is a small wooden table which is covered with white and blue stripes  tablecloth. There is a beautiful flower vase with a huge red roses bouquet on it. Near the window, there is a pile of books and work portfolios which are kept neatly on the shelf.  

                 My aunt's office is not too large but it is clean and well organized. The air is always fresh and cool because there is an air conditioner installed on the wall. I like how my aunt arrange her office. I will go there again tomorrow. Aunt Farah and Miss Destri will treat me a mini tour to the whole building and buy me a lunch in the office cafe. It will be so much fun.

24. contoh kalimat active dan passive "simple present tense, simple past tense, simple future tense, simple present continous, simple past continous tense, simple present perfect tense, simple past perfect tense"dengaan kalimat aktive : dia membaca buku di librarykalimat passive : buku di baca olehnya diperpustakaandalam bahasa inggris yamohon bantuannya​


Jawaban:

Kalimat Active :

1. Simple Present : He reads the book in library.

2. Simple past : He read the book in library.

3. Simple future : He will read the book in library.

4. Present Continuous : He is reading the book in library.

5. Past Continuous : He was reading the book in library.

6. Present Perfect : He has read the book in library.

7. Past Perfect : He had read the book in library.

Kalimat Passive :

1. Simple Present : The book is read by him in library.

2. Simple past : The book was read by him in library.

3. Simple future : The book will be read by him in library.

4. Present continuous : The book is being read by him in library.

5. Past continuous : The book was being read by him in library

Explanation:

- Simple future tenseThis tense is used to tell about an action that will be happened in the future.The structure: S + will + V1 + O
For example, I will go to Bali next month.

- Simple past future tense
This tense is used to indicate an action which would be happened in the past. This tense is mostly used to show wish or hope.
The structure: S + would + V1 + O
For example, I wish he would forgive me.

- Present continuous tenseThis tense is used to tell about something that is in progress right now.
The structure: S + to be (am / is / are) + V-ing + O
For example, She is reading a book in the library.

non verb: s+ have/ has + Been + adjective/adverb
