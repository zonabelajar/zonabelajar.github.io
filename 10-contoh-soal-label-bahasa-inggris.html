20. buatlah 10 soal berserta jawabannya mengggunakan bahasa inggris tentang label sari gandum.​


Jawaban:

Tentu, berikut ini adalah 10 pertanyaan beserta jawabannya tentang label sari gandum:

1. What is the main ingredient in wheatgrass juice?

a. Barley

b. Oats

c. Wheat

d. Rice

Answer: c. Wheat

2. What is the primary benefit of wheatgrass juice?

a. Improved digestion

b. Boosted energy levels

c. Enhanced immune function

d. All of the above

Answer: d. All of the above

3. Which nutrients are commonly found in wheatgrass juice?

a. Vitamin C and iron

b. Calcium and potassium

c. Vitamin A and folic acid

d. Vitamin B12 and zinc

Answer: c. Vitamin A and folic acid

4. What is the recommended daily serving size of wheatgrass juice?

a. 1 ounce

b. 4 ounces

c. 8 ounces

d. 16 ounces

Answer: b. 4 ounces

5. How should wheatgrass juice be stored?

a. In the refrigerator

b. In the freezer

c. At room temperature

d. It doesn't need to be stored

Answer: a. In the refrigerator

6. Is wheatgrass juice suitable for individuals with gluten intolerance?

a. Yes, it is gluten-free

b. No, it contains gluten

c. It depends on the brand

d. It is unclear

Answer: b. No, it contains gluten

7. What is the best time to consume wheatgrass juice?

a. In the morning on an empty stomach

b. Before meals

c. After meals

d. Any time of the day is fine

Answer: a. In the morning on an empty stomach

8. Can wheatgrass juice be mixed with other juices or smoothies?

a. Yes, it can be mixed with any juice or smoothie

b. No, it should be consumed separately

c. It depends on personal preference

d. It is not recommended

Answer: c. It depends on personal preference

9. What is the shelf life of wheatgrass juice?

a. 1 day

b. 3 days

c. 1 week

d. 2 weeks

Answer: b. 3 days

10. Is there any potential side effects of consuming wheatgrass juice?

a. Yes, it can cause nausea and headaches in some individuals

b. No, it is completely safe for everyone

c. It depends on the individual's health condition

d. It is not known

Answer: a. Yes, it can cause nausea and headaches in some individuals
