32. what do we do when we get home?JAWAB PAKAI BAHASA INGGRIS!!​


Jawaban:

Will and might

Both “will” and “might” are modal auxiliary verbs. This means that they are followed by the infinitive of the verb without “to”:

“It will rain later.” (Not “it will to rain…”)

“It might rain later.”

(For more information on how to use modal auxiliary verbs, see our page on can.)

We use “will” when we are sure that something will happen.

“It will be sunny later.” (100% probability)

We use “might” when something is less sure.

“It might rain later. Take an umbrella with you.” (50% probability)

The negative forms are:

“It won’t…”

“It won’t snow until December.”

“It might not…”

“It might not be sunny at the beach.”

Going to

We can use the verb “going to” (in the Present continuous tense) to talk about things that will happen because we can see evidence now.


33. Buatlah 5 soal tentang we know what to do?


5 contoh soal tentang we know what to do adalah

We have a lot of work tomorrow. You _______ be late.We ______ rush. We have plenty of time.You _______ smoke inside the school.You _______ eat too much cake. You will get fat.Children ______ tell lies. It is very naughty.

Kunci Jawaban:

mustn'tdon't have tomustn'tshouldn'tmustn'tPembahasan

Pada chapter 3 Bahasa Inggris kelas 8 SMP dibahas tentang We Know What to Do. Pada chapter ini dibagi menjadi dua materi yaitu Suggestion dan Obligation.

Suggestion adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menyatakan sebuah saran kepada orang lain. Biasanya dalam menyatakan suggestion, kita menggunakan kata Should.

Pola dalam suggestion menggunakan should adalah:

S + Should + V1

S + Should be + Adjective/ noun/ adverb

Contoh:

You should go to school now.She should be calm to resolve this problem.You should be a police.She should end this game as soon as possible.

Obligation adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menyatakan sebuah kewajiban. Kewajiban adalah sesuatu yang harus dikerjakan. Bila dilanggar, si pelaku akan mendapatkan konsekuensi. Dalam menyatakan kewajiban atau obligation, kita biasanya menggunakan kata Must.

Pola dalam Obligation menggunakan Must adalah:

S + Must + V1

S + Must be + Adjective/ noun/ adverb

Terkadang kita bisa menggunakan "have to" untuk menggantikan Must:

S + have to + V1

Contoh:

Students must wear scout uniform every Friday.They must be crazy because of Reno.Shinta must meet me at 6 pm.Rayhan must be angry.I have to go now.
