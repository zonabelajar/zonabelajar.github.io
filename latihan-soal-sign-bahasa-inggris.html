2. Pengertian sign dalam bahasa inggris~


Answer:


In English, Sign has two functions, as a verb and as a noun.

As a verb, sign means to write your name or signature on a letter or printed document.

For example, I sign the peace agreement.

As a verb, sign also means to give an order or a request to someone by using body language.

For example, The boss signs to the waiter to bring him a cup of coffee.

 

As a noun, sign means a gesture of the body to give an information.

For example, crying is a sign of sadness

As a noun, sign also means a mark that has a specific meaning.

For example, the stop sign means the drivers has to stop and not to continue their journey to the road that has this sign.

22. Buatlah cerpen dalam bahasa inggris yang di dalam cerita tersebut ada kata sometimes,sometime,stay(verb),stay(noun),sign and signature! ​


Jawaban:

Title: The Signature of Time

Once upon a time, in a small town nestled amidst rolling hills, there lived a young girl named Lily. Sometimes, life seemed mundane and predictable in this peaceful town, but Lily always found solace in her vivid imagination.

Lily's days were filled with adventures, exploring the enchanting forests that surrounded her home. Sometimes, she would venture deep into the woods, losing track of time as she marveled at the beauty of nature. Sometime during her expeditions, she stumbled upon a peculiar old cottage that seemed frozen in time.

Intrigued by the mysterious aura of the cottage, Lily decided to stay a while and investigate. As she stepped inside, she noticed faded photographs hanging on the walls, capturing moments of joy and sorrow. A sense of history seemed to linger in the air, whispering stories of the past.

In one corner of the cottage, there was a desk covered in dust. Curiosity piqued, Lily brushed away the layers of neglect to reveal an old journal. She leafed through its yellowed pages, discovering tales of love, loss, and the passage of time. Amidst the writings, she found a sign—a small drawing of an hourglass—an emblem of the significance of time.

Inspired by the journal, Lily decided to create her own mark on the world. She began writing stories and poems, pouring her heart onto the pages. Each piece she penned became a signature of her dreams and aspirations. Through her words, she hoped to leave a lasting impact on the world around her.

Days turned into weeks, and weeks into months, as Lily continued to stay devoted to her craft. Her stories began to resonate with people far and wide, spreading joy and inspiration. The young girl's signature style became recognized, and she found herself embraced by a supportive community of fellow writers.

One day, as Lily was taking a stroll through town, she noticed a poster advertising a writing competition. Without hesitation, she signed her name on the registration form, eager to showcase her talent. The competition was fierce, but Lily's unique voice shone through her carefully crafted words.

Months later, a letter arrived, bearing the news that Lily had won the competition. Her heart fluttered with excitement as she realized that her signature had now become a symbol of success and recognition. The young girl's dreams had transformed into reality, all because she dared to stay true to herself and embrace the power of words.

From that moment on, Lily continued to write stories that touched people's hearts. She understood that sometimes, it's the simplest of words that carry the greatest significance. The signature she left behind in her stories became a testament to the beauty of storytelling and the timeless power of the written word.

And so, in this quaint town, the young girl named Lily taught everyone the importance of staying true to oneself, leaving their own unique signature on the world, and cherishing the ever-changing beauty of time.

The End.
