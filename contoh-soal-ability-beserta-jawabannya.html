14. Contoh dialog ability dan anibility


Here is the dialogue which include those two grammar functions:

Said: Hey dude, I wonder if you could help me, I need a favour.

Vaclav: Sure, hopefully I can help.

S: I have been studying Russian and I need constant partner to practice it. Can you find any private teacher who's available every weekend? I know you can't do it on weekends.

V: Hmm, let me see. Oh, what about my sister?

S: Will she be able to teach on weekends?

V: Yeah, she needs more income during this pandemic, so she doesn't mind.

S: OK, thanks, can she start soon?

V: Hmm not this weekend, she won't be able to begin that soon as she has already had a doctor appoitment.

S: sure no problem.

Penjelasan:

Here are several ways of saying the ability and inability.

ability:

can (natural skill: she can sing)will be able to (future skill: she will be able to see you tomorrow)was able to (past ability with some effort: they were able to win despite his goal keeper was injured)

inability:

can't (natural inability)won't be able to (future inability)wasn't able to (past inability even with some efforts)

22. Contoh kalimat ability +,-,? Positif negatif tolong dijawab


Ability/Disability expressions adalah ungkapan yang menyatakan bahwa kita dapat/tidak dapat melakukan sesuatu.

Contoh kalimat ability:

(+) My sister can swim very well

(-) My sister cannot swim very well

(?) Can my sister swim very well?

(+) He can speak English

(-) He cannot speak English

(?) Can he speak English?

(+) She is able to ride the bicycle

(-) She is not able to ride the bicycle

(?) Is she able to ride the bicycle?

Pembahasan

Formula ungkapan ability adalah:

Subject + can + verb 1 : They can read fastSubject + cannot + verb 1 : They can't read fastCan + Subject + verb 1 ? : Can they read fast?Subject + be able to + verb 1 : They are able to read fastSubject + not be able to + verb 1 : They are not able to read fastBe able to + Subject + verb 1? : Are they able to read fast? 28. pengertian ability dan contoh kalimatnya


Expressing ability is an expressing used when a person had the ability to do something. We usually use can, could, and be able to.

Kemampuan mengekspresikan adalah ekspresi yang digunakan ketika seseorang memiliki kemampuan untuk melakukan sesuatu. Kita biasanya menggunakan can, could, dan be able to.

Pembahasan

Rumus yang digunakan untuk expressing ability adalah

subject + can/could/be able to + main verb

Ada perbedaan can dan could. Can digunakan untuk present, sedangkan could digunakan untuk past.

Contoh kalimat:

Can

I can play baduk.She can write the message in Korean.We can sing an English song in your birthday.They can come on time the meeting.He can return the book to the library for me

Could

When she was in Bali, she could speak Balinese.We could help you with the housework before she went to school.They could make fried rice after we came home.He could go up the stairs himself when he was only two years old.I could speak English when I studied at the college.

Be able to

He is able to play the piano. (present)He was able to play the piano when he was a boy. (past)Pelajari lebih lanjut

33. Tuliskan 10 pertanyaan berdasarkan 'Showing Ability and Willingness' beserta jawabannya !​


Berikut adalah beberapa frase dan contoh kalimat yang bisa digunakan untuk mengungkapkan atau mengekspresikan kemampuan (ability) dan kemungkinan (possibility).

A.        Ability (Kemampuan)

1.         Can (Dapat/ Bisa)

Contoh Kalimat: Can you help me? (Artinya: Dapatkah kamu menolongku?) I can swim well. (Artinya: Saya bisa berenang dengan baik) She can sleep well. (Artinya: Dia dapat tidur nyenyak) Etc.

2.         Be able to + Verb (Dapat/ mampu)

Contoh Kalimat: I must be able to do the assignment. (Artinya: Saya harus dapat mengerjakan tugas tersebut) She is able to speak English fluently. (Artinya: Dia mampu berbicara Bahasa Inggris dengan lancar) Etc.

3.         Managed, Succeeded (Berhasil)

Contoh Kalimat: He managed to get much money. (Artinya: Dia berhasil mendapatkan banyak uang) I succeed in a job. (Artinya: Saya berhasil dalam pekerjaan) Etc.

4.         Could (Dapat)

Contoh Kalimat: I could pass the examination. (Artinya: Saya dapat lulus ujian) He could play tennis well. (Artinya: Dia dapat bermain tenis dengan baik) She could fix this machine. (Artinya: Dia dapat membenahi mesin ini) Etc.

B.        Possibility (Kemungkinan)

1.         Menggunakan Adjectives: Probable, likely, possible. (Mungkin)

Contoh Kalimat: It is probable that tomorrow I’ll visit my Aunty. (Artinya: Mungkin saya besok akan mengunjungi bibiku) It’s likely that she will go with him. (Artinya: Mungkin dia kan pergi dengannya) It’s possible, he loves you. (Artinya: Mungkin dia mencintaimu) Etc.

2.         Menggunakan Adverbs: Probably, possibly. (Mungkin)

Contoh Kalimat: Probably, I will accept him again. (Artinya: Mungkin saya akan menerimnya kembali) Possibly, she has another man. (Artinya: Mungkin dia mempunyai pria lain) Etc.

3.         Menggunakan Nouns: Probability, Likelihood, possibility. (Kemungkinan)

Contoh Kalimat: There is probability that my father will go now. (Artinya: Ada kemungkinan bahwa ayahku akan pergi sekarang) It’s likelihood that he fall in love. (Artinya: Kemungkinan, dia jatuh cinta) There is possibility that I will marry her. (Artinya: Ada kemungkinan saya akan menikahinya) Etc.

4.         Perhaps, Maybe (Mungkin)

Contoh Kalimat: Perhaps, he wants you to go to the party. (Artinya: Mungkin dia ingin kamu pergi ke pesta) Maybe, I’ll remember him again. (Artinya: Mungkin saya kan mengingatnya lagi) Etc.

5.         May/ be going to + Verb (Mungkin/ akan)

Contoh Kalimat: His love may or may not be true. (Artinya: Cintanya mungkin benar atau tidak) I am going to go to America next year. (Artinya: Saya akan pergi ke Amerika tahun depan)

Adanya Kemudahan Transfer atau Pemindahan dalam Ruang (Spatial Transfer Ability)
Faktor yang juga memengaruhi kekuatan interaksi adalah kemudahan pemindahan manusia, barang, jasa, gagasan, dan informasi antara satu wilayah dan wilayah lainnya.

Kemudahan pergerakan antar wilayah ini sangat berkaitan dengan:

1) jarak antarwilayah, baik jarak mutlak maupun relatif;
2) biaya transportasi;
3) kemudahan dan kelancaran prasarana dan sarana transportasi antarwilayah.
