23. buatlah factual report tentang buah semangka... buatlah dengan bahasa Inggris ​


Title: Watermelon - A Factual Report

Introduction:

Watermelon, scientifically known as Citrullus lanatus, is a refreshing and delicious fruit that is highly popular worldwide. It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family and is characterized by its juicy flesh and vibrant red color. This report aims to provide factual information about watermelon, including its origin, nutritional composition, health benefits, and cultivation.

1. Origin and Distribution:

Watermelon is believed to have originated in the Kalahari Desert of Africa. It has been cultivated for thousands of years, with evidence of its consumption dating back to ancient Egypt. Today, watermelon is grown in various regions around the world, including the United States, China, Russia, India, and Brazil.

2. Nutritional Composition:

Watermelon is an excellent source of hydration as it contains about 92% water. It is also low in calories, making it a healthy choice for those watching their weight. Additionally, watermelon is rich in several essential nutrients, including:

- Vitamins: It is a good source of vitamin C and provides smaller amounts of vitamins A, B6, and K.

- Minerals: Watermelon contains minerals such as potassium and magnesium.

- Antioxidants: It is rich in lycopene, a powerful antioxidant associated with numerous health benefits.

3. Health Benefits:

Consuming watermelon offers several health benefits, including:

- Hydration: Due to its high water content, watermelon helps maintain proper hydration levels in the body.- Heart Health: The presence of lycopene and other antioxidants in watermelon is associated with a reduced risk of heart disease.- Eye Health: The fruit contains beta-carotene, which is beneficial for eye health.- Digestive Health: Watermelon contains dietary fiber, which aids in digestion and promotes a healthy digestive system.- Skin Health: The vitamins and antioxidants in watermelon contribute to healthy skin by promoting collagen synthesis and protecting against sun damage.4. Cultivation:

Watermelon thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil with ample sunlight. It is usually grown from seeds, either directly sown in the field or started indoors and transplanted. The plant requires regular watering and protection against pests and diseases. Harvesting typically takes place when the fruit reaches its peak ripeness, as determined by the sound when tapped, color, and texture.

Conclusion:

Watermelon is a delicious and nutritious fruit that provides numerous health benefits. With its high water content, essential nutrients, and antioxidants, it is an excellent choice for staying hydrated and supporting overall well-being. Whether enjoyed on its own or in various culinary preparations, watermelon continues to be a popular and refreshing fruit enjoyed by people across the globe.

Dengan kandungan air yang tinggi, nutrisi penting, dan antioksidan, semangka menjadi pilihan yang sangat baik untuk menjaga hidrasi dan mendukung kesejahteraan secara keseluruhan. 30. bahasa inggris kelas XI tenses​


A. PAST PERFECT TENSE

Past Perfect Tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan suatu aksi (action) sudah selesai dilakukan pada masa lampau di waktu tertentu sebelum melakukan aksi berikutnya.

Past Perfect Tense juga dapat digunakan membentuk conditional sentence type 3 dan reported speech.

Past Perfect Tense menggunakan auxiliary verb Had + Past Participle (V3) dalam kalimatnya. Baik Singular Subject dan Plural Subject menggunakan bentuk yang sama :

(+) S + Had + V3 + O

(-) S + Had Not + V3 + O

(?) Had + S + V3 + O + ?

Examples :

- I had locked the door before I left my room

(Saya sudah mengunci pintu sebelum saya meninggalkan ruangan saya)

- If they had gone to your house that night, they would have met your parents

(Jika seandainya mereka pergi ke rumahmu malam itu, mereka pasti sudah bertemu orang tuamu)

- Ani said that she had already done her homework

(Ani berkata bahwa dia sudah mengerjakan pekerjaan rumahnya)

- The train hadn't left when you arrived

(Kereta apinya belum berangkat ketika kamu tiba)

- Had Mrs. Laila been a teacher when she got married?

(Apakah Bu Laila sudah menjadi seorang guru ketika dia menikah?)

B. PRESENT PERFECT TENSE

Present Perfect Tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu aksi sudah dimulai sejak masa lampau dalam waktu yang tidak spesifik, tapi aksi tersebut sudah selesai dilakukan di masa lampau pada waktu tertentu, tapi hasilnya masih dirasakan di masa sekarang; ataupun aksi tersebut belum selesai dilakukan, bahkan masih dilakukan hingga masa sekarang.

Keterangan waktu yang biasanya digunakan dalam Present Perfect Tense, antara lain :

- just

- already

- since

- for

- this week/month/year

- this morning/afternoon/evening

- yet

- never/ever

Present Perfect Tense menggunakan auxiliary verb Have/Has + Past Participle (V3) dalam kalimatnya.

Singular Subject (He, She, It) dan Singular Noun (kata benda tunggal) menggunakan Has

Plural Subject (They, I, We, You) dan Plural Noun (kata benda jamak) menggunakan Have

(+) S + Have/Has + V3 + O

(-) S + Have/Has Not + V3 + O

(?) Have/Has + S + V3 + O + ?

Examples :

- She has worked in the hospital since 2000

(Dia sudah bekerja di rumah sakot sejak tahun 2000)

- Mr. Agus and Mr. Budi have been teachers in this school for 20 years

(Pak Agus dan Pak Budi sudah menjadi guru di sekolah ini selama 20 tahun)

- We haven't finished our homework yet

(Kami belum menyelesaikan pekerjaan rumah kami)

- Have you ever seen this movie before?

(Apakah kamu pernah melihat film ini sebelumnya?)

- Lucia has sent the email this morning

(Lucia sudah mengirimkan emailnya pagi ini)

C. FUTURE PERFECT TENSE

Future Perfect Tense adalah tense yang menyatakan sebuah aksi akan diselesaikan di masa yang akan datang. Seseorang yang mengatakan sebuah kalimat dalam bentuk Future Perfect Tense memposisikan dirinya di masa sekarang, dan melihat sebuah aksi diselesaikan di masa yang akan datang.

Keterangan waktu yang biasanya digunakan dalam Future Present Tense, antara lain :

- until

- before/by the end of the day

- by the end of the week/month

- by next week/month/year

- by ...

- in a week/day/month/year

- after/before

Future Perfect Tense menggunakan simple future will have + past participle (V3) dalam kalimatnya. Baik Singular Subject maupun Plural Subject menggunakan bentuk yang sama :

(+) S + Will Have + V3 + O

(-) S + Will Not Have + V3 + O

(?) Will + S + Have + V3 + O + ?

Examples :

- My parents will have been at Singapore until Sunday

(Orangtua saya akan sudah berada di Singapura sanpai hari Minggu)

- He will have arrived home at 4 pm

(Dia akan sudah tiba di rumah pukul 4 sore)

- You won't have finished the task by midnight

(Kamu tidak akan sudah menyelesaikan tugasnya sampai tengah malam)

- Will we have received the package by Tuesday?

(Akankah kami menerima paketnya hari Selasa?)

- Will the shop have closed by 9 pm?

(Akankah tokonya tutup pada pukul 9?)

39. soal bahasa inggris dan jawabannya smk kelas 10


Berikut ini adalah contoh soal Bahasa Inggris untuk SMK Kelas 10. Jawaban yang benar adalah jawaban yang digarisbawahi.

Perhatikan teks berikut!

Dear Sally,

Last holiday, my friends and I visited Ponorogo. Ponorogo is a small city in East Java province, but it is very natural and beautiful. We spent the night at a villa in Jetis. On the first day, we visited Prigi beach. We watched the amazing views of the sea and the beautiful white waves. We grilled fish and had something called “Nasi Pecel” for lunch. On the second day, we visited “Dongko” mountain resort. There were a lot of plants, especially clove trees. I love this city because the people are very friendly and the prices are cheap.

I’II send some pictures for you.

Love,

Anna

1. What kind of text is the text above?

A. Procedure text
B. Explanation text
C. Descriptive text
D. Narrative text

2. Why is the writer loves Ponorogo?

A. The people are rude
B. The prices are expensive
C. The food is tasty
D. The sea view is amazing

3. What does the text mainly tells the reader?

A. The "Dongko" mountain resort
B. The trip to Ponorogo
C. How to grill fish
D. The Prigi southern beach

Pembahasan

Descriptive text merupakan jenis teks dalam bahasa Inggris yang isinya bertujuan untuk menjelaskan sesuatu. Pada umumnya, descriptive text memiliki struktur yang terdiri dari identifikasi (gambaran umum) dan deskripsi (ciri-ciri khusus). Meskipun begitu, pada soal, teks deskriptif yang terdapat di atas ditulis dalam bentuk surat, sehingga tidak mengandung struktur khusus. Ada pun teks di atas mendeskripsikan sebuah pengalaman berkunjung seseorang yang bernama Anna ke Ponorogo.

Descriptive text memiliki ciri-ciri sebagai berikut:

Menggunakan attribute verb seperti "be".Menggunakan simple present tense.
