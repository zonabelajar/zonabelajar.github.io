22. jawabn ny ap mhon dbntu​


× Nilai semangka (s)

s + s + s = 9

3s = 9

s = 9÷3

s = 3

× Nilai nanas (n)

s + n + n = 15

3 + 2n = 15

2n = 15 - 3

2n = 12

n = 12÷2

n = 6

× Nilai pisang (p)

n - p = 12

6 - p = 12

-p = 12 - 6

-p = 6

p = -6

× Nilai semangka+nanas+pisang

= 3 + 6 - 6

= 3

Nilai 6 ada 3 maka 6 × 3 = 18

Nilai 7 ada 4 maka 7 × 4 = 28

Nilai 8 ada 5 maka 8 × 5 = 40

Nilai 9 ada 2 maka 9 × 2 = 18

Nilai 10 ada 4 maka 10 × 4 = 40

Total nilai data = 10+18+28+40+18+40 = 154 Total data = 2 + 3 + 4 + 5 + 2 + 4 = 20

Ok kita jawab soalnya

> Mean

Mean = Total nilai data/Total data

Mean = 154/20

Mean = 7,7

.

>Modus

Modus = Data terbanyak

Modus = 8

.

Jawaban Benar = 31 soal

Skor benar = 31 x 4 = 124

Jawaban salah = 36 - 31 = 5 soal

Skor salah = 5 x (-2) = (-10)

Skor tidak menjawab = 4 x (-1) = (-4)

Total Skor = 124 + (-10) + (-4) = 110
