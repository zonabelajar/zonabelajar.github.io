22. Jelaskan Tentang Simple present tense dalam bahasa indonesia dan bahasa Inggris! ​


Jawaban:

Simple present tense adalah tenses yang digunakan ketika suatu kejadian sedang berlangsung saat ini atau kejadian yang berlangsung berulang kali (kebiasaan). Tenses ini merupakan tenses yang paling sering digunakan dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Simple present tense is a tense that is used when an event is currently taking place or an event that takes place repeatedly (habit). This tense is the most tense tense often used in English

Simple Present Tense is a verb tense to tell an action happens regularly, or to describe habits and general truth.

(Simple Present Tense adalah kata kerja untuk memberitahu suatu tindakan terjadi secara teratur, atau untuk menggambarkan kebiasaan dan kebenaran umum).

PEMBAHASAN

The Formula of Simple Present Tense (Rumus Simple Present Tense)

(1) Verbal Sentence (Kalimat Verbal)

(+) S + V1 (-s/es) + Complement(-) S + do/does + not + V1 + Complement(?) Do/does + S + V1 + Complement?

➡ For Example (Contohnya)

(+) I drink milk every morning. (Saya minum susu setiap pagi)(-) I do not drink milk every morning. (Saya tidak minum susu setiap pagi.(?) Do I drink milk every morning? (Apalah saya minum susu setiap pagi?)

➡ Three sentences above show habits, we can know from adverb of time "every morning."

(Tiga kalimat di atas menunjukkan kebiasaan, kita bisa mengetahuinya dari kata keterangan waktu "setiap pagi").

(2) Nominal Sentence (Kalimat Nomina)

(+) S + Be (is/am/are) + Complement (-) S + Be (is/am/are) + not + V1(?) Be (is/am/are) + S + Complement?

➡ For Example (Contohnya)

(+) You are a student. (Kamu adalah seorang murid).(-) You are not a student. (Kamu bukan seorang murid).(?) are you a student. (Apakah kamu adalah seorang murid?)

➡ Three sentences above show about general truth because you are a student in real life.

(Tiga kalimat di atas menunjukkan kebenaran umum karena kamu adalah seorang murid dalam kehidupan nyata).

Note! (Catatan)

Adverb of time (keterangan waktu)

Everyday (setiap hari) Every morning (setiap pagi)Often (sering)Usually (biasanya)Always (selalu)etc. (dll.)

33. Cerita bahasa inggris dengan kata kerja simple present, simple past, present continous


Recount Text

Recount Text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk menceritakan pengalaman atau kejadian yang dialami di masa lampau.  

Generic Structure dari Recount text adalah:

1. Orientation: Berisi pengenalan tokoh, tempat, waktu dan tujuan cerita.

2. Events: Berisi hal-hal yang terjadi pada waktu itu (lampau).

3. Re-orientation: Berisi kesimpulan cerita atau kesan dari penulis/ tokoh terhadap pengalaman/ ceritanya.

Language features dari Recount Text:

1. Menggunakan Past tense

2. Menggunakan action verb

3. Menggunakan thinking verb.

Pembahasan

Cerita bahasa inggris dengan kata kerja simple present, simple past, present continous adalah:

This is my experience in middle school, exactly 3 years ago when I was in 7th grade. Every year, our school held scout activities which must be followed by all 7th grade students and at the end of each semester there were camp activities held at the school.

When the D-day arrived, before the activity began the opening ceremony was held to get information and direction during the camp activities. After that, my team mate and I immediately set up a tent that was assisted by a coach. After the tent was established, we immediately arranged the equipment we had brought. Shortly, the whistle rang, I quickly put on a tie and other scout gear because there would be a game between teams to train the cohesiveness and togetherness of each team. Then there was heavy rain. Considering that all the equipment was in the tent, the school finally decided that all students would take refuge in the hall, let the conditions where it was not possible to come in because there were several tents which had collapsed by the wind, and the field were very muddy. Arriving at the hall, we immediately rushed to the bathroom to change our clothes.  

After each team gathered according to each class, everyone got a box of rice which could be enjoyed together. After dinner, the rain stopped. We went straight to the courtyard to enjoy the campfire and watch the performances of each team, in the form of songs and poems. After the campfire event was over, the school announced that the female students in that night must sleep in the meeting room, while the men remained in the hall. Finally we were moved again. Simple present tense is a basic grammar which used to state facts which don't change, or events that happen routinely (repeated actions). Simple present tense devided into two kind of sentences, verbal and nominal. Each sentence has three forms (positive, negative, and interrogative)

Simple present tense adalah grammar dasar yang digunakan untuk menyatakan fakta yang tidak berubah, atau peristiwa yang rutin terjadi (aksi yang berulang).
