31. Buatlah percakapan dalam bahasa inggris tentang:-in front ( didepan) -behind ( dibelakang) -on the ( diatas) -under ( dibawah) -around ( disekitar) -out side/out of ( diluar) -in side/in ( didalam) -near ( didekat) -beside ( disamping) -between ( diantara) ( Minimal 10 kalimat percakapan dalam satu kata) Tolong dijawab ya yang bisa besok mau dikumpul ^_^


Preposition of place adalah kata depan yang menunjukan  posisi atau tempat dari suatu benda.


Pembahasan  

Presposition of place adalah kata depan yang menunjukan posisi atau tempat dari suatu benda.


Preposition of place yang umumnya digunakan adalah:

Outside berarti di luarOn berarti di atasAbove berarti di atasIn berarti di dalamAt berarti pada/ di sekitar (digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu tempat yang spesifik)Under berarti di bawahBelow berarti di bawahIn front of berarti di depanBeside berarti di sampingNext to berarti di sebelahNear berarti di dekatBehind berarti di belakangAcross from berarti di seberangOpposite from berarti berlawanan denganThrough berarti melewatiBetween berarti di antaraAround berarti di sekitar

Contoh kalimat yang menggunakan preposition of place:


My school is beside the police office.The bank is in front of the market.The cat is under the table.The ball is on the table.The fish is in the aquarium.There are some jasmine flowers beside my house.I go to school trough the high way.The ball is outside the field.My house is between a small bridge and a big stone.My school is around the City Square.

34. buatlah sebuah kalimat bahasa inggris menggunakan kata (under, beside, near, between at behind, in front of)

PEMBAHASAN
=================
Contoh kalimat dari (under, beside, near, between, at, behind, in front of) adalah..

UNDER = dibawah
The cat is UNDER the chair.
=> kucing berada di bawah kursi.

BESIDE = disamping
Maya sits BESIDE me.
=> Maya duduk disamping saya.

NEAR = Dekat
My school is NEAR from my house.
=> Sekolah saya dekat dari rumah saya.

BETWEEN (di antara)
Our school is BETWEEN the post office and the Police office.
=> Sekolah kami berada di antara kantor pos dan kantor polisi.

AT (di)
They are AT the library
=> Mereka berada di perpustakaan

BEHIND (dibelakang)
Garden is BEHIND our house.
=> Kebun berada di belakang rumah kami.

IN FRONT OF (di depan)
The teacher is IN FRONT OF the class.
=> Guru berada di depan kelas.

37. apa arti on in under beside behind next to in frent of acress between near


on = diluar/diatas (your phone is on the table)
in = didalam (My book is in my bag)
under = dibawah (i have this under control)
beside = disamping (i was beside you)
behind = dibelakang (there was someone behind your back)
next = sebelah (there's a flower shop next to the cafe)
to = untuk (i want to give a present to her
in front = didepan (there's a dog in front of me)
across = seberang (there's a garden across the river)
between = antara (this is a secret between me and you)
near = dekat (there's a swimming pool near my house)
