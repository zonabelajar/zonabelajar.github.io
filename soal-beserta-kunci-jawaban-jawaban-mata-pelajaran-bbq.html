Penjelasan:

Active Voice (kata kerja aktif) berarti menerangkan sebuah pekerjaan. Contoh menyapu, membaca, dll. Dalam bahasa Inggris active voice dapat diketahui dengan adanya V1/V2/Ving/(have/has/had+V3)

Contoh:

Marry eats fruits today. (S + V1)Marrry ate fruit yesterday. (S + V2)Marry is eating banana now. (S + Ving)Marry has just eatten banana. (S + has + V3)

Passive voice (kata kerja pasif) berarti suatu objek (O) diterangkan oleh suatu pekerjaan. Contoh buku dibaca oleh Rudi.

Dalam bahasa Inggris passive voice dapat diketahui dengan rumus:

S + to be + V3 --- to be (is/am/are/was/were)

S + to be + being + V3

S + has/have/had + been + V3

Contoh :

This book is read by Tia. (S + to be + V3)This book is being read by Tia. (S + to be + being + V3)This book has been read ny Tia. (S + has + been + V3)

Jawaban:

Berikut adalah identifikasi orientasi, kronologi, dan urutan kata keterangan dari teks tersebut:

Orientasi: "Yesterday, my family and I went to the National Zoo and Aquarium to visit the new Snow Cubs and the other animals."

Kronologi (Urutan peristiwa):

1. "In the morning, when we got to the Zoo and Aquarium there was a great big line, so we had to wait awhile to get in."

2. "After we entered the zoo, we went straight to the enclosure for the Snow Cubs."

3. "At lunchtime Dad decided to cook a BBQ."

4. "In the afternoon, we visited the aquarium."

5. "At the end of the day when we left we were going to go and get ice cream but we decided we were too tired so we drove straight home."

Kata keterangan (sequence of adverbs):

- "Yesterday" (kemarin) memberikan informasi tentang waktu.

- "In the morning" (di pagi hari) memberikan informasi tentang waktu.

- "After" (setelah) mengindikasikan urutan waktu antara kunjungan ke kebun binatang dan kunjungan ke enclosure Snow Cubs.

- "At lunchtime" (saat makan siang) menunjukkan waktu kejadian saat makan siang.

- "In the afternoon" (di sore hari) memberi tahu waktu kunjungan ke akuarium.

- "At the end of the day" (pada akhir hari) menandakan waktu akhir kunjungan.
