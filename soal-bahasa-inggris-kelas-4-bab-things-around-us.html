7. Buatlah sebuah short descriptive text about things around us​


My Bedroom

I have my own bedroom. It is small but very clean. I always tidy up my bedroom before going to school. There are two pillows and a bolster on the bed. There is a blanket on the pillows. On the corner, there is a fan. There is a clock at the the wall. There is not a cupboard in my bedroom, but there is a bookshelf near the fan. I love my bedroom very much. My bedroom is the most comfortable room I've ever slept in.

Pembahasan

Saya mempunyai kamar tidur saya sendiri. Kamar tidur itu kecil tetapi sangat bersih. Saya selalu merapikan tempat tidur saya sebelum pergi ke sekolah. Ada dua buah bantal dan sebuah guling di atas tempat tidur. Ada sebuah selimut di atas bantal-bantal. Di pojok, ada sebuah kipas angin. Ada sebuah jam dinding di dinding. Rivers are also important for transportation, providing a route for boats and barges. They are also important for recreation, such as fishing, boating, and swimming. Television

Television, or TV, is one of humanity’s most important means of communication. It brings pictures and sounds from around the world into millions of homes. People, with a television set in their houses, can sit and watch the president makes a speech or visits a foreign country. They can see a war being fought. Through television, viewers at home can see and learn about people, places, and things in a away lands. Additionally, television brings its viewers a steady stream of programs that are designed to entertain. In fact, TV provides many more entertainment programs than any other kind of information media. The programs include action-packed dramas, light comedies, soap operas, sports events, cartoons, quizzes, variety shows and motion pictures.
