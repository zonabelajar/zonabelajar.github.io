What Is the goals of procedure text?

a. Retell about something

b. Explain the details of something

c. Tell about how to make or how to do something

d. Explain about experience

What is the structure of procedure text?

a. Thesis, orientation, description

b. Purpose, material or ingredient, step or method

c. Definition and description

d. Description, steps, definition

What is the best choice of procedure text title?

a. My experince in Bali

b. How to make pancake

c. My holiday

d. Tangkuban Perahu

Pembahasan:

Teks prosedur adalah sebuah teks yang berisi langkah atau tahapan untuk melakukan suatu hal. Teks prosedur menjelaskan cara membuat atau mengerjakan sesuatu dengan langkah-langkah terstruktur.

Teks prosedur terdiri dari beberapa struktur. Ada empat bagian, yaitu tujuan, alat dan bahan, langkah pengerjaan, dan penutup.  Tujuan teks prosedur bertujuan untuk menjelaskan atau memberitahu cara membuat, mengoperasikan, atau melakukan sesuatu sesuai dengan urutan tindakan atau langkah. Alat dan bahan berisi mengenai bahan dan alat apa saja yang dibutuhkan dalam melakukan proses atau langkah-langkah tersebut. Langkah pengerjaan terdiri dari tahapan-tahapan yang harus dilalui dari awal sampai akhir.  Penutup merujuk kembali ke hal-hal pokok pada langkah-langkah yang telah disajikan, bertujuan untuk memotivasi orang untuk melakukan aktivitas atau kegiatan. Misalnya, selamat menikmati, semoga bermanfaat, dan simpulan hasil kegiatan yang dilakukan.

**Procedure: How to Make a Cup of Tea**

*Introduction:*
Making a cup of tea is a simple and comforting process. Follow these steps to brew a perfect cup of tea.

**Materials Needed:**
1. A tea bag or loose tea leaves
2. A cup or mug
3. Boiling water
4. A teaspoon
5. A saucer (optional)
6. Sugar, honey, or milk (optional)

**Steps:**
1. Boil Water:
- Start by boiling water in a kettle. Make sure it's fresh and cold.

2. Preheat the Cup:
- While the water is boiling, pour a small amount of hot water into your cup to preheat it. Swirl it around and then discard the water.

3. Add the Tea Bag or Tea Leaves:
- If using a tea bag, place it into the cup. If using loose tea leaves, add one teaspoon of tea leaves to the cup.

4. Pour Hot Water:
- Once the water reaches a rolling boil, pour it into the cup over the tea bag or tea leaves.

5. Steep the Tea:
- Let the tea steep for 3-5 minutes. The longer you steep, the stronger the flavor.

6. Remove the Tea Bag or Strain the Tea:
- If you used a tea bag, remove it from the cup. If you used loose tea leaves, strain the tea to remove them.

7. Customize Your Tea (Optional):
- Add sugar, honey, or milk to your tea according to your preference. Stir well.

8. Serve:
- Place a saucer under the cup (if you like), and serve your tea hot.

9. Enjoy:
- Find a cozy spot, sip your tea, and savor the soothing flavor.

**10 Questions and Answers:**

1. What is the first step in making tea?
- Boiling water.

2. Why should you preheat the cup?
- To maintain the tea's temperature.

3. How long should you let the tea steep?
- 3-5 minutes.

4. What should you do after steeping the tea?
- Remove the tea bag or strain the tea.

5. What can you add to customize your tea?
- Sugar, honey, or milk.

6. What do you do if you're using loose tea leaves?
- Add one teaspoon of tea leaves to the cup.

7. What is the purpose of a saucer?
- It's optional but can be used to place the cup on.

8. When should you pour the boiling water over the tea bag or tea leaves?
- After it reaches a rolling boil.

9. What should you do before discarding the hot water used for preheating the cup?
- Swirl it around in the cup.

10. How do you enjoy your cup of tea?
- Find a cozy spot and savor the soothing flavor.

19. contoh soal text procedure beserta jawabannya dgn jelas


Procedure text is a text that explains or helps us how to make or use something. Its communicative purpose is to describe how something is made through a sequence of actions or steps.

Generic structure of procedure text is

The goal (title)The ingredients, stating the materials needed to make somethingThe method, stating the steps to make the somethingPembahasan

34. bantu jawab soal ini1. What is procedure text?2. What is the generic structure of procedure text?3. What is the language features of procedure text?4. Mention kinds of procedure text.​


Jawaban:

1. Procedure text is a type of text that provides instructions or directions on how to perform a specific task or process. It is commonly used to explain a sequence of steps in order to achieve a desired outcome.

2. The generic structure of a procedure text typically consists of three main parts:

a. Goal/aim: This part introduces the purpose or objective of the procedure, explaining what the reader will be able to achieve by following the instructions.

b. Materials/equipment: This part lists the necessary tools, ingredients, or equipment needed to carry out the procedure.

c. Steps: This part presents a series of sequential instructions or steps that need to be followed in order to complete the procedure successfully.

3. The language features commonly found in procedure text include:

a. Imperative verbs: These are used to give direct commands or instructions, such as "mix," "cut," "stir," "boil," etc.

b. Simple present tense: The instructions are usually written in the present tense to indicate that they are general and applicable at any time.

c. Time connectors: Words like "first," "next," "then," "after that," and "finally" are used to indicate the order of the steps.

d. Adverbs and adverbial phrases: These are used to provide additional information about how the actions should be performed, such as "carefully," "gently," "slowly," etc.

e. Technical terms: Procedure texts often include specific vocabulary related to the task or process being described.

4. There are various kinds of procedure texts, including:

a. Recipes: These provide instructions on how to prepare and cook specific dishes.

b. Manuals: These provide instructions on how to operate or assemble a particular device or machine.

c. Instructions for using software or applications: These guide users on how to navigate and utilize specific software or applications.

d. Scientific experiments: These outline the steps to conduct scientific experiments and record observations.

e. DIY (Do-It-Yourself) projects: These provide instructions on how to create or build something, such as crafts or home improvement projects.

f. Guidelines for performing certain tasks: These offer instructions on how to perform tasks like CPR, first aid, or driving a car.
