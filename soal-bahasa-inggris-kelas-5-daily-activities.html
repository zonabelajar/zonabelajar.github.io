Contoh dari Daily Activities - Kata-kata Singkat

1. Mandi = Have a Bath

2. Sarapan = Breakfast

3. Sekolah = School

4. Belajar = Study (Homework)

5. Tidur = Sleep

Contoh dari Daily Activities - Teks

I usually awake at 5 o'clock. After that I have a bath, and prepare my books. I also like to eat my breakfast before I goes to school. In the school I learn maths, and many other lesson. At the end of the school, I come back to my house and do some homework. At night, I prepare my bed, wash my teeth, and goes to sleep.

Penjelasan:

Daily Activities atau Aktivitas Sehari-hari, adalah sebuah aktivitas/kegiatan yang kita dilakukan setiap hari dari pagi sampai malam, bangun sampai tidur. Atau kita sebut juga dengan 'nama' rutinitas sehari-hari/harian (daily routine).

The school finishes at 1 p.m. After school, I change my uniform then have lunch. Then, I go to play at my friend house. At 4 p.m I go back home and have a shower.

Dinner time, I usually help my parents set the table then help to wash dishes after eating. Then I watching TV for one hour. After that, I study and do my homework. At the end, I go to sleep at 9 p.m.
