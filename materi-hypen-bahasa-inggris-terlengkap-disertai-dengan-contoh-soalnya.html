1. seperti apa contoh-contoh noun phrase yang menggunakan hypen?


Penggunaan hyphenated phrase

1.Bilangan-benda-adjective+noun

Ex:Mia has a nine-year-old cousin in Jakarta

2. Noun-adjective+noun

Ex:it's sugar-free beverage

3. Noun-participle+noun

Ex: they Will held a computer-aided testimoni

4. Adjective-participle+noun

Ex: Anto is good looking person
