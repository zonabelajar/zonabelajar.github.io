19. Percakapan Perawat Dan Pasien Dalam Bahasa Inggris​


Jawaban:

Nurse : Perawat

Patient : Pasien

Nurse: Good morning! How are you feeling today?

Patient: Good morning, I'm feeling okay.

Nurse: That's good to hear. I'm here to take your vitals and give you your medications. Have you had any pain or discomfort since we last spoke?

Patient: No, I haven't had any pain.

Nurse: Great. Let me just take your blood pressure and temperature first.

(After taking vitals)

Nurse: Your blood pressure and temperature look good. Now, here are your medications. Do you have any questions about them?

Patient: Yes, actually. I noticed that one of the pills looks different than the others. Is that normal?

Nurse: Oh, let me check. That pill is a new medication that your doctor prescribed. It's okay to take it with your other medications.

Patient: Okay, thank you for explaining that to me.

Nurse: Of course. Is there anything else you need or any other concerns you have?

Patient: No, I think that's it for now.

Nurse: Alright then. Don't hesitate to call us if you need anything. Have a good day!

Patient: You too, thank you.

Semoga Bermanfaat

27. Contoh kasus keperawatan bahasa Inggris


Jawaban:

A 65-year-old male patient is hospitalized with pneumonia. The nurse administers antibiotics, provides supplemental oxygen, and monitors vital signs. The patient shows improvement during the treatment.

Penjelasan:

A 65-year-old male patient was admitted to the hospital with a diagnosis of pneumonia. Upon assessment, the nurse found that the patient was experiencing shortness of breath, coughing with sputum production, and elevated temperature. The nurse implemented interventions such as administering prescribed antibiotics, monitoring vital signs, providing supplemental oxygen, and promoting deep breathing and coughing exercises. The nurse also educated the patient and family members about the importance of medication compliance, respiratory hygiene, and follow-up appointments. Throughout the patient's stay, the nurse closely monitored the response to treatment, documented changes in the patient's condition, and collaborated with the healthcare team to ensure comprehensive care. The patient showed improvement in symptoms, including decreased temperature and improved oxygen saturation, indicating a positive response to treatment.

32. Teks deskripsi tentang perawat dalam bahasa inggris


Jawaban:

Kelas: SMA

Pelajaran: English

Kategori: jobs

Kata kunci: nurse, description

Pembahasan:

A nurse is someone who helps the doctor and takes care of the patients. She has to work in shifts and sometimes has to stand by at night in the hospital. To be a nurse, you need to be good at biology, chemistry and other science subjects and take nursing as your major at university.

artinya:

Perawat adalah seseorang yang berkerja membantu dokter dan merawar para pasien. Mereka harus bekerja dengan sistem shift serta terkadang harus menjada rumah sakit di malam hari. Untuk menjadi perawat, anda harus menguasai biologi, kimia dan ilmu alam lainnya serta kuliah di jurusan keperawatan.


33. Sejarah keperawatan menggunakan bahasa inggris


Jawaban:

The history of nursing in England is related to professional development since the 1850s. The history of nursing itself begins with ancient history, when sick people were treated in temples and places of worship. In the early Christian era, breastfeeding in Britain was carried out by certain women in the Christian Church, their services extended to patients in their homes. These women have no real training by today's standards, but experience teaches them valuable skills, especially in the use of herbal medicines and traditional medicine, and some gain fame as doctors in their day. Remnants of the religious nature of nurses remain in Britain today, mainly by maintaining the position of "Sister" for senior female nurses.
