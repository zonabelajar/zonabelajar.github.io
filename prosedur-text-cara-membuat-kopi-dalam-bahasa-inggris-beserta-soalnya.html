Kopi susu biasanya sering dinikmati ketika sedang bersantai di sore hari, bersantai bersama teman, atau sambil berangkat kerja di pagi hari kita bisa memesan milk coffee sebagai minuman yang menemani macet.

► To make your own coffee milk syrup (cara membuat sirup kopi sendiri): Brew 1/2 cup finely ground coffee with 2 cups cold water in a coffeemaker (masak kopi yang sudah di haluskan dengan 2 gelas air dinign menggunakan mesin pembuat kopi). Combine brewed coffee and 1 cup sugar in a saucepan; simmer until reduced by half, about 30 minutes (campur kopi yang di masak dengan segelas gula di dalm panci). 7. Akhirnya, Secangkir kopi sudah siap disajikan.

Make Instant Iced Coffee

1. Mix 2 teaspoons of instant coffee powder with 120 ml of hot water.

2. Add sugar or spices to the coffee solution if desired.

3. Add 1⁄2 cup cold water or milk to the hot coffee solution.

4. Pour the cold coffee solution over the ice.

5. Immediately serve instant iced coffee.

Chocolate coffee

Ingredients:

1.    1 tablespoon instant coffee
2.    1 tablespoon chocolate powder
3.    450 ml hot milk
4.    2 tablespoons sugar
5.    50 grams ice cubes

How to make:

1.    Prepare a glass or a cup. Put sugar, instant coffee, and chocolate powder into the glass/cup. Stir it.
2.    Add hot milk, and stir it again until mixed well.
3.    After it is cold, add ice cubes.
4.    Finally, chocolate coffee is ready to serve.

39. Buatlah teks prosedur membuat minuman dalam bahasa Inggris( kecuali teh & kopi )​


Tools and materials :

• Glass

• Spoon

• Ice cube shaver

• Unflavored soda

• Cocopandan Syrup (can be replaced with other flavors according to taste)

• Vanilla flavored sweetened condensed milk

• Ice

Steps:

• First, pour enough milk into the glass.

• Second, pour enough syrup into the glass.

• Third, pour unflavored soda until it fills of the glass.

• Fourth, stir gently so that the foam from the soda does not come out of the glass until everything is mixed.

• Fifth, shave the ice with an ice cube shaver.

• Sixth, put the shaved ice cubes into a glass containing a mixture of soda, milk, and syrup earlier.

• Seventh, put a little syrup on the ice

• “Es Soda ” is ready to be served.
