18. Buatlah masing masing 1 kalimat menggunakan Modal Auxiliary “Can, Could, May,and Might” dalam bahasa Indonesia dan di artikan kedalam bahasa Inggris ​


1. Can
You can buy anything with your money but you can’t buy love
(Kamu dapat membeli apapun dengan uangmu tapi kamu tidak dapat membeli cinta)

2. Could
You could run faster than me two years ago
(Kamu dapat berlari lebih cepat dari saya dua tahun lalu)

3. May
He may work out and eat healthy food everday
(Dia mungkim berlatih dan memakan makanan sehat setiap hari)

4. Might
If I have cleaned the room, might I play with my friend?
(Jika aku sudah membersihkan ruangan, bolehkan saya bermain dengan teman saya?)

Maaf kalau salah, semoga membantu:)

19. percakapan bahasa Inggris tentang "school mate cannot get internet connection"​


Jawaban:

A: Hey, have you heard about Sarah? She’s been having trouble connecting to the internet at school.

B: Really? That’s tough. How is she managing without internet access?

A: I think it’s been quite challenging for her. She can’t access online resources for research and assignments.

B: That must be so frustrating. Is there any reason why she can’t connect?

A: I heard from her that there’s an issue with the school’s Wi-Fi network. It’s been unreliable lately.

B: That’s unfortunate. I hope the school can fix it soon so that everyone can have a stable connection.

A: Yeah, it’s affecting her productivity and making it harder for her to keep up with online assignments.

B: Maybe she can try using mobile data instead, if it’s allowed. That way, she can at least access some essential online resources.

A: That’s a good idea. I’ll let her know about it. Hopefully, she can find a temporary solution until the Wi-Fi is fixed.

B: Definitely. It’s important for students to have reliable internet access, especially in this digital age.

A: I completely agree. Let’s hope Sarah gets her internet connection sorted out soon.
