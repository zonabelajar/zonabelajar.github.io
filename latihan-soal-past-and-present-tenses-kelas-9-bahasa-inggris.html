8. pengertian present perfect continous tenses dalam bahasa inggris


The present perfect continuous tense (also known as the present perfect progressivet ense) shows that something started in the past and is continuing at the present time.

The present perfect continuous is formed using the construction:
has/have been + verb base+ -ing.

example: I have been singing for 5 hours.

source: www.grammarly.com

34. perbedaan antara present tense past tense and future tense dalam bahasa inggris​


Jawaban:

if the present tense is used for events that are usually done or can be called 'habits', if past tense is used for activities that have occurred in the past,

while future tense is used for something that has not yet happened but has been planned, so you could say ideals.

Jawaban:

simple present tense

Used to show facts, habits, and general conditions that occur at this time.

formula: S + V / -s, es + complement

The time signal word for this tense is usually, always, every, sometimes, normally, seldom, etc.

Simple Past Tense

Used to indicate an event in the past.

formula: S + V2 + complement

The said time signals for this tense are last year, last month, yesterday, in 1996, last Sunday, etc.

Simple Future Tense

Used to indicate an action or a situation in the future.

formula: S + will / be going to + V1 + complement

The said time signal for this tense is next, tomorrow, five days later, etc.

39. Creating simple past and present perfect tenses​


1.) Simple past:

(+) She bought a new doll two days ago.

(-) She didn't (did not) tell her childhood to me yesterday.

(?) Did She tell her childhood to me yesterday?

2.) Present perfect:

(+) They have done their duty just now

(-) They haven't (have + not) done their duty.

(?) Have they done their duty just now?

Penjelasan:

Simple past: Untuk menyatakan aktifitas yang terjadi di masa lampau.

Auxiliary yang digunakan yaitu: V² dan did.

Present perfect: Untuk menyatakan aktifitas yang sudah lengkap terjadi di masa yang baru saja terjadi.

Auxiliary yang digunakan yaitu: Have/ has dan V³.


40. Pengertian present tenses continuous dalam bahasa Inggris.


Jawaban:

Present continuouse tense is used when we want to describe something that started in the past but still in progress at present time or activity that by now has not yet finished or completed.

Penjelasan:

Present continuous tense digunakan ketika kita ingin menggambarkan sesuatu yang dimulai di masa lalu tetapi masih berlangsung pada saat ini atau kegiatan yang sekarang belum selesai atau tuntas.

Jadi ciri-ciri kalimat dalam present continuous tense adalah bahwa pada saat ini (yaitu pada saat kalimat itu diucapkan), kegiatan itu belum kelar, belum selesai, masih berjalan atau masih berlangsung.
