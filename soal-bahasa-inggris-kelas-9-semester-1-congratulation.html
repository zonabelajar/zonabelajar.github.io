4. Percakapan bahasa inggris congratulation


Congratulating

JAWABAN:  

After a long struggle and hard work, Ammar is finally elected as the leader of students’ association (OSIS) at his school. Many of his friends who support him congratulate him.

Firdaus : That’s wonderful Ammar… (shaking Ammar’s hand).

Ammar : All praise is due to Allah. Thanks.

Ameera : Congratulation! You deserved it guy. Nice work, Ammar.  

Ammar : Thanks a lot. I still need your support.

Ameera   : Insha Allah… we will.

Yusuf : Well done…We must help you (while shaking Ammar’s hand).

Ammar : Thank you.

Farhan  :That’s great. Your parents must be very proud of your achievement (then he shakes Ammar’s hand).

Ammar :Thanks. Nothing will happen without Allah’s help. I’m glad you think so. But I still have to learn a lot.

PEMBAHASAN:

Terjemahannya:

Setelah melalui perjuangan dan kerja keras yang panjang, akhirnya Ammar terpilih menjadi ketua perkumpulan siswa (OSIS) di sekolahnya. Banyak dari teman-temannya yang mendukungnya memberikan selamat kepadanya.

Firdaus: Ammar yang luar biasa… (menjabat tangan Ammar).

Ammar: Segala puji bagi Allah. Terima kasih.

Ameera: Selamat! Kamu pantas mendapatkannya. Kerja bagus, Ammar.

Ammar: Terima kasih banyak. Saya masih membutuhkan dukungan kalian.

Ameera: Insha Allah… kami akan.    

Yusuf: Bagus sekali… Kami pasti membantu mu (sambil menjabat tangan Ammar).

Ammar: Terima kasih.

Farhan: Bagus sekali. Orang tuamu pasti sangat bangga dengan pencapaianmu (kemudian dia menjabat tangan Ammar).

Ammar: Terima kasih. Tidak ada yang akan terjadi tanpa pertolongan Allah. Saya senang kalian berpikir demikian. Tapi saya masih harus belajar banyak.

14. Buatlah Ringkasan Materi Congratulation Kelas 10 ! Dan contoh Gambar nya ! Pakai B. inggris Mapel : Bahasa & Sastra inggris ​


Jawaban:

congratulation

noun

1. The act of expressing joy or acknowledgment, as for the achievement or good fortune of another.

2. often congratulations An expression of such joy or acknowledgment: sent him my congratulations on winning the award.

interjection

congratulations

Used to express such joy or acknowledgment: I heard you got a promotion. Congratulations!

Origin

From Middle French, con- +‎ gratulation, cognate with Latin congratulor (congrātulor, “I congratulate”).

36. Soal pilihan ganda bahasa inggris tentang congratulation


Here are five multiple choice questions about congratulations in English:

1. Which of the following is the correct way to congratulate someone on their wedding?

a) "Congratulations on your divorce!"

b) "Congratulations on your engagement!"

c) "Congratulations on your wedding!"

d) "Congratulations on your new job!"

Answer: c) "Congratulations on your wedding!"

2. Which of the following is the correct way to congratulate someone on their birthday?

a) "Happy condolences on your birthday!"

b) "Happy condolences on your new job!"

c) "Happy birthday to you!"

d) "Congratulations on your birthday!"

Answer: c) "Happy birthday to you!"

3. Which of the following is the correct way to congratulate someone on their promotion?

a) "Congratulations on your demotion!"

b) "Congratulations on your new job!"

c) "Congratulations on your promotion!"

d) "Congratulations on your retirement!"

Answer: c) "Congratulations on your promotion!"

4. Which of the following is the correct way to congratulate someone on their new house?

a) "Congratulations on your new car!"

b) "Congratulations on your new house!"

c) "Congratulations on your new job!"

d) "Congratulations on your retirement!"

Answer: b) "Congratulations on your new house!"

5. Which of the following is the correct way to congratulate someone on their graduation?

a) "Congratulations on your graduation!"

b) "Congratulations on your new job!"

c) "Congratulations on your promotion!"

d) "Congratulations on your retirement!"

Answer: a) "Congratulations on your graduation!"
