16. jelaskan tentang simple present tense,simple present continues tense, simple present tense. mohon bantuannya ​


Penjelasan:

Simple present tense

Simple present tense adalah tenses yang digunakan ketika suatu kejadian sedang berlangsung saat ini atau kejadian yang berlangsung berulang kali (kebiasaan).

S + Verb (1) + to be (is, am, are) + O

Example :

she is a doctorhe is handsome

Simple present continuous tense

Present continuous tense adalah tense yang berfungsi untuk mengungkapkan aksi yang sedang dilakukan pada masa sekarang (ketika berbicara) dan sesuatu di masa depan.

S + to be ( is, am, are) + verb (ing) + O

Example :

she is cooking I am reading a book

Simple present tense
Formula:
1. (+) S + V1 + O (-) S + do not + V1 + O (?) do + S + V1 + O
2. (+) S (org ketiga tunggal: he, she, it, Ani or luqman) + V1s + O
(-) S + does not + V1 + O
(?) Does + S + V1 + O
3. (+) S+ to be (am/is/are) + non verb (-) S + to be + not + non verb (?) To Be + S + non verb

Examples
1. (+) you have a pen
(-) you do not have a pen
(?) do you have a pen?
2. (+) They go to school
(-) They do not go to school
(?) do they go to school?
3. (+) I and Nana buy new dresses
(-) I and Nana do not buy new dresses
(?) Do I and Nana buy new dresses?
4. (+) Alex and Doni play football
(-) Alex and doni do not play football
(?) Do Alex and Doni play football?
5. (+) she makes cakes
(-) she does not make cakes
(?) does she make cakes?
6. (+) He brings a book
(-) he does not bring a book
(?) does he bring a book?
7. (+) Vivi puts her bag on the table
(-) Vivi does not put her bag on the table
(?) does Vivi put her bag on the table?
8. (+) she is a teacher
(-) she is not a teacher
(?) is she a teacher?
9. (+) I am a student
(-) I am not a student
(?) are you a student?
10 (+) they are your friends
(-) they are not your friends
(?) are they your friends?

Present continuous tense
Formula
(+) S + to be (am/is/are) + Ving + O
(-) S + to be + not + Ving + O
(?) To be + S + Ving + O

Examples
1. (+) I am watching tv
(-) I am not watching tv
(-) are you watching tv?
2. (+) you are taking my wallet
(-) you are not taking my wallet
(?) are you taking my wallet?
3. (+) they are sleeping
(-) they are not sleeping
(?) are they sleeping?
4. (+) she is walking on the fire
(-) she is not walking on the fire
