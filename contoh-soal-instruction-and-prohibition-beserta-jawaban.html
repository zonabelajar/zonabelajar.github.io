16. Write example of prohibition and instruction (min.10)


Prohibitions

Don’t touch me!Don’t say it!Don’t drink the alcohol!Don’t disturb!Don’t run!Don’t be sad!Don’t step on the grass!Don’t be late!Don’t worry!Don’t be lazy!

Instructions

Open the book!Clean your room now!Let’s answer the question!Open the window, please!Let’s start the quiz!Watch the book first!Call me when you get home!Turn off the radio, please!Take your book!Let’s play the game!

Pembahasan

Prohibition adalah sebuah kalimat dalam bahasa Inggris yang digunakan untuk mengungkapkan sebuah larangan atau peringatan bagi orang lain untuk tidak melakukan sesuatu. Kalimat prohibition biasanya diawali dengan kalimat “Don’t / do not” yang artnya jangan. Prohibition memiliki tiga macam bentuk yaitu verbal prohibition, nominal prohibition dan prohibition no + verb-ing.

Verbal prohibition (Don’t + Verb 1)

Contoh: Don’t touch me!

Nominal prohibition (Don’t + be + Adverb/Noun/Adjective)

Contoh : Don’t be sad!

Prohibition no + verb-ing

Contoh : No smoking!

Instruction merupakan jenis kalimat dalam bahasa Inggris yang digunakan untuk memberi instruksi, nasihat atau permintaan. Rumus dari giving instruction adalah verb 1 + object. Contoh:

Open the book!Watch the label!Eat your vegetables!Bring your id card!Turn left and go straight!

29. buatlah contoh drama singkat mengenai instruction dan prohibition


A : Hi Jes, hi Anna. Do you know how to make pancakes?

B : Hi Diana. Yes I do. Do you have a pen and paper to write it down?

C : Hi Diana. Who are you making pancakes for?

A : It's for my parents, I would like to make it for them tomorrow.

B : Ok. We would like to help you. First you need to gather all the ingredients.

C : The ingredients are one and a half cup of flour, three and a half teaspoon of baking powder, 1 teaspoon of salt, 1 tablespoon of white sugar, one and a quarter cup of milk, one egg and 3 tablespoons of melted butter.

A : Ok. Then?

B : Get a large bowl and sift together the flour, salt, sugar and baking powder. Make a hole in the center and pour in the milk, egg and the melted butter. Mix it all up until it has a smooth consistency. Do not overdo it, stop until it's smooth or you will ruin the batter!

C : And then you have to heat the frying pan with a tiny bit of oil in low - medium heat. Do not pour the oil because you only need a very tiny bit of oil. You don't want to fry the batter. Scoop the batter onto the frying pan, and brown the both sides. Serve it while it's hot. You could drizzle some honey on top of it. That is it.

A : Thank you both so much. I will let you know how it goes. I'm sure my parents will love it!

B : No problem Diana. Please let us know how it goes.

C : You're welcome Diana. Good luck!
