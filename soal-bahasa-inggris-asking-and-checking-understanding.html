22. dialog bahasa inggris tentang asking for attention and of checking someone's understanding ? tolong dijawab yang panjang yh plissss 3 jam lagi dikumpulinn


Pembahasan:

Sebelum membuat dialog yang menggunakan expression of asking for attention dan expression of checking someone's understanding, kita sebaiknya mengetahui bagaimana kalimat yang digunakan untuk mengekspresikannya. Berikut contohnya.

Expression of asking for attention

- Attention, please!

- Stop talking please!

- Everybody may i have your attention, please?

- Please, look at me

- Hey!

- Kamu juga dapat meminta perhatian orang lain dengan menyebut namanya

Expression of checking someone's understanding

- Do you get it?

- Do you understand what i'm saying?

- Are you with me?

- Do you get what i mean?

- With me, so far?

Berikut dialognya.

A: Hey, B!

B: Hey. What's up?

A: I want to ask how to make this instant cup noodle.

B: Oh, it's easy. first, open the cup noodle package then take out the seasoning and the plastic fork, after that pour hot water into the cup noodle until this mark. next, close it and wait for about five minutes. Finally, mix it with the seasoning.

A: Wait, i don't get it. can you explain it slowly?

B: Alright. First, open the package and take out the seasoning and the plastic fork. Do you get it?

A: Yes.

B: Then, pour hot water into the cup noodle until this mark. Can you see the mark?

A: Yes, and then?

B: next, close it and wait for about five minutes. With me, so far?

A: Yes, i'm following

B: After that, Mix the noodle and the seasoning, then you can enjoy it.

A: Oh, i see. thank you B

B: You're welcome.
