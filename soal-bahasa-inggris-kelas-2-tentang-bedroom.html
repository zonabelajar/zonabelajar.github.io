30. percakapan bahasa inggris dengan situasion : you have nice bedroom your friend wants to know about it talk about the things in your bedroom what would both of you say ?


Friend : Your bedroom is very neat and clean!
Can we talk about these things in you room, please?
You : No problem! What do you want to say or ask?
Friend : The stuffy dolls here are very cute! Where do you buy it?
You : You like it? I bought it when my mom and I went to France!
Friend : Wow, I like France! Especially the Eiffel tower! And, is this big collection of souvenirs yours?
You : Yeah, I like collecting souvenirs!
Friend : And this table! I like the color! And the way it is decorated, I like it! And it's neat, too!
You : Well, thank you, I also like it's color!
Friend : And this bed! Oh, so lovely! I hope I can have this bed!
You : Woah, I love this kind of color, too!
Friend : Really? And this collection of these pretty jewelries? Are they made of pure gold?
You : No, silly! They are fake, but they are quite pretty, aren't they? Do you want one?
Friend : Oh, may I? Thank you so much! And they are pretty, though.
