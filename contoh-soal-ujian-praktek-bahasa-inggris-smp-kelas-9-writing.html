3. how to make juice mango dalam bahasa inggris dan artinya. tolong kasih tau ya cara membuatnya untuk persiapan ujian praktek kelas 9 smp


how to make juice mango = cara membuat jus mangga
material = alat & bahan
-mango = mangga
-knife = pisau
-water = air
-glass = gelas
-blender = blender
steps = cara / langkah
1.firts , peel and slice mango = pertama , kupas dan potong mangga
2.then , put into the blender = setelah itu ,masukan ke dalam blender
3.and , give some water = dan , beri sedikit air
4.next , turn on the blender = selanjutnya , hidupkan blender
5.while , arrange speed of blender = sementara itu , atur kecepatan blender
6.last , turn off the blender and pour the manggo juice into the glass = terakhir , matikan blender dan tuangkan jus mangga kedalam gelas
*if you want , you can put some garnish or you can mix the manggo to more fruits = jika kamu mau , kamu bisa menambahkan beberapa hiasan atau mencampur  mangga dengan buah-buahan yang lain.

6. Contoh pidato bahasa inggris dengan tema belajar di rumah. Untuk ujian praktek


This is the example of English speech about studying at home:

Today I will discuss studying at home during the pandemic.

An unexpected pandemic makes us have to change everything. Our lifestyle changes, our way of life changes. Including the way we learn has changed drastically. This is a reality that we have to face and live with.

Studying of course can be done anywhere. At school, at home, on the road, we can study. What we are facing now is a complete, if not partial, shift in our learning methods to online and at home. This is certainly out of the ordinary for those of us who meet with friends and teachers every day, now suddenly have to be alone at home facing a smartphone screen.

There are some negative effects when we study online at home. One of them is the less and limited study time. There is no normal human interaction there. Everything is done through the smartphone screen. Another effect is the more free time for students. And as the saying goes: idle hands are the devil's playground. More and more time to play and useless things.

What should we do in the face of these problems? The bottom line is one: the ability to manage time. By studying at home, we should be more diligent in trying and researching the various lessons we get, because we have more time and are not cut off by commuting to school and others.

So no matter how we study, at home or at school, we must remain diligent and enthusiastic, not lazy and negligent.

Percakapan Bahasa Inggris: Berkenalan dengan Orang Lain (Greeting & Introduction)

Get acquainted with others

Mengenalkan diri merupakan sebuah kegiatan yang paling umum dilakukan, terlebih ketika seseorang berharap ingin membangun hubungan dengan orang lain, tidak terkecuali macam hubungan yang sedang dijalin. Dengan berkenalan terlebih dahulu, selanjutnya akan terjadi percakapan dan hubungan yang lebih baik.

Perkenalan juga dapat dibedakan baik secara formal maupun secara informal. Adapun perkenalan secara formal misalnya perkenalan dengan teman kerja atau dengan partner bisnis dan segala perkenalan yang membutuhkan kata kata formal.

Berikut adalah beberapa contoh yang dapat digunakan untuk sebuah perkenalan formal.

Hi! My name is Eadlyn.

(Hai, nama saya adalah Eadlyn.)

Good morning/afternoon/evening. I am Eadlyn. Nice to meet you.

(Selamat pagi/siang/malam. Saya Eadlyn. Senang bertemu dengan Anda.)

How are you today? My name is Eadlyn!

(Apa kabar Anda hari ini? Nama saya Eadlyn.)

I am Eadlyn. It is very nice to meet you.

(Nama saya Eadlyn/Saya Eadlyn. Senang sekali bertemu dengan Anda.)

Hello! I am Eadlyn. I am happy to finally meet you.

(Halo! Saya Eadlyn. Saya senang akhirnya dapat bertemu dengan Anda.)

Hi, good morning. My name is Eadlyn. I come from Bandung.

(Hai, selamat pagi. Nama saya Eadlyn. Saya datang dari Bandung.)
