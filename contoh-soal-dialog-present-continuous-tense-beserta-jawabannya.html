7. buatlah dialog tentang present continuous tense , 4 baris​


Amber: Hey, what're you doing?

[Amber: Hey, apa yang sedang kamu lakukan? ]

Clio: I'm watching some films.

[Clio: Aku sedang menonton beberapa film. ]

Amber: That's nice. Are you watching K-drama?

[Amber: Itu bagus. Apalah kamu sedang menonton K-drama (drama Korea)?]

Clio: No, I am watching Frozen II.

[Clio: Tidak, aku sedang menonton Frozen II. ]

Penjelasan:

Present continous digunakan untuk menyatakan hal yang sedang terjadi di masa sekarang. Biasanya ditandai dengan kata now atau at the moment.

Form:

(+)S+is/am/are+V-ing(-)S+is/am/are+not+V-ing(?)Is/am/are+S+V-ing+?

is --> she, he, it, benda tunggal

are --> you, they, we, benda jamak

am --> I

V-ing: present participle

Contoh:

(+) I am reading book.

[(+) Aku sedang membaca buku. ]

(-) I am not reading book.

[(-) Aku tidak sedang membaca buku. ]

(?) Am I reading book?

[(?) Apakah aku sedang membaca buku? ]

Berikut adalah contoh dialog yang menggunakan berbagai tenses yang berbeda:

Person A: Hi, guys! What are you doing this weekend?

Person B: I'm going to a concert on Saturday night. I've been waiting for this for months!

Person C: That sounds fun! I went to a concert last weekend too. The band was amazing!

Person A: Oh, really? Who did you see?

Person C: I saw Coldplay. They were playing all their hit songs.

Person B: I wish I could have gone with you. I've always wanted to see Coldplay live.

Person A: Don't worry, they'll be back in town next year. We can all go together then.

Person C: I've never been to a music festival, but I'm planning to go to one next summer.

Person B: You should definitely go! It's so much fun to see all your favorite artists in one place.

Person B: I'm sure it will be. We'll have a great time together.

Dalam dialog di atas, kita menggunakan berbagai tenses:

1. Present Continuous Tense: "What are you doing this weekend?"

2. Future Continuous Tense: "I'm going to a concert on Saturday night."

3. Past Tense: "I went to a concert last weekend."

4. Past Continuous Tense: "I was waiting for this for months."

5. Present Perfect Tense: "I've always wanted to see Coldplay live."

22. Jelaskan pengertian present continuous tense beserta rumus dan contohnya !


Present continuous tense atau present progressive tense adalah bentuk kata kerja yang digunakan untuk menyatakan aksi yang sedang berlangsung sekarang (present) atau rencana di masa depan (future).

rumusnya

( + ) S + be (am/is/are) + present participle (-ing)

( - ) S + be (am/is/are) + not + present participle (-ing)

( ? ) be (am/is/are) + S + present participle (-ing)?

Contohnya

( + ) The ships are sailing

( - ) The ships are not sailing

( ? ) Are the ships sailing ?

Dengan membuat dialog (percakapan) dilengkapi bentuk kalimat berupa present continuous, future, dan present perfect tense.

Present continuous:

Merupakan bentuk kalimat menyatakan suatu peristiwa/kegiatan dengan kata kerja berupa -ing terjadi pada masa kini secara bertahapan.

Penggunaan umum:

S + to be (is/am/are) + V-ing + O + C

To be diperhatikan dari subyeknya.

Contoh pada dialog:

— Where are you going?

— I'm going to finish

(Apabila verb dilalui verb, maka verb kedua menjadi to verb)

Simple future:

Disebut juga future tense merupakan bentuk pernyataan kegiatan dilakukan nanti atau belum dilakukan sekarang tapi akan dilakukan pada waktu berikutnya.

Penggunaan umum:

S + will + V1 + O + C

Contoh pada dialog:

— I will accompany.

— She will out from the hospital tomorrow.

Present perfect:

Pernyataan pada kalimat dari suatu pekerjaan sudah dilakukan pada waktu itu.

Penggunaan umum:

S + has/have + V3 + O + C

has/have diperhatikan/dipilih dari subyeknya.

Contoh pada dialog:

— Haven't you finished it?

— I should have finished it.

— Candi has an accident and she has hospitalized

37. Jelaskanlah apa itu present continuous tense, present perfect dan present perfect continuous tense. Berilah masing-masing contoh kalimat!


Present continuous tense

Aksi yang sedang berlangsung.

Contoh : They are playing in the backyard.

Present perfect tense

Aksi yang terjadi di masa lampau dan sudah berakhir.

Contoh : I have eaten the cake you gave me.

Present perfect continuous tense

Aksi yang terjadi di masa lampau dan masih berlangsung.

Contoh : She has been painting for 2 hours.

Jawaban:

Present continuous tense adalah bentuk tense yang dapat kamu gunakan untuk menjelaskan sesuatu atau menyatakan aksi yang sedang berlangsung selama waktu tertentu pada masa sekarang (present).

contoh: Aunt Christine is warming up the car while Scott looks for his new leather coat.

Present perfect continuous tense adalah suatu bentuk kata kerja yang digunakan untuk menyatakan aksi yang telah selesai pada suatu titik di masa lalu atau aksi telah dimulai di masa lalu dan terus berlanjut sampai sekarang.

contoh: The construction laborers are thirsty since they have been removing the scaffoldings. (Pekerja konstruksi haus karena mereka telah memindahkan perancah.)

Person B: I will be taking a break from studying tomorrow. I am going to the movies with some friends.

Total kalimat dalam dialog ini adalah 15 kalimat, dan tenses yang digunakan adalah:

- Simple Present Continuous: "What are you doing tonight?"

- Simple Past Tense: "I went to the gym this morning."

- Past Continuous: "I was lifting weights when I saw you walking in."

- Simple Future Continuous Tense: "I will be taking a break from studying tomorrow."

- Simple Present Perfect Tense: "I have studied a lot, but I still need more time."

- Simple Past Perfect Tense: "I have already finished studying."
