7. tema : 17 Agustus description dan identification bahasa inggris​


Jawaban:

Theme: 17th August - Indonesian Independence Day

Description:

17th August is a significant and celebrated day in Indonesia as it marks the country's Independence Day. It commemorates the proclamation of independence from Dutch colonial rule on August 17, 1945. This momentous occasion is a national holiday, and the entire nation comes together to celebrate with great enthusiasm and patriotism.

On this day, Indonesians take part in various festivities and events held across the country. One of the most iconic traditions is the raising of the national flag, known as "Merah Putih," at government buildings, schools, and public places. The flag-raising ceremony is conducted with great respect and solemnity.

Another highlight of the day is the parade, where school children, military personnel, and community groups march through the streets in a display of unity and pride. The parade is accompanied by traditional music, dance performances, and cultural showcases that reflect the diversity and rich heritage of Indonesia.

Indonesians also celebrate Independence Day with games and competitions, such as sack races, tug-of-war, and eating contests. These activities foster a sense of camaraderie and joy among the participants.

Throughout the day, neighborhoods and communities organize various events, including cultural exhibitions, art performances, and food festivals that showcase the country's culinary delights.

Independence Day is not just about celebrating the past but also a time for Indonesians to reflect on their national identity and values. It is a reminder of the sacrifices made by the nation's founding fathers and a reaffirmation of their commitment to building a united and prosperous Indonesia.

Identification:

The text describes the celebration of 17th August, which is Indonesia's Independence Day. It mentions the significance of the day, the events and traditions observed, and the sense of pride and patriotism displayed by the Indonesian people during the celebration. The text also highlights the cultural and historical aspects of the celebration, emphasizing the importance of unity and national identity.

11. texs description bahasa inggris malioboro


Descriptive text of Malioboro:

Malioboro is a legend and well-known shopping centre in Indonesia. It is located in Sosromenduran village, Gedong Tengen, Yogyakarta, Daerah Istimewa Yogyakarta, Indonesia. Malioboro is actually a joint street area of Jalan Margo Utomo, Jalan Malioboro, dan Jalan Margo Mulyo. The long of the street as shopping centre is up to 2,5 kilometer from Yogya pillar to Yogyakarta Post Office.

The name of Malioboro in the history are two versions. First, Marlborough is a name of noble when Indonesia is dominated by England (1811 - 1816). Second, malioboro means bouquet.

As shopping centre, there are many people at that street sell special souvenirs and clothes. There are a lot of tourists visit this place every day as it near to Borobudur Temple, about 40 km. This tourism place now becomes favorite destination to complete their trip.

Pembahasan

Descritive text adalah sebuah teks yang mana menggambarkan secara detail tentang seseorang, tempat, atau benda. Dalam mendeskripsikan suatu tempat, informasi yang lazim diperlukan adalah nama, lokasi, definisi, manfaat, kelebihan, dan interaksi yang dilakukan oleh masyarakat di tempat tersebut.

Berikut terjemahan teks deskripsi di atas.

Malioboro adalah pusat perbelanjaan yang legendaris dan terkenal di Indonesia. Letaknya di desa Sosromenduran, Gedong Tengen, Yogyakarta, Daerah Istimewa Yogyakarta, Indonesia. Malioboro sebenarnya adalah gabungan dari 3 jalan yaitu Jalan Margo Utomo, Jalan Malioboro, dan Jalan Margo Mulyo. Panjang jalanan tersebut sebagai pusat perbelanjaan mencapai 2,5 kilometer dari tugu Yogya ke Kantor Pos Yogyakarta.

Nama Malioboro dalam sejarah memiliki 2 versi. Pertama, Marlborough adalah nama seorang bangsawan ketika Indonesia masih dalam kekuasaan bangsa Inggris (1811 - 1816). Kedua, Malioboro artinya karangan bunga.

Sebagai pusat perbelanjaan, ada banyak wisatawan menjual cindera mata atau pakaian. Ada banyak wisatawan berkunjung ke lokasi ini karena lokasinya dekat dengan Candi Borobudur, sekitar 40 km. Tempat wisata ini kini menjadi tujuan favorite untuk menyempurnakan perjalanan wisata mereka.

17. Description komodo pake bahasa inggris



Komodo dragons are the largest living lizards in the world. They are identified by their massive size, flat heads, bowed legs and long, thick tails. The name comes from rumors that a dragon-like creature lived on the Indonesian island of Komodo. No Western scientists had seen a Komodo dragon until 1912, according to the San Diego Zoo. Local people call them "ora," or "land crocodile.”

The average size of a male Komodo dragon is 8 to 9 feet and about 200 lbs., according to the Smithsonian National Zoological Park, but they can reach a whopping 10 feet (3 meters) in length. Females grow to 6 feet (1.8 m).

Komodos come in a variety of colors, including blue, orange, green and gray. Their skin is rough and durable, reinforced with bony plates called osteoderms. They have long claws and a large, muscular tail.

Descriptive text bertujuan untuk menjelaskan kepada pembaca mengenai seseorang, tempat, atau benda secara detail.

Dalam menulis descriptive text, tenses yang digunakan adalah simple present tense.
