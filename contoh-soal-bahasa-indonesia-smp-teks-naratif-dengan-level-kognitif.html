4. berikan contoh teks naratif dalam bahasa indonesia singkat​


Jawaban:

Judul: Anak yang Mencari Bintang Jatuh

Orientasi:

Pada suatu hari, ada seorang anak laki-laki bernama Andi yang tinggal di sebuah desa kecil. Andi sangat suka melihat bintang di langit malam. Dia sering bermimpi untuk bisa meraih bintang-bintang itu.

Komplikasi:

Suatu malam, Andi melihat sebuah bintang jatuh. Dia sangat senang dan segera berlari keluar rumah untuk mengejarnya. Andi berlari secepat mungkin, tetapi bintang itu semakin jauh dan semakin kecil.

Resolusi:

Andi terus berlari tanpa lelah. Dia tidak menyerah meski bintang itu semakin sulit untuk dijangkau. Akhirnya, Andi berhasil meraih bintang itu. Bintang itu ternyata adalah sebuah batu kecil yang jatuh dari langit.

Amanat:

Jangan pernah menyerah pada mimpimu, meski itu terlihat mustahil. Dengan usaha dan kerja keras, semua hal bisa dicapai.

Penjelasan:

Teks naratif ini memiliki struktur yang lengkap, yaitu orientasi, komplikasi, resolusi, dan amanat. Orientasi menjelaskan tentang latar belakang cerita, yaitu Andi yang suka melihat bintang dan bermimpi untuk bisa meraihnya. Komplikasi menjelaskan tentang peristiwa yang terjadi, yaitu Andi melihat bintang jatuh dan mengejarnya. Resolusi menjelaskan tentang penyelesaian dari peristiwa yang terjadi, yaitu Andi berhasil meraih bintang itu. Amanat menjelaskan pesan moral yang ingin disampaikan oleh penulis, yaitu jangan pernah menyerah pada mimpimu.

Semoga contoh-contoh ini dapat membantu Anda memahami teks naratif.

Sura and Baya

A long time ago, there were two animals, Sura and Baya.(pada zaman dahulu, ada 2 ekor binatang, Sura dan Baya). Sura was the name of a shark and Baya was a crocodile. (Sura adalah hiu dan Baya adalah buaya).They lived in a sea.(mereka hidup di lautan).

Once Sura and Baya were looking for some food. (segera sesudah sura dan baya melihat beberapa makanan) Suddenly, Baya saw a goat.(Tiba-tiba, baya melihat seekor kambing).

"Yummy, this is my lunch," said Baya.
("Enakk, ini adalah makanan siangku," kata Baya).

"No way! This is my lunch. ("Tidak akan! Ini adalah makanan siangku.) You are greedy" said Sura. (kamu rakus", kata Sura). Then they fought for the goat. (Lalu, mereka berkelahi untuk mendapatkan kambing itu.) After several hours, they were very tired. (Setelah beberapa jam, mereka lelah).

Feeling tired of fighting, they lived in the different places. (merasa lelah untuk berkelahi, mereka tinggal di temoat yg berbeda). Sura lived in the water and Baya lived in the land.(sura tinggal di lautan dan baya tinggal di daratan.) The border was the beach, so they would never fight again. (Perbatasannya adalah pantai, sehingga mereka tidak akan pernah berkelahi lagi.)

One day, Sura went to the land and looked for some food in the river. (Pada suatu hari, sura pergi ke daratan dan melihat beberapa makanan di sungai). He was very hungry and there was not much food in the sea. (Ia sangat lapar dan di sana tidak ada banyak makanan di lautan). Baya was very angry when he knew that Sura broke the promise.(baya sangat marah ketika ia tahu bahwa sura mengingkari janjinya).

They fought again. (Mereka berkelahi lagi.) They both hit each other. (mereka menggigit satu sama lain). Sura bit Baya’s tail. (Sura menggigit ekor baya). Baya did the same thing to Sura. (baya melakukan hal yang sama kepada sura). He bit very hard until Sura finally gave up and Awent back to the sea. (Ia menggigit sangat keras hingga akhirnya sura menyerah dan kembali ke lautan lagi.) Baya was happy.(baya merasa senang).

True Friends

Once upon a time, there were two close friends who were walking through the forest together. They knew that anything dangerous can happen any time in the forest. So they promised each other that they would always be together in any case of danger.

Suddenly, they saw a large bear getting closer toward them. One of them climbed a nearby tree at once. But unfortunately the other one did not know how to climb up the tree. So being led by his common sense, he lay down on the ground breathless and pretended to be a dead man.

The bear came near the one who was lying on the ground. It smelt in his ears, and slowly left the place because the bears do not want to touch the dead creatures. Moral of the Story – A true friend in need is a friend indeed.

1. Flock of Doves
Once upon a time, there was a flock of doves that flew in search of food led by their king. They had flown a long distance and were very tired. The smallest dove picked up speed and found some rice scattered beneath a banyan tree. So all the doves landed and began to eat.
Suddenly a net fell over them and they were all trapped. They saw a hunter approaching carrying. The doves desperately fluttered their wings trying to get out, but to no avail. The king had an idea. He advised all the doves to fly up together carrying the net with them.
Each dove picked up a portion of the net and together they flew off carrying the net with them. The hunter looked up in astonishment. He tried to follow them, but they were flying high over hills and valleys. They flew to a hill near a city of temples where there lived a mouse who could help them. He was a faithful friend of the dove king.
The mouse agreed saying that he would set the king free first. The king insisted that he first free his subjects and the king last. The mouse understood the king’s feelings and complied with his wishes. He began to cut the net and one by one all the doves were freed including the dove king.

2. Kawanan Merpati
Suatu waktu, kawanan burung merpati terbang mencari makanan yang dipimpin oleh raja burng. Mereka telah terbang jarak jauh dan sangat lelah. Seekor merpati terkecil terbang lebih cepat dan melihat padi tersebar di bawah pohon beringin. Maka semua merpati itu turun dan mulai makan.
Tiba-tiba ada jaring yang jatuh dari atas mereka dan mereka semua terjebak. Mereka melihat seorang pemburu mendekat. Merpati berusaha keras terbang dengan sayap-sayapnya untuk keluar, tetapi tidak berhasil. Raja merpati akhirnya punya ide. Dia menyarankan semua merpati untuk terbang bersama membawa jaring itu sekalian.

Jawaban:

1. Narrative text is a type of text that consists of imaginary stories, engineered true stories, or fairy tales. The narrative text tells a story that has a chronological collection that is interconnected. The purpose of the narrative text is to please the reader.

2. Narrative text consists of three stuctures as explain below:

Orientation: This part is introducing the story through introducing the characters, the place and time where the story began and so on.

Complication: This part is about the problem appear in the story when the main character begins to face it.

Resolution: This part is telling the way the main character overcome the problem and also the part in which the story is ended.

3. Linguistic Feature :

Nouns tertentu sebagai kata ganti orang, dalam cerita, misalnya : stepsister, housework,Adjectives yang membentuk noun phrase , misalnya : long back hair, two red apples,Time connectives dan conjunctions untuk mengurutkan kejadian, misalnya : then, before that, dsb.Adverb dan adverbial phrase untuk menunjukkan lokasi kejadian atau peristiwa misalnya : here, in the mountain, happily ever after, dsb.Saying verbs yang menandai ucapan, seperti : said, told, dan thinking verbs yang menandai pikiran, perasaan tokoh misalnya : thought, felt, dsb.Action verbs dalam past tense, misalnya : stayed, dsb.

4. Kinf of narative text :

FAIRY TALE (DONGENG) : Cerita yang sudah umum di ketahui oleh orang-orang di seluruh dunia, contoh : Cinderella, Snow White, dll.LEGEND ( LEGENDA ) : Cerita tentang asal usul terjadinya suatu daerah, benda, binatang, gedung, missal : Tangkupan Perahu, Roro Jonggrang, Lake Toba.MYTHE ( MITOS ) : Cerita yang sudah menjadi kepercayaan rakyat dan mempengaruhi kehidupan masyarakat di sekitarnya. Contoh : Nyai Roro Kidul.FABLE ( FABEL ) : Cerita binatang yang bertingkah laku seperti manusia. Contoh : Mouse deer.FOLKLORE ( CERITA RAKYAT ) : Cerita yang ada di masyarakat yang biasanya disebarluaskan dari mulut ke mulut. Contoh : Angkling Dharma, Wali Songo, dll.
