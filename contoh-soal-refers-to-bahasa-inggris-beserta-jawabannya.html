36. bahasa indonesia the underlined word refers to


Bahasa Indonesia dari "the underlined word refers to" adalah kata yang bergaris bawah merujuk pada.

Kata "underlined" merupakan kata sifat yang memberi keterangan kata "word" Sedangkan kata "refers to" merupakan kata kerja.

Pembahasan

Contoh soal dengan pertanyaan "the underlined word refers to" yaitu

The white pelican is one of the most successful fish eating birds. The success is largely due to its command hunting behaviour. A group, perhaps two dozens birds, will gather is a curved are some distance offshore The birds then begin to move forward toward the shore, beating the water furiously with their wings, driving the fish before them.

The underlined word refers to....

a. fish

b. white pelicans

c. birds

d. a group

Pada soal ini, kata "their" merujuk pada subjek pada teks tersebutm yaitu white pelicans.
