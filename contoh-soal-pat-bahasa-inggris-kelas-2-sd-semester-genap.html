14. matematika kelas 4 sd pat ​


~Bangun Datar

______________

Jawaban:

1.728 m² (Opsi B)

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Luas Taman Tomat

= (4/4 - 1/4) × L Persegi

= ¾ × s²

= ¾ × 48²

= ¾ × 2.304

= (3 × 2.304) ÷ 4

= 6.912 ÷ 4

= 1.728 m²✔

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

– Detail Jawaban

Mapel : MatematikaKelas : VMateri : Bangun DatarKode Soal : 2Kode Kategorisasi : 5.2.3

Jawaban:

B. 1.728m2

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Pertama tama kita cari dulu luasnyah yaa..

luas persigi adalah=S²

berarti 48²=2.304

diketahui lagi jika ¼ ditanamj jagung dan sisnyah tomat

beratti ¼×2.304=576

tinggal mencari luaa tonat berati 2.304-576=1.728m²

15. berikan jawaban latihan soal pat /ukk kelas 8 Bahasa Inggris beri kunci jawaban nya​


Soal 1:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

My sister ________ tennis every Saturday morning.

a) plays

b) play

c) played

d) playing

Jawaban: a) plays

Soal 2:

Complete the sentence with the correct preposition:

I'm going to the store ________ some groceries.

a) at

b) with

c) for

d) to

Jawaban: c) for

Soal 3:

Choose the correct question tag for the following sentence:

He doesn't like coffee, ________?

a) does he?

b) isn't he?

c) doesn't he?

d) don't he?

Jawaban: a) does he?

Soal 4:

Identify the correct comparative form of the adjective "good":

a) gooder

b) goodest

c) better

d) goodier

Jawaban: c) better

Soal 5:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

She ________ a book when I arrived.

a) reads

b) is reading

c) read

d) has read

Jawaban: b) is reading

Soal 6:

Choose the correct word order for the sentence:

carrots / do / like / you / ?

a) You do like carrots?

b) Do you like carrots?

c) Carrots you do like?

d) Do like you carrots?

Jawaban: b) Do you like carrots?

Soal 7:

Identify the correct spelling of the word:

a) beutiful

b) beutifull

c) beautiful

d) beautifull

Jawaban: c) beautiful

Soal 8:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

The students ________ to the teacher's instructions.

a) listens

b) listen

c) listened

d) listening

Jawaban: b) listen

Soal 9:

Choose the correct possessive form of the noun:

The dog is ________ bone.

a) its

b) it's

c) their

d) they're

Jawaban: a) its

Soal 10:

Identify the correct punctuation for the sentence:

My favorite color is blue ________.

a) .

b) ,

c) !

d) ?

Jawaban: a) .

38. menjelaskan zona laut teritorial Kelas 6 SD kisi kisi IPS untuk PAT​


Jawaban:

Laut teritorial atau perairan teritorial (bahasa Inggris: Territorial sea) adalah wilayah kedaulatan suatu negara pantai selain wilayah daratan dan perairan pedalamannya; sedangkan bagi suatu negara kepulauan seperti Indonesia, Jepang, dan Filipina, laut teritorial meliputi pula suatu jalur laut yang berbatasan dengannya perairan kepulauannya dinamakan perairan internal termasuk dalam laut teritorial pengertian kedaulatan ini meliputi ruang udara di atas laut teritorial serta dasar laut dan tanah di bawahnya dan, kedaulatan atas laut teritorial dilaksanakan dengan menurut ketentuan Konvensi Perserikatan Bangsa-Bangsa tentang Hukum Laut(United Nations Convention on the Law of the Sea)[1]lebar sabuk perairan pesisir ini dapat diperpanjang paling banyak dua belas mil laut (22,224 km) dari garis dasar (baseline-sea)

Istilah laut teritorial dan perairan teritorial kadang-kala digunakan pula secara informal untuk menggambarkan di mana negara memiliki yurisdiksi, termasuk perairan internal, zona tambahan, zona ekonomi eksklusif dan landas kontinen berpotensi.
