1. soal personal letter beserta jawabannya


Q : what is the structure of personal letter?
A  : 1. Date : Date when the letter is writen (top left).
      2. Address : Place where you are writing fron (top right).
      3. Salutation & Name : Greeting and the person’s name you are writing to.
      4. Introduction : The opening of the letter usually starts with “how are you?”  or refers to previous letter.
      5. Body : The main part of the letter. It includes what you want to write to the other person.
      6. Closure : The part indicates the letter is going to end.
      7. Complimentary close : Short expressions like “love you” , “sincerely yours” , “love”.
      8. Signature : Signature or initials of the writer.
      9. Postscript P.S : After thougt in a letter. You begin with P.S. and end it with your initials.

13. soal dan pembahasan letter


Pembahasan:
Letter adalah text functional yang merupakan materi SKL no.12Letter adalah text yang berupa surat, lengkap dengan addressee/penerima, addresser/pengirim, pembukaan surat, isi surat, penutup surat.Ada bermacam-macam surat, ada surat yang bersifat pribadi ada yang bersifat resmi. Biasanya, surat resmi dilengkapi dengan kepala surat (kop surat) yang menunjukkan pengirim surat lengkap dengan alamat kantor/perusahaan. 

B.Inggris::::
Letter is a functional text which is a matter SKL no.12 Letter is a text in the form of a letter , complete with the addressee / receiver , addresser / sender , the opening of a letter , the contents of the letter , a cover letter . There are all sorts of letters , there is a personalized letter there was an official nature . Usually , an official letter equipped with the letterhead ( letterhead ) showing the sender's complete letter with the address of the office / company .

Soal:
Soal yang biasanya muncul dalam UN:A.Gambaran Umum.- What is the text about?- The letter tells us about……..B.Function of the text: Fungsi/tujuan surat.- The function of the letter is….- The writer wrote the letter to…..- Why did the addresser send such a letter?C.Addresser (sender) and addressee (receiver): pengirim dan penerima surat.- Who sent the letter?- The addresser of the letter is……- Who is the receiver of the text?- Who is the addressee?D.Pertanyaan tersirat/tersurat mengenai informasi dalam surat (Where, when, how, who, which dsb).- Where does the writer live?- When did the writer write the letter?  * ingat : Tanggal penulisan surat ada di bagian kiri/kanan atas surat.  * Kata “tomorrow” didalam surat berarti satu hari setelah tanggal surat.   *  Kata “ yesterday” di dalam surat bearati satu hari sebelum tanggal penulisan surat. Demikian juga      kata lain yang berhubungan dengan waktu, seperti “two weeks ago’, “two weeks later” dan sebagainya     supaya dicermati dan dihitung dari tanggal penulisan surat.E.Reference: Menanyakan kata ganti.- The word “I” / “you” in the text refers to…..- The word “here”/”there” in the text refers to……   Ingat: Kata “here” kembali ke tempat penulis menulis surat, sedang kata “there” kembali ke tempat   penerima surat.

35. Sebutkan contoh soal business letter dan jawabannya


Jawaban:

Business letters adalah jenis surat yang biasanya digunakan dalam perihal perniagaan atau administrasi perkantoran. Ada beberapa jenis business letters yang termasuk sering digunakan seperti letter of inquiry, letter of complaint, letter of apology, letter of order and request dan lainnya.

Berikut ini adalah salah satu contoh business letter. (Letter of inquiry)

Dear Mr.Villalibre,

I am writing to enquire in relation to one of your instagram account which offers the football boots product, 'Casa de Villalibre'. I am interested in conducting discussion on the collaboration with my company which runs the football academy for the young adult. I wonder if you could inform me the details of your product mentioned in your social media and schedule an online meeting this week.

I look forward to hearing from you.

Best Regards,

Ahmed Al-Hilal

Penjelasan:

Berikut contoh soalnya:

1. What does the sender want to ask to Mr.Villalibre? The permission to discuss about the collaboration project and the details of the product.

2. Which platform does Mr.Al-Hilal know about Mr.Villalibre's product?

He knows it from Instagram.

3. When is the suggested online meeting expected to be held? This week.

4. What is the meaning of 'conduct'?

It means to 'hold' or 'do'.

37. Tuliskan contoh soal personal letter dalam Bahasa Inggris dan jawabannya!


Penjelasan:

Contoh Personal Letter 1

Apt BLK 30, Eunos St. #05 – 116

Singapore 1441

2nd April 2009

Dear Liza,

I have received you letter asking me to meet you at your house this Sunday to discuss the urgent matter you have. I regret however to say that it will not be possible for me to see you on Sunday as I already have a previous appointment . I shall see you on Monday next week at 5 p.m . Hoping the change of date will be convenient to you .

Your sincerely

Daisy

Arti

Liza sayang,

Saya telah menerima surat Anda yang meminta saya untuk menemui Anda di rumah Anda hari Minggu ini untuk membahas masalah mendesak yang Anda miliki. Namun saya menyesal mengatakan bahwa tidak mungkin bagi saya untuk melihat Anda pada hari Minggu karena saya sudah memiliki janji sebelumnya. Aku akan menemuimu pada hari Senin minggu depan jam 5 malam. Berharap perubahan tanggal akan nyaman bagi Anda.

Yang Tulus,

Daisy

1. What does the letter tell about ….

A. Liza’s problem

B. Liza’s appointment

C. Daisy’s previous letter to Liza

D. Daisy’s regret for not being able to come (Jawaban Benar)

E. A meeting held at Liza’s house

40. Soal pilihan ganda tentang application letter beserta jawabannya


1. Servers give directions to bussers. (F)

2. Hosts and hostesses are assisted by servers. (T)

3. Many employees bus tables on busy nights. (T)

Pembahasan

The Post Meridian Restaurant

NOW HIRING

Are you friendly? Do you work well with other people? Then join the front-office team at the Post Meridian Restaurant. We are currently hiring wait staff.

Servers are responsible for the following duties:

• Working with the host or hostess to help customers to their seats

• Putting utensils and napkins on tables before customers arrive

• Explaining the different dishes on the menu to customers

• Taking food orders

• Bringing orders to kitchen staff

• Delivering food from the kitchen to customers

• Helping bussers clear tables during busy shifts

• Following directions from the head waiter

For more information, call Jim Paisley at 555-1907.

1. False. Servers tidak memberi arahan (giving direction) kepada bussers, mereka hanya membantu bussers membersihkan meja (clear tables).

2. True. Servers bekerja bersama host atau hostess untuk membantu pelanggan ke tempat duduk mereka.

3. True. Saat sibuk (on a busy night), banyak karyawan yang membantu membersihkan meja.
