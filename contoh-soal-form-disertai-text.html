5. Contoh medical form , purchase form , order form, feservation form ?


Answer:

1. Medical form

Medical form is a form which is filled in by a patient to a doctor. This form contains the patient's personal health information, such as his/her illness, allergies, currently taken medicines, family health history, etc.

2. Purchase form

Purchase form is also known as purchase order (PO) form. Purchase form is a commercial document which is made by a buyer to a seller and used as a request to purchase products or services (normally in large quantities). This form contains The company name, client's information, types, quantities, and agreed prices of products or service purchased.

3. Order form

Order form is a commercial document which is filled in by customers and used to order goods from a company. This form contains The company name, client's information, types, quantities, and agreed prices of products ordered.

4. Reservation form

Reservation form is a form which is filled in when booking or reserving something. it can be a hotel room, entertainment tickets, transportation, etc. This form contains the client's information, a number of things or places booked, and the date and time of when it's used. Some reservation forms require down payment as a reservation deal.

The example of each form is attached below in the form of a picture.

25. What is the form of each text called?​


Jawaban:

General Knowledge:

Personal Letters:

The first text above is a personal conventional letter.The second text below is a personal electronic mail (personal email)

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran General Knowledge (pengetahuan umum) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Berdasarkan teks dalam gambar pada soal, kedua jenis teks tersebut adalah Personal Letters yang artinya adalah "surat pribadi" dari dua orang yang bersahabat namun terpisahkan oleh jarak dan waktu.

Surat pribadi bisa berbentuk surat elektronik (email) maupun surat jenis konvesional yang dituliskan di atas kertas, dimasukkan ke amplop dan dikirimkan melalui pos ke alamat yang dituju.

Berdasarkan teks, maka teks yang pertama (gambar di atas) adalah surat pribadi jenis konvensional (biasa dilakukan sebelum tahun 2000) dan teks yang kedua adalah surat pribadi jenis surat elektronik (email) yang dikirimkan melalui internet karena pada saat surat pertama sampai ke alamat yang dituju, koneksi internet di tempat orang yang mengirimkan surat sudah mulai terwujud, jadi surat balasannya dikirimkan melalui internet, berupa email.
