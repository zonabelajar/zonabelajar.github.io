4. Tuliskan dan Jelaskan tentang simple present continous tense beserta contoh nya !!


Jawaban:

Present continous tense adalah bentuk tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan bahwa suatu aksi sedang berlangsung selama waktu tertentu pada waktu sekarang (present). Bentuk ini menunjukkan bahwa aksi tersebut terjadi sebelum (begins before), selama (is in progress at the present), dan berlanjut setelah (continues after) waktu atau aksi lainnya.

Rumus :

Positive (+) Subjek + be (am/is/are) + verb (-ing) + …

Example: 1. She is sleeping right now 2. We are studying today

(-) Negative Subjek +be (am/is/are) + not + verb (-ing) + …

Example: 1. She is not sleeping right now 2.  We are not studying today

(?) Interrogative Be (am/is/are) + subjek + verb (-ing) + …?

Example: 1. Is she sleeping right now?  2. Are we studying today?

Simple Present

a) Untuk aksi yang terjadi berulang-ulang atau secara reguler.

Contoh kalimat:

1. Alan reads one book a day.

2. Shella usually walks to her campus.

3. The odd semester starts on September

b) Untuk menyatakan fakta, situasi, dan keadaan yang kita tidak harapkan untuk berubah.

Contoh kalimat:  Shella lives in Bogor.

Present Continuous

a) Untuk aksi yang terjadi selama waktu pembicaraan.

Contoh kalimat:

1. Alan cannot pick up the phone right now because he is reading his book.

b.) Untuk aksi yang berlangsung sekitar waktu pembicaraan

Contoh kalimat:

1. Alan is reading three books a day these days because he needs to catch up to his personal reading goal.

c) Present continuous tense untuk menyatakan situasi dan aksi yang terjadi hanya sementara atau kita harapkan untuk berubah.

Contoh kalimat:  

1. Shella is living in Depok for the time being due to her study.

2. Alan is working at a library this two weeks because his classes are on break.

21. buatlah 5 contoh kalimat Past continous tense,Future continous tense, and present continous tense​


Jawaban:

1. Contoh kalimat past continuous tense digunakan ketika kita membicarakan hal yang sedang terjadi di masa lampau. Bagaimana mengungkapkan kalimatnya dalam bahasa Inggris? 

Kalimat past continuous tense ditandai dengan was/were + V1+ing dan waktu yang lebih spesifik. Kalimatnya juga bisa digunakan bersama dengan simple past atau past continuous juga dengan menggunakan when atau while.

Berikut contoh kalimat bahasa Inggris past continuous tense:

(+) Fina was talking to me when her mother called. / Fina sedang berbicara denganku ketika ibunya menelepon.

(-) Fina was not talking to me when her mother called. / Fina tidak sedang berbicara denganku ketika ibunya menelepon.

(?) Was Fina talking to you when her mother called? / Apakah Fina sedang berbicara denganmu ketika ibunya menelepon?

(+) I was preparing for the exam in October. / Aku sedang mempersiapkan ujian pada bulan Oktober.

(-) I wasn’t preparing for the exam in October. / Aku tidak sedang mempersiapkan ujian pada bulan Oktober.

(?) Were you preparing for the exam in October? / Apakah kamu sedang mempersiapkan ujian pada bulan Oktober?

2. Kalimat past future continuous tense digunakan ketika berbicara mengenai sesuatu yang akan sedang berlangsung di masa lampau.

Kalimat past future continuous tense ditandai dengan would + be + Ving.

Berikut contoh kalimat bahasa Inggris past future continuous tense:

- They would be preparing the presentation if the light didn’t turn off. / Mereka akan mempersiapkan presentasinya jika lampunya tidak mati.

- The teacher said that they should be studying math. / Gurunya berkata bahwa mereka seharusnya belajar matematika.

- The manager said that they would be filming a new drama. / Manajernya berkata bahwa mereka akan syuting drama baru.

3. Apa itu present continuous tense? Melansir British Council, we use the present continuous (am/is/are + -ing) to talk about temporary things which have begun but haven't finished.

Kita menggunakan present continuous (am/is/are + -ing) untuk berbicara tentang hal-hal sementara yang telah dimulai tapi belum selesai.

Contoh kalimat present continuous tense

Berikut contoh kalimat bahasa Inggris present continuous tense:

(+) I am making a glass of ice tea. / Aku sedang membuat segelas es teh.

(-) I am not making a glass of ice tea. / Aku tidak sedang membuat segelas es teh.

(?) Are you making a glass of ice tea? / Apakah kamu sedang membuat segelas es teh?

(+) My brother is studying in his room. / Kakakku sedang belajar di kamarnya.

(-) My brother is not studying in his room. / Kakakku tidak sedang belajar di kamarnya.

(?) Is your brother studying in his room? / Apakah kakakmu sedang belajar di kamarnya?

33. rumus active dari present perfect continous, past future perfect continous tense, present future perfect continous tense beserta contohnya


Present Perfect Continous
(+) S + Have/Has + Been + V-ing + O/C >>> She has been eating the cake for 2 hours
(-) S + Have/Has + not + Been + V-ing + O/C >>> She hasn't been eating the cake for 2 hours
(?) Have/Has + S + Been + V-ing + O/C? >>> Has she been eating the cake for 2 hours?

Past Future Perfect Continous
(+) S + Would + Have + Been + V-ing + O/C >>> She would have been eating cake for two hours
(-) S + Would + not + Have + Been + V-ing + O/C >>> She wouldn't have been eating the cake for 2 hours
(?) Would + S + Have + Been + V-ing + O/C? >>> Would she have been eating the cake for 2 hours?

Future Perfect Continous
(+) S + Will + Have + Been + V-ing + O/C >>> She will have been eating cake for two hours
(-) S + Will + not + Have + Been + V-ing + O/C >>> She won't have been eating the cake for 2 hours
(?) Will + S + Have + Been + V-ing + O/C? >>> Will she have been eating the cake for 2 hours?
