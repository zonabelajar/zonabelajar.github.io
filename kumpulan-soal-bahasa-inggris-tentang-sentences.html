I was disappointing / disappointed with the film. I had expected it to be better.

The movie was frightening / frightened. I really do not want to watch horror movie anymore.

We were all horrifying / horrified when we heard about the disaster.

It is sometimes embarrassing / embarrassed when you have to ask people for money.

Are you interesting / interested in playing football?

In enjoyed the football match. It was quite exciting / excited.

It was a really terrifying / terrified

Afterwards everybody was very shocking / shocked.

It has been raining all day. I hate this weather. (depress-)

This weather is depressing.

This weather makes me depressed.

Astronomy is one of Tom’s main interests. (interest-)

Tom is interested.

He finds astronomy very interesting.

I turned off the television in the middle of the programme. (bor-)

The programme was boring.

I was bored.

Amelia is going to America next month. She has never been there before. (excit-)

She is really excited about going.

It will be an exciting experience for her.

Diana teaches young children. It is a hard job. (exhaust-)

She often finds her job exhausting.

Penjelasan:

Pada intinya perbedaan antara akhiran kata sifat -ed dan -ing adalah:

-ed itu artinya ter-. misal pada kata interested, ini artinya tertarik.

-ing itu artinya me-. misal pada kata interesting, ini artinya menarik.
