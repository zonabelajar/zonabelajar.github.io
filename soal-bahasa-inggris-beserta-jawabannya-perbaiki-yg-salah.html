5. soal reading bahasa inggris beserta jawabannya


1.Stalagmites are formed by …

(A) drops of water which enter through cracks in the ceiling.

(B) underground rivers which flow through the cave.

(C) water dripping from an overhead stalactite.

(D) water which trickles down a slope.

Jawaban : (C) water dripping from an overhead stalactite.

2. Sinkholes are …

(A) the decorative dripstone features found in caves.

(B) natural openings on the surface that lead to caves.

(C) colorful layers of mineral deposits.

(D) None of the above

Jawaban : (B) natural openings on the surface that lead to caves.

3. Which speleothem grows upward from the floor?

(A) Stalagmites

(B) Stalactites

(C) Sinkholes

(D) Curtains

Jawaban : (A) Stalagmites

4. An “inclined ceiling” is one which …

(A) is straight.

(B) is crooked.

(C) is slanted.

(D) is wet.

Jawaban : (C) is slanted.

5. Which of the following are NOT caused by dripping water?

(A) Stalactites

(B) Stalagmites

(C) Slopes

(D) Curtains

Jawaban : (C) Slopes

6. The information in the passage is most relevant to which field of study?

(A) Geography

(B) Archaeology

(C) Physics

(D) Geology

Jawaban : (D) Geology

7. “ Curtains” can also be called …

(A) column.

(B) draperies.

(C) stalagmites.

(D) rims.

Jawaban : (B) draperies.

8. The word speleothem comes from which language?

(A) Latin

(B) French

(C) Greek

(D) English

Jawaban : (C) Greek

9. Stalagmites are formed by …

(A) drops of water which enter the cave through cracks in the ceiling.

(B) underground rivers which flow through the cave.

(C) water which seeps through the cave floor.

(D) water which trickles down a slope.

Jawaban : (A) drops of water which enter the cave through cracks in the ceiling.

10. Which speleothem hangs from the ceiling of a cave?

(A) Stalagmites

(B) Stalactites

(C) Columns

(D) Rimstones

Jawaban : (B) Stalactites

24. Soal dialok tentang telepone bahasa inggris berserta jawaban


Pembahasan

Soal dialog tentang telephone Bahasa Inggris beserta jawaban adalah

Read the dialogue below carefully. It is a conversation using expression of making a phone call.

Edi       : Hello Dad, it's me, Edi. Are you busy right now? I need your help.

Mr. Abi: Hello, Edi. No, I'm free now. I have just finished my afternoon meeting. I will go home soon. What's the matter, Son?

Edi       : Great. Can you pick me up at school? My bicycle is broken. Mom is attending a community meeting and she cannot pick me up.

Mr. Abi: Sure. I'll be there in fifteen minutes. Please wait for me inside the school gate.  

Edi       : OK, I will. What about my bicycle, Dad?

Mr. Abi: I will ask your uncle to pick it up and bring it to the garage near your school. See you later, Edi.

Edi      : See you, Dad. Thank you very much.

Mr. Abi: You are welcome, Son.

A. a boy and his father

B. a boy and his uncle

C. a boy and his mother

D. a boy and his teacher

E. a boy and his friend

B. Edi's mother is not attending a community meeting.

C. Edi's bicycle is not broken.

D. Edi's bicycle cannot be used.

E. Edi's bicycle is being repaired.

3. Why does Edi's mother cannot pick him up at his school?

B. Edi's mother is attending a community meeting.

Should (Giving Advice)

1. I think they should wait until they finish their study.

2. I think you should stay at home this evening.

3. or should I keep sending his mail to you?

4. You should ask Ivanka, our computer technician, for advice.

5. Everyone should travel.

Should be (Giving Criticism)

1. He should be wearing a coat.

2. It should be open now.

3. Our dog should be running to us [...]

4. They should be coming tonight, [...]

5. They should be in bed. They shouldn't be watching television.

ーーーーーーーーーーーー

Explication

Should adalah sebuah modal verb yang dapat digunakan untuk memberikan saran. Should mengacu pada aksi yang sebaiknya dilakukan di masa depan.

You have wronged her. You should apologize.You look tired. You should rest.

Should juga digunakan untuk menyatakan sesuatu yang diharapkan akan terjadi/menjadi kenyataan.

I should graduate next year.The train should arrive in five minutes.

Should be digunakan untuk mengekspresikan sesuatu yang seharusnya terjadi tapi tidak. Should be mengacu pada masa sekarang (present) atau masa depan (future).

You have a fever. You should be in bed, not working.The President should be giving a speech tonight, but he had to cancel it because of the terrorist attack.

Jadi, should be = is/am/are supposed to

Descriptive Text

Descriptive text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk mendeskripsikan sesuatu (manusia, hewan, tumbuhan dan benda) secara khusus atau spesifik.

Generic Structure dari Descriptive text adalah:

Identification: Berisi tentang penjelasan singkat dari apa yang akan di deskripsikan.Description: Mendeskripsikan benda, hean, tumbuhan dan manusia secara spesifik atau khusus.

Language features dari Descriptive text adalah:

Menggunakan simple present tenseMenggunakan kata yang spesifik atau tidak terlalu umumMenggunakan adjective untuk mendeskripsikan sesuatuPembahasan

Georgy is my beautiful Persian cat. Georgy has a large round(3) head, a short nose, big eyes, full cheeks, and small ears with rounded tips. He has short legs and a short tail. He is so fat with a long, thick, shiny golden coat(4) with a fine texture. He is so cute.

That long, beautiful coat doesn’t stay clean and tangle-free(10) on its own. It must be gently but thoroughly combed and brushed every day, and regular bathing—at least once a month—is a good idea.

Identifikasi (Identification. Bagian pertama dari sebuah teks deskriptif ini dipergunakan sebagai paragraf untuk meperkenalkan subjek yang akan dideskripsikan.   Deskripsi (Description). Bagian kedua dari teks deskriptif ini membahas berbagai hal terkait ciri-ciri yang dimiliki subjek yang akan dideskripsikan (attributes of particular thing). Ciri-ciri yang dimaksudkan di sini yakni bisa mencakup tampilan fisik (appearance), bentuk (shape), warna (color), kualitas (quality), perilaku (habit), dan juga sifat (characteristic) yang dimiliki oleh subjek tersebut.

Contoh: an active young girl, a comfortable long bench, a rare insect, dan lain sebagainya.  Penggunaan berbagai jenis kata sifat (adjective) yang bersifat mendeskripsikan subjek (describing), menjelaskan jumlah (numbering), dan juga mengklasifikasikan (classifying). Contoh: it has good voice, it is black, the market is near from here,  dan lain-lainnya.  Menggunakan thinking verbs atau feeling verbs guna mengungkapkan pandangan penulis secara pribadi terkait subjek yang dideskripsikan. Contoh: My little sister is as sweet as candy, She is not as black as she is painted, today is as hot as in the desert, dan lain-lain.  Menggunakan action verbs guna menjelaskan perilaku atau kebiasaan subjek yang dibahas. Contoh:  come, prepare, do, eat, meet, dan lain-lain.
