8. contoh dialog asking & showing attention​


Sure, here's an example of a dialog where one person asks for attention, and the other person shows that they are attentive:

Person A: "Hey, do you have a moment? I'd like to talk about something that's been on my mind."

Person B: "Of course! I'm all ears. What's on your mind? I'm here to listen."

Person A: "Thank you! It's about my recent job interview. I'm feeling quite nervous about it."

Person B: "I understand how important that must be for you. Please go ahead and share your thoughts and feelings. I'm here to support you."

Person A: "I appreciate your support. The interview went well, but I'm unsure about my chances of getting the job."

Person B: "I see. It's natural to feel uncertain after an interview, but remember that you did your best. I believe in you and your abilities."

Person A: "Thank you for saying that. It means a lot to me."

Person B: "You're welcome! Don't hesitate to reach out if you need anything else. I'm here for you."

In this dialogue, Person A expresses their need for attention, and Person B responds with empathy, active listening, and reassurance, showing that they are genuinely paying attention to Person A's concerns.

32. perbedaan showing care, attention, symphaty beserta contohnya ?


1. Showing care adalah sebuah kalimat yang menyatakan simpati terhadap seseorang. Contoh: A: I'm sorry to hear that. B: I appreciate your thoughts and prayers. 2. Attention adalah sebuah kalimat yang menyatakan perhatian terhadap seseorang sedangkan Intention adalah kalimat perencanaan. 3. Shympaty sama halnya dengan showing care yaitu ekspresi kesedihan yang ditunjukan untuk teman yang sedang mendapat masalah contoh: our friend is sad because his/her grandfather is sick.1. Showing Care: Showing Care (Menenjukkan Keperdulian) adalah perasaan dimana seseorang peduli kepada orang yang lainnya.

A: I am not feeling well today.
B: I hope you get well soon!
A: Thank you.
B: You're welcome!

2. Attention: Attention (Perhatian) adalah perasaan dimana kita inging di amati oleh orang lain

A: Hey! Pay attention to me!
B: Oh, sorry!

3. Sympathy: Sympathy (Simpati/Belas Kasih) adalah perasaan dimana kita merasa sedih untuk orang lain.

A: Are you sad?
B: Yes, I am.
A: Why?
B: My mother died today.
A: Oh my God, I'm so sorry!
B: It's okay.
