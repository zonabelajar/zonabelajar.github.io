3. social function and structure of song


Jawaban:

Song is similar kind to a poem technically but with more adapted to the melody instead of pure verse. It has social function too, they are

- to inspire the listeners with the message beyond

- to motivate the listeners either from its tune and melody or the lyric itself.

- to express public opinion about the social phenomena, usually used for the chants in demonstration

- to entertain public

- to be used as a media for language learning.

Meanwhile, the structure of it consist of:

- the verse

- the pre-chorus

- the chorus

- the bridge

- the hook

Pembahasan:

Song can be considered as a new kind of poem which is accompanied by the melody or tune. It is one of the most popular work of art in the modern world beside movies or dance. Song has the personal function as the media to express how one feels about something or the opinion on certain issues. Meanwhile the social function has been mentioned above.

As for the structure of the song, it can be elaborated as follows:

- The verse: it's the introductory part of the song, usually before the pre-chorus. The songwriter expresses the issue or the feeling about it along with the certain description of the situation sometimes.

-The pre-chorus: it's usually the few lines before the chorus

- The chorus: it's the part which is sang repeatedly, one of the main attractions of the song.

- the bridge: it's the part which normally include the peak moment or the twist in the song.

- the hook: the important part of the song, normally the song title is taken from here. It can be put at the beginning or the end of the chorus.

Penjelasan:

Definisi lagu adalah karya musikal yang ditulis sebagai ekspresi pribadi penulisnya yang berisikan melodi, irama, dan lirik.

Struktur umum lagu meliputi:

1. Chorus - Bagian yang diulang, biasanya berisi judul lagu atau tema utama.

2. Verse - Bagian lirik lagu yang berbeda antara satu sama lain.

3. Bridge - Bagian lirik lagu yang menghubungkan verse dan chorus.

4. Pre-chorus - Bagian sebelum chorus yang membangun suasana sebelum sampai ke chorus.

Ciri kebahasaan dalam lagu:

1. Simile - Perumpamaan, contoh: "My love is like a red, red rose."

2. Personification - Penghayatan, contoh: "The sun greeted me this morning."

3. Metaphor - Metafora, contoh: "Your eyes are the ocean, I'm lost at sea."

4. Hyperbole - Hiperbola, contoh: “I have already forgotten a thousand ways to cry.”

5. Alliteration - Aliterasi, contoh: "Round the ragged rock the ragged rascal ran."

Fungsi sosial lagu:

1. Menyampaikan pesan dan makna kepada pendengar.

2. Menghibur dan mengundang reaksi emosi pendengar.

3. Mempengaruhi dan menginspirasi pendengar.

4. Melestarikan budaya, adat dan nilai tradisional.

5. Merefleksikan gaya, cita rasa dan zaman ketika lagu itu diciptakan.

6. Menjadi sarana ekspresi diri bagi pencipta lagu.

7. Membentuk identitas dan solidaritas sosial dalam komunitas.

8. Sebagai media pendokumentasian peristiwa atau pengalaman.

Definition of Song:

A song is a musical composition that is typically sung by the human voice with a specific melody and rhythm. It is often accompanied by instruments such as guitars, drums, or pianos.

Generic Structure of Song:

Verse: The main body of the song that tells the story or conveys the message.

Chorus: The part of the song that is repeated several times, usually with the same melody and lyrics.

Bridge: A section that connects the verse and chorus and provides a contrast to them.

Language Features of Song:

Simile: A figure of speech that compares two things using "like" or "as." Example: "Your love is like a rose."

Personification: Giving human qualities to non-human things. Example: "The wind whispered in my ear."

Metaphor: A figure of speech that directly compares two things without using "like" or "as." Example: "Life is a journey."

Hyperbole: An exaggeration used to emphasize a point. Example: "I've been waiting for a million years."

Alliteration: The repetition of the same sound or letter at the beginning of words in a sentence. Example: "Peter Piper picked a peck of pickled peppers."

Social Function of Song:

Entertainment: Songs are often created for the purpose of entertainment and enjoyment.

Communication: Songs can convey a message or story to the listener.

Cultural expression: Songs can express cultural values, beliefs, and traditions.

Emotional expression: Songs can be used to express emotions such as love, sadness, or happiness.

Social commentary: Songs can provide commentary on social issues and current events.

28. Find out the structure of the song you're my best friend ?


Jawaban:

The song "You're My Best Friend" is a popular song by the band Queen. It was written by the band's bass guitarist John Deacon and released in 1975 as part of their album "A Night at the Opera." The song follows a typical structure commonly found in popular music, which is verse-chorus form.

The structure of "You're My Best Friend" can be outlined as follows:

1. Intro: The song may start with an instrumental introduction that sets the mood for the song.

2. Verse 1: The first verse introduces the lyrics and melody of the song. It usually tells a part of the story or expresses a specific emotion.

3. Chorus: The chorus is the catchy and memorable part of the song that is repeated multiple times throughout. It often contains the main message or theme of the song and has a melodic and lyrical hook.

4. Verse 2: The second verse continues the story or develops the theme further.

5. Chorus: The chorus is repeated again to reinforce the main message of the song.

6. Bridge: The bridge is a section that provides a contrast to the verses and chorus. It may have a different melody, rhythm, or lyrical content. In "You're My Best Friend," the bridge section features a key change and showcases a different musical arrangement.

7. Chorus: The chorus is repeated again, usually with heightened energy or intensity.

8. Outro: The song may end with a final chorus repetition, a fade-out, or a specific musical conclusion.

It's important to note that the structure of songs can vary, and different variations or additional sections may be present depending on the artist's creative choices. The structure provided here is a general guideline based on common songwriting practices.

Generic structure of the song atau struktur lagu merupakan susunan unsur-unsur musik pada sebuah lagu yang membentuk sebuah komposisi lagu yang bermakna. Sebuah lagu mempunyai bentuk/ struktur yg terdiri asal intro, verse, chorus, bridge, serta coda.

Intro

Intro atau introduction merupakan bagian awal berasal sebuah lagu atau bisa pula dikatakan menjadi pengantar. Intro juga berfungsi buat menyampaikan ketika bagi penyanyi dan pendengar buat mempersiapkan diri sebelum lagu dimainkan. umumnya intro berupa musik instrumental yg nadanya diambil dari verse atau reff lagu. Disamping itu, intro pula dipergunakan buat membuat melodi, harmonic, ataupun irama.

Verse

Verse adalah sebuah bagian dalam lagu, menjadi nyanyian pada bagian awal sebelum masuk ke bagian bridge atau chorus. Bagian ini menceritakan kisah berasal lagu tersebut serta umumnya ada dua atau 3 bait pada sebuah lagu.

Chorus atau Refrain

chorus serta refrain itu tidak selaras, kesamaan antara keduanya hanyalah menjadi bagian yang berisi pesan utama cerita yang disajikan melalui lirik lagu. Bagian ini adalah yang paling sering diulang karena memberikan ringkasan cerita lagu.

Bridge

umumnya bridge digunakan buat menjembatani antara bagian-bagian lagu. misalnya, menjembatani antara chorus menggunakan verse atau sebaliknya. Nada yg dimainkan di bridge ini umumnya dibuat sangat tidak sinkron dengan nada pada verse serta chorus, sebagai akibatnya bagian ini bisa mengubah tempo, volume, atau instrumentasi namun tidak terdengar absurd.

Coda atau Outro

Coda atau outro merupakan bagian akhir asal lagu tadi. Dimana, isinya merogoh beberapa lirik serta nada yang telah ada sebelumnya atau mengulang bagian intro, chorus atau hanya menunda diri menggunakan memakai instrument musik.
