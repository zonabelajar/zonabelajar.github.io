3. cara mengerjakan soal jumble word?


Cara mengerjakan: 1. Baca dan pahami soal dengan baik. 2. Identifikasi makna, bentuk dan fungsi dari masing-masing kata yang disediakan. 3. Tentukan kata yang berfungsi sebagai subject, predikat, dan object. 4. Identifikasi pilihan yang didahului oleh subject. 5. Cobalah menyusun dengan beberapa pilihan. 6. Tentukan pilihan yang paling tepat.

4. Arrange the jumble word into positive, negative and introgative sentence in simple present tense.plis tolong jawab​.di kumpul besok pagi


Hallo kak, jadi kalau simple present tense rumusnya seperti berikut:

Positif: Subjek > Kata kerja (+s/es jika tunggal) > Objek > Preposisi tempat/waktu/gerak.

Negatif: Subjek > do/does not ("do" jika subjek jamak "does" jika subjek tunggal) > Kata kerja (tidak perlu +s/es karena sudah diwaliki do/does) > Objek > Preposisi tempat/waktu/gerak.

Introgatif: Do/does > Subjek > Kata kerja > Objek > Preposisi tempat/waktu/gerak > Tanda tanya (?)

Catatan:

Pronoun "I" walaupun tunggal jika ditambah verb "do" tidak menjadi "does" karena "I" adalah orang pertama.

To be = Auxiliary verb (kata kerja pembantu.)

Benar = Do/does have. Salah = Do/does has.

Jawaban:

1.

(+) Dimas waches TV in the living room.

(-) Dimas does not watch Tv in the living room.

(?) Does Dimas watch TV in the living room?

2.

(+) I study in my room every night.

(-) I do not study in my room every night.

(?) Do i study in my room every night?

3.

(+) My mom washes the dish every day.

(-) My mom does not wash the dish every day.

(?) Does my mom wash the dish every day?

4.

(+) I am at home every weekend.

(-) I am not at home every weekend.

(?) Am I at home every weekend?

5.

(+) My cat has a soft fur.

(-) My cat does not have a soft fur.

(?) Does my cat have a soft fur?

Maaf jika ada yang salah, semonga membantu!!!

26. Apakah yang dimaksud dengan jumble word, jumble sentence, close text? Jelaskan dalam bahasa inggris


jumble word is the sequence of words from a randomized sentence
jumble sentence is writing skills that train students to compose a random sentence into a unified paragraph or text
cloze test is an exercise, test, or assessment consisting of a portion of language with certain items, words, or signs removed (cloze text), where the participant is asked to replace the missing language item.
