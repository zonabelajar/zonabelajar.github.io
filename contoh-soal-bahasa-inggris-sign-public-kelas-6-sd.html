26. Pengertian sign dalam bahasa inggris~


Answer:


In English, Sign has two functions, as a verb and as a noun.

As a verb, sign means to write your name or signature on a letter or printed document.

For example, I sign the peace agreement.

As a verb, sign also means to give an order or a request to someone by using body language.

For example, The boss signs to the waiter to bring him a cup of coffee.

 

As a noun, sign means a gesture of the body to give an information.

For example, crying is a sign of sadness

As a noun, sign also means a mark that has a specific meaning.

For example, the stop sign means the drivers has to stop and not to continue their journey to the road that has this sign.

 

Detil tambahan:

Kelas: SMA

Pelajaran: Bahasa inggris

Kategori: Part of speech

Kata kunci: Noun, verb, sign

Answer:

The public signs are normally the traffic signs which tell you about what must or mustn't be done on the road. Here are what they are said from the attached signs (in order) and its meaning.

1. Keep Left (tetap di lajur kiri)

2. No entry (dilarang masuk)

3. Do not turn right (dilarang belok kanan)

4. Do not park here. (dilarang parkir disini)

5. Keep off the grass (hindari rumput taman)

6. Do not overtake (dilarang menyalip)

7. Slippery when wet (awas jalan licin)

8. Beware the dog! (Awas ada binatang buas domestik)

9. Two way traffic (jalan dua arah)

10. No phone is allowed (telpon seluler tidak boleh digunakan)

11. Do not turn around. (dilarang memutar balik)

34. Sebutkan apa saja yang termasuk dalam public places dan fungsinya dalam bahasa inggris !


VOCABULARY BUILDING

Dalam bahasa Indonesia, Public Places merupakan tempat umum. Tempat dimana berkerumunnya orang-orang dalam melakukan tujuan tertentu.

Berikut nama-nama Public Places dalam bahasa Inggris beserta fungsinya :

1. Cinema

▶The place where we can watch variety of movies.

{ Tempat dimana kita bisa menonton berbagai jenis film }

2. Supermarket

▶The place where we can buy the daily needs, vegetables and fruits.

{ Tempat dimana kita bisa membeli kebutuhan harian, sayur-mayur dan buah-buahan }

3. Restaurant

▶The place where we can buy and eat delicious meals with the great ambience.

{ Tempat dimana kita bisa membeli dan makan makanan yang lezat dengan suasana yang tidak biasa }

4. Book Store

▶The place where we can buy variety of books.

{ Tempat dimana kita bisa membeli berbagai jenis buku }

5. Library

▶The place where we can lend books.

{ Tempat dimana kita bisa meminjam buku }

6. Bank

▶The place where we can save/deposit our money.

{ Tempat dimana kita bisa menyimpan/mendeposit uang kita }

7. Airport

▶The place where the planes land or take off.

We can travel across countries by a plane.

{ Tempat dimana pesawat mendarat atau terbang landas. Kita bisa keliling ke luar negeri dengan menggunakan pesawat }

8. Mosque

▶The place where moslems pray to Allah

{ Tempat dimana umat muslim shalat }

9. Hospital

▶The place where the patients are cured/hospitalized.

{ Tempat dimana para pasien disembuhkan/dirawat }

10. Post Office

▶The place where we can send/post the mails and the packages.

{ Tempat dimana kita dapat mengirimkan surat dan paket }

English:

Subject: Importance of Environmental Awareness

Ladies and Gentlemen,

Good morning to all of you. I stand here today to talk about a topic that affects us all, and that is the importance of environmental awareness.

As we all know, the world we live in today is facing numerous environmental challenges, such as climate change, deforestation, and pollution. These issues not only affect our health and well-being, but also the future of our planet.

However, it is not too late to act. Each and every one of us has the power to make a positive impact on the environment. By being environmentally aware and making small changes in our daily lives, such as reducing, reusing, and recycling, we can help to protect our planet for future generations.

Let us all make a pledge to become more environmentally aware and to take action to protect the environment. We have the power to make a difference, so let us not waste this opportunity.

Thank you for your time and attention.

Indonesia:

Judul: Pentingnya Kesadaran Lingkungan

Yang terhormat Bapak, Ibu dan teman-teman sekalian,

Selamat pagi untuk kita semua. Saya berdiri di sini hari ini untuk berbicara tentang topik yang mempengaruhi kita semua, yaitu pentingnya kesadaran lingkungan.

Seperti yang kita semua tahu, dunia tempat kita hidup saat ini sedang menghadapi berbagai tantangan lingkungan, seperti perubahan iklim, deforestasi, dan polusi. Masalah ini tidak hanya mempengaruhi kesehatan dan kesejahteraan kita, tetapi juga masa depan planet kita.

Namun, belum terlambat untuk bertindak. Setiap orang memiliki kekuatan untuk membuat dampak positif pada lingkungan. Dengan menjadi sadar lingkungan dan membuat perubahan kecil dalam kehidupan sehari-hari kita, seperti mengurangi, menggunakan ulang, dan mendaur ulang, kita dapat membantu melindungi planet kita untuk generasi mendatang.

Mari kita semua membuat janji untuk menjadi lebih sadar lingkungan dan bertindak untuk melindungi lingkungan. Kita memiliki kekuatan untuk membuat perbedaan, jadi jangan sia-siakan kesempatan ini.
