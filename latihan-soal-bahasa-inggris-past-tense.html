I was sicked yesterday

Joni didn't go to school last Monday

Menggunakan kata go bukannya 'went' karena itu kalimat lampau tetapi tidak terjadi karena 'Joni tidak pergi ke sekolah senin lalu' yang berarti itu sebuah kata lampau tetapi tidak terjadi.

Did they come last night?

Jadi saya gunakan kata come bukannya 'came'.

20. Apa Saja Rumus Bahasa Inggris Simple Past Tense​


Jawaban:

Rumus dari simple past tense: Subject + Verb 2 + Object.

positive (+) : subject (s) + verb 2 + object (o)

negative (-) : subject (s) + did not + infinitive tanpa to

interrogative (?)
=> positive : did + subject (s) + infinitive tanpa to

24. Apa kegunaan Past future tense dalam bahasa inggris


Simple Past Future Tense dibentuk dengan dua tipe, pertama yaitu ‘would’ dan bare infinitive (bentuk simpel dari verb). Tipe kedua dibentuk dari ‘was/were’, present participle ‘going’ dan infinitive. Infinitive yaitu suatu kata yang terdiri dari to + bare infinitive. Tipe kedua lebih sering digunakan untuk menyatakan aksi yang telah direncanakan.

29. perbedaan antara present tense past tense and future tense dalam bahasa inggris​


Jawaban:

if the present tense is used for events that are usually done or can be called 'habits', if past tense is used for activities that have occurred in the past,

while future tense is used for something that has not yet happened but has been planned, so you could say ideals.

Jawaban:

simple present tense

Used to show facts, habits, and general conditions that occur at this time.

formula: S + V / -s, es + complement

The time signal word for this tense is usually, always, every, sometimes, normally, seldom, etc.

Simple Past Tense

Used to indicate an event in the past.

formula: S + V2 + complement

The said time signals for this tense are last year, last month, yesterday, in 1996, last Sunday, etc.

Simple Future Tense

Used to indicate an action or a situation in the future.

formula: S + will / be going to + V1 + complement

The said time signal for this tense is next, tomorrow, five days later, etc.

Then, one day, the Wizard saw a Cat chasing the Mouse around the village. Scared that his pet Mouse would be killed by the Cat, he turned the Mouse into a Cat so that it could defend itself.

The next day, the Wizard saw his Cat frightened by a Tiger and  immediately changed him into a
Tiger. The villagers said, “That’s not a Tiger! It’s just a Mouse that the Wizard changed into a Tiger. He won’t eat us or even scare us.”
When the Tiger heard this, he was furious with the Wizard. He thought, ‘As long as the Wizard is alive, the truth about my real nature will always be spoken!’ But as soon as the Wizard saw the Tiger coming, he understood his plan and shouted, “Get back into the form of a Mouse.” 

The Tiger shrank and became a little Mouse, once again.
