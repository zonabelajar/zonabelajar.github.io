9. berikan jawaban latihan soal pat /ukk kelas 8 Bahasa Inggris beri kunci jawaban nya​


Soal 1:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

My sister ________ tennis every Saturday morning.

a) plays

b) play

c) played

d) playing

Jawaban: a) plays

Soal 2:

Complete the sentence with the correct preposition:

I'm going to the store ________ some groceries.

a) at

b) with

c) for

d) to

Jawaban: c) for

Soal 3:

Choose the correct question tag for the following sentence:

He doesn't like coffee, ________?

a) does he?

b) isn't he?

c) doesn't he?

d) don't he?

Jawaban: a) does he?

Soal 4:

Identify the correct comparative form of the adjective "good":

a) gooder

b) goodest

c) better

d) goodier

Jawaban: c) better

Soal 5:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

She ________ a book when I arrived.

a) reads

b) is reading

c) read

d) has read

Jawaban: b) is reading

Soal 6:

Choose the correct word order for the sentence:

carrots / do / like / you / ?

a) You do like carrots?

b) Do you like carrots?

c) Carrots you do like?

d) Do like you carrots?

Jawaban: b) Do you like carrots?

Soal 7:

Identify the correct spelling of the word:

a) beutiful

b) beutifull

c) beautiful

d) beautifull

Jawaban: c) beautiful

Soal 8:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

The students ________ to the teacher's instructions.

a) listens

b) listen

c) listened

d) listening

Jawaban: b) listen

Soal 9:

Choose the correct possessive form of the noun:

The dog is ________ bone.

a) its

b) it's

c) their

d) they're

Jawaban: a) its

Soal 10:

Identify the correct punctuation for the sentence:

My favorite color is blue ________.

a) .

b) ,

c) !

d) ?

Jawaban: a) .

26. matematika kelas 4 sd pat ​


~Bangun Datar

______________

Jawaban:

1.728 m² (Opsi B)

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Luas Taman Tomat

= (4/4 - 1/4) × L Persegi

= ¾ × s²

= ¾ × 48²

= ¾ × 2.304

= (3 × 2.304) ÷ 4

= 6.912 ÷ 4

= 1.728 m²✔

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

– Detail Jawaban

Mapel : MatematikaKelas : VMateri : Bangun DatarKode Soal : 2Kode Kategorisasi : 5.2.3

Pertama tama kita cari dulu luasnyah yaa..

luas persigi adalah=S²

berarti 48²=2.304

diketahui lagi jika ¼ ditanamj jagung dan sisnyah tomat

beratti ¼×2.304=576

tinggal mencari luaa tonat berati 2.304-576=1.728m²

38. bantuin kakak.... soalnya ini PAT yg nomor 2 aja​


Penyelesaian :

Rumus Keliling Persegi Panjang :

Keliling = 2p + 2l

Jawab :

Keliling = 86

2p + 2l = 86

2(25) + 2(l) = 86

50 + 2l = 86

2l = 86 - 50

2l = 36

2l/2 = 36/2

l = 18 cm

Lebar = 18 cm

______________

Semoga Membantu

Jawaban:

18 cm

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

K = 86 - ( 2 × 25 ) - ( 2 × L )

K = 86 - 50 - ( 2 × L )

K = 36 - ( 2 × L )

L = 36 ÷ 2

L = 18 cm

Jadi, lagu Memandang Alam diciptakan oleh Soerjono (Pak Kasur).
