15. buat cepen bahasa inggris tentang lomba cerdas cermat


I am a 5th grade student in Beringin Jaya VII Elementary School, I love reading books in the library with my best friend named Zahra and Kirana. At rest time, we prefer to spend some time in the library. Oh yes, I forgot to introduce about myself. My name is Alika. I am the second child of three children. I have an older sister and a younger sister.
While studying in class, suddenly Teacher enters the class and calls me, Zahra and Kirana. Teacher called us because we were the top three in the class. Teacher explained that we will be included in the Great Intelligence at Jabodetabek level. I was very excited at the time because I was able to represent the school to participate in the meticulous race.
The competition is held in 2 weeks, and to fill my time, Zahra and Kirana are more often in the library to reading. I am more active learning than ever because I want to give the best for my school. Teacher always accompanies us to study and give new problems that we have never had before in class. Therefore, we were always late from school because of the extra hours to practice.
The race finally arrived. Intelligent contest is held in Taman Mini Indonesia Indah. I was amazed to see the participants who will follow the race, because very many in number. I, Zahra and Kirana had felt nervous because of the many participants. However, we immediately eliminated that feeling and focused back on our goal of winning the race.
The preliminary round consists of 4 teams at each session. This first round is a session to answer the matter in turns, the second round is a session to answer the question of seizure, and the final round is to compose the highest cans. The higher the can, the higher the value. My team won this preliminary round because we answered many questions and compiled the tallest cans.
Passing the preliminary round I was getting tense because during the semi-final of his opponent increasingly tough and smart. The semifinal round of the system is the same as the preliminary round. We won this round and went into the final round. In the final round, the race system is the same as the preliminary rounds and the semi-finals, plus the wording. I'm very nervous because this part of the word is part of what I have to accomplish by myself. The words I had to write were quite difficult but I was able to solve them. Cooperation is needed in this race to victory.
Arrived during the announcement of the winner, I was very happy because the team from SD Beringin Jaya VII won the 1st champion. We became champion 1 because the word stacking competition and stacking cans got the highest score from the other team. Cup champion 1 we brought back to SD Beringin Jaya VII and we are very happy because it can bring the good name of SD Beringin Jaya VII in Jabodetabek area.
